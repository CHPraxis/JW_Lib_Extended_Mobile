A Day to Remember

The evening before he died, Jesus passed a loaf of unleavened bread and a cup of wine to his apostles and instructed them to eat and to drink. He also told them: “Keep doing this in remembrance of me.”​—Luke 22:19.

This year the anniversary of this event falls on Thursday, April 1, after sundown. Jehovah’s Witnesses around the world will be gathered on this special night to observe this Memorial in the manner Jesus commanded. You are most warmly invited to join us. Please check with Jehovah’s Witnesses locally for the exact time and place of this special meeting.
