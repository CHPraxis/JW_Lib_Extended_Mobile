Will You Be There?
The “Divine Victory” International Assembly of Jehovah’s Witnesses begins this month. You are invited to attend. All sessions are free.
The principal talk will be “Divine Victory​—Its Meaning for Distressed Humanity.” The program will also feature helpful Bible discussions on practical matters of life, as well as thrilling, costumed dramas in Bible settings.
