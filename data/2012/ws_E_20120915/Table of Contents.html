PURPOSE OF STUDY ARTICLES

STUDY ARTICLES 1, 2 PAGES 3-14

The Bible shows that important events will soon happen. These articles discuss ten events that we need to know about. Five of them have to do with the destruction of Satan’s world, and the other five have to do with the new world.

STUDY ARTICLE 3 PAGES 15-20

We all long for the end of this wicked system and for Paradise on earth. Whether you have been waiting for some months or for many years, why is patience important? This article will help us to understand how we can become more patient.

STUDY ARTICLE 4 PAGES 21-26

All God’s people look forward to the end of this wicked system. This article will show why we do not need to know the exact day or hour when the end will come.

STUDY ARTICLE 5 PAGES 27-32

Conventions have been an important part of pure worship for thousands of years. This article gives us some examples of special conventions in Bible times and in recent times and reminds us of the benefits we can receive from attending conventions.
