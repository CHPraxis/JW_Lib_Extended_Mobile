PROFILE
Son of Jesse, and the second King of Israel. A skilled poet and musician, David wrote more than 73 of the psalms. He humbly sought Jehovah’s direction in his life. (1 Samuel 23:2; 30:8; 2 Samuel 2:1) Jehovah called David “a man agreeable to my heart.”—Acts 13:22.
