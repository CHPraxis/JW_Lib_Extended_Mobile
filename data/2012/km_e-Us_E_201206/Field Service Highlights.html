Field Service Highlights

When comparing January 2012 to the same month last year, the number of publishers increased by 4.8 percent in the Bahamas and by 14.6 percent in the Turks and Caicos Islands. During the first five months of the service year, the number of Bible studies increased over the previous year by 10.3 percent in Bermuda and by 2.8 percent in the United States.
