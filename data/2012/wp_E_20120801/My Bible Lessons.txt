
My Bible Lessons
AGES 3 AND UNDER

Caleb learns that his friend is sick.
So, he says: “I know what I’ll do.
I’ll write him a letter to make him feel better, and then I’ll deliver it too!”
Show kindness, and you’ll both be happy! 1 Peter 3:8
ACTIVITIES

House
Desk
Caleb
Sun

Mention a friend who is not feeling well, and talk to your child about how both of you can encourage that person.
