PURPOSE OF STUDY ARTICLES
STUDY ARTICLES 1, 2  PAGES 6-19
What prophecies in the Bible can help you to understand what will soon happen in the world? These two articles talk about the vision of the giant image in Daniel chapter 2 and the visions of the wild beast and its image in Revelation chapters 13 and 17. These visions tell us much about the future.
STUDY ARTICLE 3  PAGES 21-26
A Christian who gets baptized promises to put God first in his life. The decisions made by Jehovah’s faithful servants in Bible times can make us want to examine how we are using our time, energy, and the things we have.
STUDY ARTICLE 4  PAGES 27-32
This article shows how the Bible writers were “borne along by holy spirit” and why we can be sure that the Bible is from God. (2 Peter 1:21) The article will also help us to continue to be thankful for the Bible.
