PURPOSE OF STUDY ARTICLES

STUDY ARTICLE 1 PAGES 3-8

Why did Jesus urge his followers to keep watchful? In this article, we will learn about three ways that Jesus was watchful during his life on earth and how we can imitate his example.

STUDY ARTICLE 2 PAGES 9-14

How can we benefit from the examples of Jehovah’s servants of the past who showed courage? We will learn the answer to this question in this article that will help us act courageously.

STUDY ARTICLE 3 PAGES 15-20

Each of us shows a certain spirit, or attitude. From this article, we will learn how we can help to preserve a good spirit in the congregation.

STUDY ARTICLE 4 PAGES 21-26

Christians in religiously divided homes may have challenges every day. This article discusses how they can promote peace in the home and do their best to help their family to accept the truth.
