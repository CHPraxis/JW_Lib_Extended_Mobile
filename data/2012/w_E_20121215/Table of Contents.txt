PURPOSE OF STUDY ARTICLES
STUDY ARTICLES 1, 2  PAGES 4-13
What does it mean to be truly successful in life? These articles show that the correct answer differs from the way the world views the matter. We will also see that to be truly successful, we must remain faithful to God and accept the responsibilities given us.
STUDY ARTICLES 3, 4  PAGES 19-28
How are anointed Christians—and in a sense their companions of the “other sheep”—“temporary residents”? (John 10:16; 1 Pet. 2:11) These articles will give us the answer and will strengthen us in our determination to remain such, unitedly preaching as part of an international brotherhood.
