Field Service Highlights

The following all-time peaks in regular pioneers were reported for the month of September 2011: 142,354 in the United States, 170 in the Bahamas, and 39 in the Turks and Caicos Islands. Additionally, there was an increase of 11 percent in Bible studies conducted in Bermuda during the same month. What a fine effort is being made to show others that Jehovah’s Word is truth!—John 17:17.
