PURPOSE OF STUDY ARTICLES
STUDY ARTICLES 1, 2  PAGES 3-14
These articles will give us good reasons to apply Jehovah’s counsel on marriage. They will help us to increase our appreciation and respect for God’s gift of marriage. They will also explain why it is important to work hard to save a difficult marriage and how applying Scriptural counsel can result in a happy marriage.
STUDY ARTICLE 3  PAGES 15-20
In this article we will learn why Jehovah can be described as a God who always fulfills his purpose at his chosen time. This will strengthen our faith in Jehovah and in his Word, the Bible. It will also help us to make sure that we use our time wisely while we wait for salvation by Jehovah.
STUDY ARTICLE 4  PAGES 21-26
Although we are imperfect, we love God and we want to reflect his glory. This article explains how we can reflect Jehovah’s glory. It shows what we must do in order to imitate God and please him. (Ephesians 5:1) It also shows how we can keep on glorifying him.
