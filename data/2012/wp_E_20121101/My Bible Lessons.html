My Bible Lessons

AGES 3 AND UNDER

A rainy day.

“I’m stuck inside.

Why won’t it stop?”

Tomoko cried.

But then, surprise!

The sun appeared.

The rain was gone.

Tomoko cheered!

She ran outside,

and to her delight,

Tomoko saw

an amazing sight.

Tomoko said,

“I did not know

that rain from God

makes the flowers grow!”

ACTIVITIES

  • Tomoko

  • Flowers

Find the hidden objects.
