Field Service Highlights
When comparing October 2011 with the same month last year, there was an increase of 4.3 percent in regular pioneers in the Bahamas and an increase of 6.9 percent in Bible studies in Bermuda. The Turks and Caicos Islands reached new peaks of 302 total publishers and 45 regular pioneers. Additionally, the United States reached a new peak of 143,738 regular pioneers. This is an increase of 1,384 over the previous peak reported last month. This fine report reflects our love for Jehovah and fellowman.—Matt. 22:37-39.
