Theocratic News

Auxiliary pioneer work is meeting a fine response world wide:

◆ Japan reported 5,080 auxiliary pioneers in September, 61 percent above their temporary-pioneer peak.

◆ Australia had 1,260 auxiliary pioneers in September; Dominican Republic, 568, and Korea, 953.

◆ Israel was happy to have five sharing in the auxiliary pioneer work in September, a month in which they usually did not have any temporary pioneers.

◆ Barbados reported 36 auxiliary pioneers in September as compared with 3 for September of a year ago.
