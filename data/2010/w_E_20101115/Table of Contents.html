Purpose of Study Articles

STUDY ARTICLES 1-3 PAGES 3-16

These articles are especially designed for youths. The first article shows how young ones can be guided by the advice found in God’s Word, the Bible. The second discusses how they can resist peer pressure. And the final article considers what realistic goals youths can set for themselves.

STUDY ARTICLES 4, 5 PAGES 24-32

Learn how you can uphold Jehovah God’s sovereignty. Consider what it means to be an integrity keeper. Review features of the life of upright Job. These articles show how you, like Job and others in the past, can walk in your integrity and stick to Jehovah as your Sovereign Lord.
