Purpose of Study Articles
STUDY ARTICLES 1, 2  PAGES 10-18
These two articles will help us to understand the significance of being baptized “in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the holy spirit.” (Matt. 28:19) You will find in them helpful ideas on how you can live up to your dedication.
STUDY ARTICLES 3, 4  PAGES 19-28
In Jesus’ parable of the wheat and the weeds, he illustrated the future developments with regard to “the sons of the kingdom.” What do the wheat and the weeds represent? How does this parable see its fulfillment in our day? Does this parable concern only the anointed?
