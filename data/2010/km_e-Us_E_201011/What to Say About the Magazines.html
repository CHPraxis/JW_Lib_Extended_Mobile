What to Say About the Magazines

The Watchtower November 1

“Do you think that angels are interested in what we do? [Allow for response.] The Bible makes this interesting statement. [Read Luke 15:10.] This article discusses what Jesus said about how spirit creatures affect us.” Feature the article that begins on page 16.

Awake! November

Read 2 Timothy 3:16. Then say: “Do you agree and feel that we can trust everything the Bible says? [Allow for response.] This article discusses whether archaeology and secular history corroborate what the Bible says about ancient Egypt.” Feature the article that begins on page 15.

The Watchtower December 1

“Many people believe that there are intelligent beings in the spirit realm who influence our lives. What do you think? [Allow for response. Then read Revelation 12:7-9.] This magazine shows what the Bible says about those who live in the spirit realm, how they affect us, and whether we can contact them.”

Awake! December

“Have you ever wondered why Christmas is celebrated on December 25 when the Bible doesn’t say when Jesus was born? [Allow for response.] According to this verse, Jesus’ birth could not have happened during the winter. [Read Luke 2:8.] This magazine reveals the origin of some popular Christmas customs.”
