What to Say About the Magazines

The Watchtower February 1

“Most of us have our own religion. Do you think it matters to God how we choose to worship him? [Allow for response. Then read Matthew 15:9.] This article considers four aspects of true worship as identified by Jesus.” Feature the article that begins on page 16.

Awake! February

“Some believe in a Creator, whereas others feel that such belief is unscientific and unreasonable. What is your opinion? [Allow for response.] According to the Bible, true faith is based on evidence. [Read Hebrews 11:1.] This article discusses some of the evidence that points to a Creator.” Feature the article that begins on page 22.

The Watchtower March 1

“Do you consider the Bible to be the Word of God, or do you think it’s just a good book? [Allow for response.] Notice what the Bible itself says. [Read 2 Timothy 3:16, 17.] This magazine discusses how to make an informed decision on this issue and why it is important to do so.”

Awake! March

“Like most people, you probably admire the beauty in nature. Would you agree that nature also displays wise design? [Allow for response. Then read Psalm 104:24.] This magazine cites interesting examples of the design found in nature and discusses what these teach us.”
