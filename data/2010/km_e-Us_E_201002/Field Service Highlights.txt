Field Service Highlights
We are pleased to report a new peak in regular pioneers in the United States. The total of 127,938 reporting in October 2009 is an increase of 940 over the previous peak in September 2009. This is the second consecutive month that a new all-time peak in regular pioneers has been reached.
We are happy to report a new peak in the Turks and Caicos Islands for October 2009. The total of 617 Bible studies being conducted is an increase of 4.4 percent over the previous peak in September 2009. Also of interest, when we compare the first two months of this service year with the same period last year, we note an increase of 90.6 percent in return visits.
