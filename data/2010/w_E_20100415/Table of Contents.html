Purpose of Study Articles

STUDY ARTICLE 1 PAGES 3-7

Jehovah invites young people to listen, learn, and follow his direction. This article discusses how Bible reading, prayer, and good conduct can help youths to worship Jehovah wholeheartedly.

STUDY ARTICLE 2 PAGES 7-11

We know that Jehovah’s purpose keeps moving forward toward its complete fulfillment. This article examines the holy spirit’s role—in the past, at present, and in the future—in the outworking of that purpose.

STUDY ARTICLE 3 PAGES 20-24

As Satan’s world nears its end, we are being bombarded all the more with images of things that can harm our relationship with God. In this article, we will discuss what some of these things are, why Satan uses them, and how we can protect ourselves.

STUDY ARTICLE 4 PAGES 24-28

What will help us to maintain our zeal for God’s service? Against what human tendency do we need to guard ourselves if we want to keep on following Christ? This article will consider the answers to these important questions.
