What to Say About the Magazines

The Watchtower December 1

“Would you agree that single parents often have it harder than married parents? [Allow for response.] The Bible encourages us to act with consideration toward those who have challenges. [Read Psalm 41:1.] The article that begins on page 22 explains how we can show consideration for single parents.”

Awake! December

“Many have asked, ‘If there’s a God, why doesn’t he get rid of the Devil?’ Have you ever wondered that? [Allow for response.] This article on pages 10 and 11 answers that question from the Bible. It also tells us what the world will be like when the Devil is gone.” Read Revelation 21:3, 4.

The Watchtower January 1

“Many people believe that the garden of Eden is a myth. Would it surprise you to learn that Jesus spoke about Adam and Eve as real people? [Allow for response. Then read Matthew 19:4-6.] This magazine answers some common questions about the garden of Eden.”

Awake! January

Show the cover and ask, “How would you answer this question? [Allow for response.] The Scriptures encourage people to be peaceable. [Read James 3:17.] So then, why hasn’t religion united mankind? Will religion ever be a force for peace? This magazine explores the answers to those questions.”
