Purpose of Study Articles
Study Articles 1, 2  PAGES 3-11
What is integrity? Why is it so important? How can we acquire and maintain our integrity? Is it possible to regain integrity once it is lost? You will find answers to such questions in these two articles.
Study Article 3  PAGES 12-16
Jesus has a unique role in the outworking of God’s purpose. This article examines six titles or designations that apply to Jesus. These highlight his uniqueness. We will also see how we can imitate Jesus in the way he has fulfilled his unique role.
Study Article 4  PAGES 16-20
An examination of what the apostle Paul told Ephesian elders, as recorded in Acts chapter 20, can help us to see how he was able to bear thorough witness. And we will find practical lessons in how and why we can bear thorough witness to the good news.
