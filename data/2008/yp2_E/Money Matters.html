As your parents have probably drummed into your head, money doesn’t grow on trees. They’re right. That’s why it’s important for you to handle money responsibly. While money serves a vital purpose, it can create stress, damage friendships, and destroy your relationship with God. Certainly, your attitude toward money can have a profound effect on you. Chapters 18-20 will show you how to maintain a balanced view.
