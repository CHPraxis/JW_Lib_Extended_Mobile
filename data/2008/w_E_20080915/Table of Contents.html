Purpose of Study Articles

Study Articles 1, 2 PAGES 3-11

This two-part series focuses on Psalm 70. There we read that Jehovah is “the Provider of escape.” The articles examine how Jehovah provided escape for his servants in Bible times and how he does so for us.

Study Article 3 PAGES 16-20

It can be a challenge for a married couple to remain united today, even when both are servants of Jehovah. This article contains practical suggestions on how Christians can keep Jehovah in their marriage, as well as what they can do when difficulties arise.

Study Article 4 PAGES 20-24

We face a choice: Will we seek to receive God’s holy spirit and come under its influence, or will we allow the world’s spirit to dominate? This article analyzes both possibilities, and it helps us to make the choice that leads to happiness.
