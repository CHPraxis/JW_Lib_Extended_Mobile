What to Say About the Magazines
The Watchtower Nov. 1
“Do you think that if husbands and wives applied this Bible counsel, marital bonds would be strengthened? [Read Job 31:1. Then allow for response.] This article discusses Bible principles that can help couples maintain commitment to their marriage.” Highlight the article that begins on page 18.
Awake! Nov.
“Most people agree that the ocean’s resources are being depleted at an alarming rate. Do you think human governments will successfully address the problem? [Allow for response. Then read Matthew 6:10.] This article examines some of the reasons for concern and highlights God’s solution.” Feature the article that begins on page 20.
The Watchtower Dec. 1
“This time of year, many people are thinking about Jesus. What influence would you say that Jesus has had on you? [Allow for response.] The Bible encourages us to follow Jesus’ example. [Read 1 Peter 2:21.] Doing that can make us a better, happier person. This magazine explains why.”
Awake! Dec.
“People have been pondering this question for centuries. [Point to the question on the cover.] Where do you think we can find a meaningful answer? [Allow for response.] Note why we can look to God for an answer. [Read Psalm 100:3.] This magazine shows what the Bible says on the subject.”
