Unseen Spirits​—Do They Help Us? Or Do They Harm Us?
TODAY there is widespread interest in unseen spirits. Some persons believe in them; others do not. What evidence is there as to whether superhuman spirits really exist? How can we know for certain?
Numerous reports tell of communications from the spirit realm. Can the living really talk with the dead? Can dead ancestors help us? Are there other sources from which spirit messages come?
This booklet has been published to aid you to find, satisfying answers to these questions.
