What Has Happened to Love?

Suicide ranks as the 2nd or 3rd killer of the young in almost every industrialized country of the world. WHY? One expert: “So many kids who commit suicide feel unloved, unwanted at home.”

Marriage mates often show little real love for each other. One divorce for every two marriages in some countries. A survey shows, ‘Most murderers are husbands, wives, or relatives.’

Loneliness—“The most devastating malady of our time.” Wrote one person: “I’m cut off, sad, just putting in time. I eat alone, walk alone, sleep alone and talk to myself. There isn’t anyone around to hear me.”

DON’T WE ALL NEED LOVE?

◇ “Love is the single most important element in happiness.”—Survey of 100,000 persons.

◇ ‘The sudden loss of love is a significant contributor to heart disease and premature death.’—Medical expert.

What if husbands ‘loved their wives as themselves’?—Ephesians 5:28.

What if parents ‘loved their children and did not irritate them’?—Titus 2:4; Colossians 3:21.

What if everyone ‘loved his neighbor as himself’?—Matthew 22:39.

How much happier we all would be!

◇ “Know this, that in the last days critical times hard to deal with will be here. For men will be lovers of themselves . . . having no natural affection.”—2 Timothy 3:1-5.

Because of today’s lack of love God will soon take action to remove wickedness. But he will preserve everlastingly on the cleansed earth those loving him.—Psalm 145:20.
