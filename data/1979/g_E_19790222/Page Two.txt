World unity would bring many benefits to mankind. A number of steps have been taken to bring it about; more are proposed. The charter of the United Nations contains commendable goals. But there are many stumbling blocks to world unity. Is it an unreachable goal? No. Mankind will without fail be united in a loving brotherhood. But how? And when?
