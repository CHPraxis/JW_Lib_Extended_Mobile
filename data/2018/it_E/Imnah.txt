IMNAH
(Imʹnah) [May [God] Appoint (or, Number); [God] Has Appointed (or, Numbered)].
1. First-named son of Asher and forefather of the Imnites.​—Ge 46:17; Nu 26:44; 1Ch 7:30.
2. The Levite whose son Kore was the gatekeeper to the east in temple service, in charge of the voluntary offerings of Jehovah, in Hezekiah’s time.​—2Ch 31:14.
