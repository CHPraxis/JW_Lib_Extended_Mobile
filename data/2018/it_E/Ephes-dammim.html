EPHES-DAMMIM

(Eʹphes-damʹmim).

The camping site of the Philistines between Socoh and Azekah, from which point Goliath strode out to mock the battle lines of Israel. (1Sa 17:1, 4-10) Ephes-dammim is apparently the same as Pas-dammim, mentioned at 1 Chronicles 11:13. Its location today is uncertain.
