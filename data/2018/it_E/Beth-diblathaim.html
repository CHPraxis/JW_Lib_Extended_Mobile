BETH-DIBLATHAIM

(Beth-dib·la·thaʹim) [House of Diblathaim].

A city in Moab (Jer 48:22) the location of which is unknown. However, it may be the place called Almon-diblathaim mentioned at Numbers 33:46. A city called Beth-diblathen is mentioned in the inscription of the Moabite Stone as having been built by Mesha, king of Moab.
