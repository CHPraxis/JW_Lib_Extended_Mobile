AMZI
(Amʹzi) [shortened form of Amaziah, meaning “Jehovah Is Strong”].
1. A Levite of the family of Merari and an ancestor of Ethan, who was one of the singers at Jehovah’s house appointed by David.​—1Ch 6:31, 44-47.
2. A priest, son of Zechariah, and an ancestor of Adaiah, who is listed as residing in Jerusalem and doing service at the temple in the time of Nehemiah.​—Ne 11:12.
