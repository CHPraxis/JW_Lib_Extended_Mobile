CONANIAH

(Con·a·niʹah) [Jehovah Has Firmly Established].

1. The Levite in charge of the contributions for temple service during Hezekiah’s reign.​—2Ch 31:4, 12, 13.

2. A Levite chief among those generously contributing sheep, goats, and cattle for the great Passover celebration held in the 18th year of Josiah’s reign.​—2Ch 35:9, 19.
