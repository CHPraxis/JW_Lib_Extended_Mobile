NEDABIAH
(Ned·a·biʹah) [Jah Is Willing (Noble; Generous)].
Last-named son of King Jeconiah (Jehoiachin), born during Jeconiah’s exile in Babylon. (1Ch 3:17, 18; 2Ki 24:15; Jer 29:1, 2, 4, 6) Nedabiah was a descendant of David of the tribe of Judah and an uncle of Zerubbabel, the postexilic governor.​—1Ch 3:1, 17-19; Hag 1:1.
