ELIEHO-ENAI

(Elʹie·ho-eʹnai) [Toward Jehovah Are My Eyes].

1. A gatekeeper of the Korahites appointed by David; the seventh son of Meshelemiah of the tribe of Levi.​—1Ch 26:1-3.

2. The son of Zerahiah who, accompanied by 200 males of the paternal house of Pahath-moab, returned from Babylon to Jerusalem with Ezra.​—Ezr 8:1, 4.
