HELEZ
(Heʹlez) [He Has Rescued].
1. One of David’s mighty men, a Paltite or Pelonite. (2Sa 23:8, 26; 1Ch 11:26, 27) When David organized the monthly service groups, Helez was given charge of the seventh division.​—1Ch 27:1, 10.
2. A descendant of Judah; son of a certain Azariah and father of Eleasah.​—1Ch 2:3, 39.
