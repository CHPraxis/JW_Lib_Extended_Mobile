HEBRONITES

(Heʹbron·ites) [Of (Belonging to) Hebron].

A Levite family descended from Kohath’s son Hebron. (Ex 6:16, 18; Nu 3:27; 26:58; 1Ch 26:23, 24) King David assigned 1,700 capable Hebronites to serve in administrative capacities over the region W of the Jordan and 2,700 over the territory E of the Jordan.​—1Ch 26:30-32.
