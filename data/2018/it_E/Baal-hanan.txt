BAAL-HANAN
(Baʹal-haʹnan) [Baal Has Shown Favor; Baal Is Gracious].
1. Son of Achbor; the seventh of eight kings of Edom who ruled “before any king reigned over the sons of Israel.”​—Ge 36:31, 38, 39; 1Ch 1:49, 50.
2. The Gederite whom David made chief “over the olive groves and the sycamore trees that were in the Shephelah.”​—1Ch 27:28, 31.
