MERIBATH-KADESH

(Merʹi·bath-kaʹdesh) [Quarreling at Kadesh].

A southern limit of Israel’s territory as seen by Ezekiel in vision. (Eze 47:13, 19; 48:28) The name Meribath-kadesh alludes to Israel’s quarreling with Jehovah at “the waters of Meribah” while dwelling at Kadesh.​—Nu 20:1-13; see KADESH, KADESH-BARNEA; MERIBAH No. 2.
