JAMIN
(Jaʹmin) [Right Hand].
1. The second-listed son of Simeon. (Ge 46:10; Ex 6:15; 1Ch 4:24) He founded the family of the Jaminites.​—Nu 26:12.
2. A descendant of Judah through Hezron’s grandson Ram.​—1Ch 2:9, 25, 27.
3. A postexilic Levite who helped explain the Law to the people assembled in Jerusalem.​—Ne 8:7.
