AHLAI

(Ahʹlai).

1. Likely the daughter of Sheshan of the tribe of Judah, given in marriage to her father’s Egyptian servant Jarha for whom she bore Attai. However, Ahlai, if actually a son of Sheshan, may have died early.​—1Ch 2:31, 34, 35.

2. Father of Zabad, who was a mighty man in David’s army.​—1Ch 11:41.
