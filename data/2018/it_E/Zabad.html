ZABAD

(Zaʹbad) [[God] Has Endowed].

1. An Ephraimite in the family of Shuthelah.​—1Ch 7:20, 21.

2. A descendant of Judah through Jerahmeel; his great-grandfather was an Egyptian; son of Nathan.​—1Ch 2:3, 25, 34-37.

3. One of David’s mighty men; son of Ahlai.​—1Ch 11:26, 41.

4. One of the assassins of King Jehoash of Judah; son of Shimeath the Ammonitess. (2Ch 24:26) He is also called Jozacar.​—2Ki 12:21; see JOZACAR.

5, 6, 7. Three of the Israelites whom Ezra encouraged to dismiss their foreign wives and sons; sons of Zattu, Hashum, and Nebo, respectively.​—Ezr 10:10, 11, 27, 33, 43, 44.
