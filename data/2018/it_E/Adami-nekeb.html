ADAMI-NEKEB

(Adʹa·mi-neʹkeb) [Ground of Piercing (Boring)].

A place in the southern part of Naphtali. (Jos 19:33) Its site is generally identified as Khirbet et-Tell (Tel Adami), above and W of Khirbet ed-Damiyeh, about 16 km (10 mi) ENE of Nazareth and approximately midway between Tiberias and Mount Tabor. Its position commanded a pass on an old caravan route between Gilead and the Plain of Acco.
