SHEBUEL
(Shebʹu·el) [He Returned to God; Captive of God].
The two men named Shebuel are both alternately referred to as Shubael.
1. A Levitical son or descendant of Moses’ son Gershom. (1Ch 23:15, 16) Shebuel’s (Shubael’s) paternal house was enrolled when David reorganized the Levitical services (1Ch 24:20, 30b, 31), being given duties that included caring for the stores.​—1Ch 26:24.
2. One of the sons of Heman and an expert musician selected by lot to head the 13th division of sanctuary musicians.​—1Ch 25:4, 6, 9, 20.
