SHEMUEL
(She·muʹel) [Name of God].
The same Hebrew name is also translated “Samuel.”
1. Head of a forefather’s house; son or descendant of Issachar’s son Tola.​—1Ch 7:1, 2.
2. Simeon’s tribal representative to the delegation that divided the Promised Land into tribal allotments; son of Ammihud.​—Nu 34:17, 18, 20.
