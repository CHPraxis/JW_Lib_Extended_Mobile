ESHEK
(Eʹshek) [possibly, Acting Violently].
A descendant of King Saul. The Scriptural record mentions that this Benjamite had three sons and that the sons of his firstborn, Ulam, “came to be valiant, mighty men, bending the bow, and having many sons and grandsons, a hundred and fifty.”​—1Ch 8:1, 33, 39, 40.
