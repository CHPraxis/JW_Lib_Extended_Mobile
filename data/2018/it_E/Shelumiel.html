SHELUMIEL

(She·luʹmi·el) [Peace of God].

A Simeonite chieftain. Shelumiel assisted with the national census that was taken about a year after the Exodus from Egypt. He was chieftain of the army of Simeon; also, he presented his offering when the tabernacle altar was inaugurated.​—Nu 1:4, 6; 2:12; 7:36-41; 10:19.
