KENAZ

(Keʹnaz).

1. One of the sons of Esau’s son Eliphaz and a sheik in the land of Edom.​—Ge 36:11, 15, 42, 43; 1Ch 1:36, 53.

2. The father of Othniel and Seraiah and brother to Caleb son of Jephunneh.​—Jos 15:17; Jg 1:13; 3:9, 11; 1Ch 4:13.

3. A descendant of Caleb the son of Jephunneh through Elah.​—1Ch 4:15.
