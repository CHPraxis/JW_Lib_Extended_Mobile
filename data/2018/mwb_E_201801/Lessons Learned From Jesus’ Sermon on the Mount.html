TREASURES FROM GOD’S WORD | MATTHEW 4-5

Lessons Learned From Jesus’ Sermon on the Mount

5:3

Are you conscious of your spiritual need?

The phrase “those conscious of their spiritual need” literally means “those who are beggars for the spirit.” (Mt 5:3; ftn.) We can show our eagerness to receive spiritual help from God by . . .

  • reading the Bible every day

  • preparing for and attending Christian meetings

  • reading our publications and, as time allows, material on our website

  • watching each month’s program on JW Broadcasting
