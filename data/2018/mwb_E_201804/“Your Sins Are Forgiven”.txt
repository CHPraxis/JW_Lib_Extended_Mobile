





TREASURES FROM GOD’S WORD | MARK 1-2
“Your Sins Are Forgiven”




2:5-12





What do we learn from this miracle?
  Sickness is linked to inherited sin

  Jesus has the authority to forgive sins and the power to heal sick people

  Under the Kingdom, Jesus will remove imperfection and sickness forever
