TREASURES FROM GOD’S WORD | JOHN 13-14

“I Set the Pattern for You”

13:5, 12-15

By washing the feet of his apostles, Jesus taught them to be humble and to perform lowly tasks in behalf of their brothers.

How can I show humility when . . .

  • disputes, or disagreements, arise?

  • I receive counsel or correction?

  • the Kingdom Hall needs cleaning or maintenance?
