TREASURES FROM GOD’S WORD | LUKE 2-3

Young Ones​—Are You Growing Spiritually?

From childhood on, Jesus set a fine example in seeking spiritual interests and in showing respect to his parents.

Young ones, how can you imitate Jesus in each of the following ways?

  • 2:41, 42

    Participating in spiritual activities

  • 2:46, 47

    Growing in spiritual understanding

  • 2:51, 52

    Respecting your parents
