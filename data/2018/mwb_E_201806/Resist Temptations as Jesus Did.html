TREASURES FROM GOD’S WORD | LUKE 4-5

Resist Temptations as Jesus Did

Satan tries to break our attachment to Jehovah by corrupting the desires of our heart. He adapts his tactics to each person’s inclinations and circumstances.

What powerful weapon did Jesus use to resist three common temptations? (Heb 4:12; 1Jo 2:15, 16) How can I imitate his example?

  • 4:1-4

    “The desire of the flesh”

  • 4:5-8

    “The desire of the eyes”

  • 4:9-12

    “The showy display”
