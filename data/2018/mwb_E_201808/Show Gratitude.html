Show Gratitude

17:11-18

What do we learn about gratitude from this incident?

  • Sincere expressions of appreciation give evidence of Christian love and are a sign of good manners

  • Those who wish to please Christ owe love and appreciation to all, regardless of nationality, race, or religion
