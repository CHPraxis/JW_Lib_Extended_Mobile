TREASURES FROM GOD’S WORD | LUKE 21-22

“Your Deliverance Is Getting Near”

21:25-28

Jesus will soon come as Executioner and Deliverer. We must prepare spiritually so that our deliverance will be assured.

HOW DO WE PREPARE?

  • By developing TRUST IN JEHOVAH,

  • which gives us COURAGE,

  • which leads us to a POSITIVE ATTITUDE,

  • which coupled with BROTHERLY LOVE

  • enables us to ENCOURAGE fellow believers
