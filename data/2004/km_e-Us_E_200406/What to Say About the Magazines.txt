What to Say About the Magazines
The Watchtower June 15
“Wouldn’t you agree that training children in today’s modern world is a challenge? [Allow for response.] Notice this assurance that parents can succeed. [Read Proverbs 22:6.] This issue of The Watchtower offers practical advice to help parents meet this challenge.”
Awake! June 22
“Some feel it is unscientific to believe in God. But did you know that there are scientists who say that science actually supports belief in a Creator? [Allow for response.] This issue of Awake! considers some of the evidence that has caused them to reach that conclusion.” Read Romans 1:20.
The Watchtower July 1
“In times of tragedy, many question whether God really cares about people and notices their suffering. Have you ever wondered about that? [Allow for response.] This magazine discusses how God shows concern for us today and how he has provided the basis for eliminating all suffering.” Read John 3:16.
Awake! July 8
“For many people, the teen years are a time of both joys and challenges, wouldn’t you agree? [Allow for response.] This magazine discusses the changes that adolescents undergo. It also examines some sage advice that can help them make the best of their teen years.” Read Ecclesiastes 12:1.
