What to Say About the Magazines
The Watchtower Feb. 15
“Many people are troubled by the bad things that have been done in the name of religion. Some feel that religion is at the root of mankind’s problems. Have you ever wondered about this? [Allow for response. Then read Revelation 18:24.] This magazine examines what the Bible says on this subject.”
Awake! Feb. 22
“Many people delight in learning about animals, and some enjoy having them as pets. [Allow for comment.] This magazine discusses some of the challenges of owning a pet. It also explains God’s promise of a time when all animals will be at peace with one another and with mankind.” Read Isaiah 11:6-9.
The Watchtower Mar. 1
“On one occasion, Jesus Christ was asked: ‘Which commandment is first of all?’ Notice his reply. [Read Mark 12:29, 30.] Have you ever wondered what Jesus meant? [Allow for response.] The article ‘How We Show Our Love for God’ examines the meaning of those famous words.”
Awake! Mar. 8
“At one time, many people lived in fear of a nuclear war. Do you think nuclear war is likely today? [Allow for response.] This magazine examines current developments that concern all of us. It also explains the Bible’s promise of a world where fear will be a thing of the past.” Read Zephaniah 3:13.
