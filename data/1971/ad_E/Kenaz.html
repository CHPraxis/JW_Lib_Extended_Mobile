KENAZ

(Keʹnaz) [hunting].

1. One of the sons of Esau’s son Eliphaz and a sheik in the land of Edom.—Gen. 36:11, 15, 42, 43; 1 Chron. 1:36, 53.

2. The father of Othniel and Seraiah and brother to Caleb son of Jephunneh.—Josh. 15:17; Judg. 1:13; 3:9, 11; 1 Chron. 4:13.

3. A descendant of Caleb the son of Jephunneh through Elah.—1 Chron. 4:15.
