EMEK-KEZIZ

(Eʹmek-keʹziz) [perhaps, low plain of cutting off, or, low plain cut off].

A Benjamite city. (Josh. 18:21) Emek-keziz’ exact situation is today unknown. The meaning of its name and its mention along with Jericho, Beth-hoglah and Beth-arabah in the Jordan valley may indicate a location in that vicinity.
