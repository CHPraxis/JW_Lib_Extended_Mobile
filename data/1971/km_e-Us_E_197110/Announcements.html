◆ Many planning a visit to the world headquarters of Jehovah’s witnesses in Brooklyn inquire as to how long it takes to tour the facilities. It takes approximately 1 hour and 45 minutes to tour the printing plant at 117 Adams Street, 45 minutes for the home and office at 124 Columbia Heights, and 20 minutes for the shipping facilities at 30 Columbia Heights. Facilities are open for tours Monday through Friday from 8 a.m. to 11:55 and from 1 p.m. to 5:40; Saturday from 8 a.m. to 11:55.
