A Night of Remembrance
YOU ARE INVITED
ANNUAL COMMEMORATION OF THE DEATH OF JESUS CHRIST
TUESDAY, APRIL 2, 1996
KINGDOM HALL OF JEHOVAH’S WITNESSES
Jesus himself instituted a memorial of his death on the night before he died. It was a simple ceremony. Jehovah’s Witnesses invite you to join them in observing this important event at the Kingdom Hall nearest to your home. Check with Jehovah’s Witnesses locally for the exact time and place of the meeting.
