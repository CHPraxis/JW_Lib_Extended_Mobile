A Night of Remembrance

You Are Invited

Annual Commemoration of the death of Jesus Christ

Wednesday, April 14, 1976

Kingdom Hall of Jehovah’s Witnesses

Jesus himself instituted a memorial of his death on the night before he died. It was a simple ceremony. Jehovah’s Christian witnesses invite you to join them in observing this important event at the Kingdom Hall nearest to your home. Check with the Witnesses locally for the exact time.
