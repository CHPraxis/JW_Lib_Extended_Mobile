● When recommendations are sent in by letter for appointment of elders, ministerial servants or pioneers, it will be appreciated if these are always signed by the congregation committee. If someone is away and cannot sign, please give the explanation. In most cases, of course, these recommendations are sent in on S-2 forms at the time of the circuit overseer’s visit.

● The price for all Watchtower and Awake! binders is now $2.00 to congregations and public, and $1.75 to pioneers.

● When ordering the 1977 Yearbook, publishers and pioneers are requested to do so through the congregation with which they are associated. Orders for the Yearbook in the following languages may be placed after the dates listed. December 1 — English. December 20 — French, Italian, Japanese, Portuguese, Spanish. January 20 — Dutch, German, Korean. Yearbooks are 50 cents each for publishers. These are cash items and so payment must accompany all orders along with a Remittance and Credit Request form. Regular and special pioneers on the list before July 1, 1976, may receive a free Yearbook by making request of the brother handling the literature. This will be included in the congregation’s order, and credit may be requested from the Society for the pioneers’ copies. Pioneers may obtain additional copies at 15 cents each.
