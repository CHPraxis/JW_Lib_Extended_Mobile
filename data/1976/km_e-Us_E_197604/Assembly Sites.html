Assembly Sites

The district assembly tentatively scheduled for Pontiac, Michigan, has been replaced by one in Detroit, Michigan, on June 23-26. The circuits affected by this, according to the list in the March issue of Our Kingdom Service, are noted below along with indication of the closest assembly:

June 17-20, Kalamazoo, Mich.: Ind. 1⁄4 7; Mich. 2⁄3 3, 9.

June 23-26 (Wednesday through Saturday), Detroit, Mich.: Ind. 1⁄4 7; Mich. 1, 2, 1⁄3 3, 5, 2⁄3 6, 8, 10, 11, 12; Ohio 2⁄3 5.

June 24-27, Kalamazoo, Mich.: Mich. 4-A, 1⁄3 6, 13.

Also, the Laurel, Maryland, district assemblies are now definite.
