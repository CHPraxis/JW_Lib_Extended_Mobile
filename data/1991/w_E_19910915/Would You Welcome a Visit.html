They never spoke of the name of God or of the coming Kingdom of God, which I found to be key subjects in my Bible. I have stopped going to my family church and have started to study the Bible, thanks to your truthful literature.

“I would like to begin fellowship with a local congregation of Jehovah’s Witnesses. Please send a message to them to come to my home so that we may talk.”
