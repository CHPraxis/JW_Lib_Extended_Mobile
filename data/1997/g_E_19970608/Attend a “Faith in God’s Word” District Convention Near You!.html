Attend a “Faith in God’s Word” District Convention Near You!

This three-day convention of Jehovah’s Witnesses begins on May 23, 1997, in the United States and will continue into early September for Spanish-language conventions. In the United States alone, 193 gatherings are scheduled: 143 in English, 35 in Spanish, and 15 in ten other languages—American Sign Language, Chinese, French, Greek, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Portuguese, Tagalog, and Vietnamese.

Don’t miss the many motivating Bible presentations and practical demonstrations. And enjoy the real-life drama about how Christians cope with anxiety. All sessions are free. Attend at any location listed below.
