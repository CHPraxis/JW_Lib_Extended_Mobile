
TREASURES FROM GOD’S WORD | JOB 28-32
Job Was an Example of Integrity



Job was determined to follow Jehovah’s moral standards

31:1
  He controlled his eyes and gave romantic attention only to his wife










Job was a good example in how he treated others

31:13-15
  He was humble, just, and merciful. He was considerate of all people regardless of their social or economic status










Job was generous, not selfish

31:16-19
  He was helpful and gave to those in need
