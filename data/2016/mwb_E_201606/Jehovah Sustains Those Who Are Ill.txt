
TREASURES FROM GOD’S WORD | PSALMS 38-44
Jehovah Sustains Those Who Are Ill



Faithful servants can be confident of Jehovah’s support during any adversity






41:1-4
  David became very ill

  David showed consideration to lowly ones

  David did not expect a miraculous recovery, but he looked to Jehovah for comfort, wisdom, and support

  Jehovah viewed David as a man of integrity
