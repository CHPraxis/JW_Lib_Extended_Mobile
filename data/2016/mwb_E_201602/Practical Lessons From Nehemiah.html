TREASURES FROM GOD’S WORD | NEHEMIAH 12-13

Practical Lessons From Nehemiah

Nehemiah zealously defended true worship

13:4-9, 15-21, 23-27

  • High Priest Eliashib allowed Tobiah, who was an unbeliever and an opposer, to influence him

  • Eliashib gave Tobiah a space in a temple dining hall

  • Nehemiah threw all of Tobiah’s furniture out, cleansed the room, and returned it to its proper use

  • Nehemiah continued to remove all uncleanness from Jerusalem
