
TREASURES FROM GOD’S WORD | 2 CHRONICLES 33-36
Jehovah Values Genuine Repentance



MANASSEH

Jehovah allowed him to be captured by Assyria and taken to Babylon in fetters





REIGN BEFORE CAPTURE
  Built altars to false gods

  Offered own sons as sacrifices

  Shed innocent blood

  Promoted spiritistic practices throughout the nation


REIGN AFTER RELEASE
  Humbled himself greatly

  Prayed to Jehovah; offered sacrifices

  Removed altars to false gods

  Urged nation to serve Jehovah





JOSIAH






THROUGHOUT REIGN
  Searched for Jehovah

  Cleansed Judah and Jerusalem

  Repaired the house of Jehovah; found the book of the Law
