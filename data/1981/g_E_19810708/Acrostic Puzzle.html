Acrostic Puzzle

Directions: Guess the words defined and write them over their numbered dashes. (As an additional clue, each cited scripture contains the word to be guessed or one similar to it.) Then transfer each letter to the correspondingly numbered square in the pattern. The filled pattern will contain a quotation reading from left to right. The first letters of the guessed words will form the name of the book from which the quotation was taken.
