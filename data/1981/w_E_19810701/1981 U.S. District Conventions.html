1981 U.S. District Conventions

The location of “Kingdom Loyalty” District Conventions to be held in Queens, N.Y., this summer has been changed, as follows:

July 30–August 2: ELMONT, N.Y. (Italian also), Belmont Park Race Track, Hempstead Turnpike at Cross Island Parkway.

August 6-9: ELMONT, N.Y., Belmont Park Race Track (above address).

August 13-16: ELMONT, N.Y. (Spanish and Greek only), Belmont Park Track (above address).
