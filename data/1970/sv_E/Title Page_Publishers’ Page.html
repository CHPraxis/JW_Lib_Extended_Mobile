Saving the Human Race​—In the Kingdom Way

THE HUMAN RACE includes you, the Reader. You would like to believe that the human race is worth saving. Men of affairs are now being driven to make frantic efforts to save the human race, not just for future generations, but for their own selves. For them, now is the critical time, or never! Do you think their efforts will succeed? The Creator of the human race has a better way than man’s, and it is bound to succeed in saving the human race. Why put yourself in line for disillusionment by trusting in man’s way? Save yourself time and get new hope by specializing on learning God’s way, the Kingdom way.

That you may do so, the Publishers have prepared this booklet that contains the public address that is being given world wide on this fascinating subject.

​—THE PUBLISHERS
