Theocratic News

◆ A special pioneer couple in Alabama enjoy conducting about 45 studies each month, with some 100 persons associated in these studies. More than 30 are attending meetings.

◆ The Chicago, Illinois, Fillmore Unit recently reported 103 publishers, all of whom made back-calls and conducted Bible studies during the month. Far several months the congregation has averaged two studies per publisher.

◆ Austria’s new peak of 9,263 publishers is 7 percent above last March.

◆ In spite of ban and continued opposition, Malawi had a 28-percent increase, with 18,629 publishers reporting.

◆ Lesotho reports a 34-percent increase​—406 publishers. Despite state of emergency there, 902 attended Memorial.

◆ A 33-percent increase for Togo​—1,470 publishers. Memorial attended by 4,994.

◆ For the first time the Philippines had 50,182 publishers reporting; a 10-percent increase.

◆ A new publisher peak of 4,142 for Peru is 23 percent over last March.
