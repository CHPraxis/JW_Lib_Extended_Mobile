Record Milk Production

● The average cow in the United States produces over 7,000 pounds of milk a year. Some cows average from 10,000 to 12,000. One cow on record, however, produced more than 42,000 pounds of milk in one year! During the year, this cow produced more than 70 quarts in a single day, on several occasions, compared to an average of from 20 to 32 quarts for other cows.
