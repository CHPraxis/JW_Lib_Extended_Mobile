Is Evolution an Established Fact?

This is the challenging title of Chapter 1 and the thought-provoking frontispiece of the fascinating book Did Man Get Here by Evolution or by Creation?

Had you ever thought there might be two sides to the question of the origin of man? To many persons the process of evolutionary development of mankind is accepted fact. So much so that many educators immediately dismiss any contradiction as “impossible” or, at least, as “theoretical” or “unprovable.” But, really, how “provable” is biological evolution?

On the one hand evolution is declared to be a fact, but on the other it is acknowledged by leading exponents that the process is “far from completely known,” the causes “only dimly discerned,” the difficulties “staggering.” Be armed with accurate knowledge.
