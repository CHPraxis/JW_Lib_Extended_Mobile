JEHOVAH keeps his purposes secret until due time to reveal them. “He revealeth the deep and secret things’’ in due time. (Dan. 2:22) “Surely the Lord God will do nothing, but he revealeth his secret unto his servants the prophets. ’ ’—Amos 3: 7.

The purposes of Jehovah are stated in his Word by his prophets, but no one can understand them until God by his power opens the seals. Chapter five of The Revelation calls attention to the seven seals on the scroll in Jehovah God’s right hand.
