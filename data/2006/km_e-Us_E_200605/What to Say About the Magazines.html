What to Say About the Magazines

The Watchtower May 15

“Do you think you would enjoy living under the conditions described in this inspired verse? [Read 2 Peter 3:13. Then allow for response.] This magazine explains what the new heavens and the new earth are. It also describes how life will be different when God fulfills his purpose for the earth.”

Awake! May

“Some people believe that to succeed in today’s world, you have to be aggressive. In contrast, note what Jesus recommended. [Read Matthew 5:5, 9.] Would you agree with that? [Allow for response.] This magazine highlights three benefits of being peaceable.” Feature the article that begins on page 28.

The Watchtower June 1

“In most cultures, there was a time when the elderly were treated with respect, as can be seen in this ancient law. [Read Leviticus 19:32.] Do you think that such respect is still common today? [Allow for response.] This magazine considers how God cares for the elderly and how we can do so also.”

Awake! June

“Terrorism is not new, but today it has grown into a worldwide phenomenon, touching almost everyone’s life. Why do you think that is? [Allow for response.] This issue of Awake! shows from the Bible when terrorism will end and how God will bring about real peace on earth.” Read Micah 4:4.
