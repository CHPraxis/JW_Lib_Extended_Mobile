A Night to Remember
On the night before he died, Jesus Christ instituted the Memorial of his death. Using wine and unleavened bread as symbols, Jesus commanded: “Keep doing this in remembrance of me.”​—Luke 22:19.
Jehovah’s Witnesses warmly invite you to join them in obeying Jesus by observing this annual event. Please see the back of this handbill or check with Jehovah’s Witnesses locally for the meeting place, the day, and the time.
