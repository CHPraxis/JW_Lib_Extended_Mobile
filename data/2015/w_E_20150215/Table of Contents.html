▪ Imitate Jesus’ Humility and Tenderness

The Bible encourages us to follow Jesus’ steps closely. (1 Pet. 2:21) As imperfect humans, can we follow the perfect example of Jesus? The first of these two articles considers how we can copy his humility and tenderness. The second article shows how we can imitate his courage and discernment.

▪ Preparing the Nations for “the Teaching of Jehovah”
