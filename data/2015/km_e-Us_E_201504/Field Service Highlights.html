Field Service Highlights

Some exciting features of the United States report for October 2014 include the following: A total of 785,888 Bible studies were conducted, an increase of 5,894 compared with the same month last year. It was also thrilling to see 2,911 baptized, an average of about 728 each week. If we make ourselves available, Jehovah can use us to help make disciples before the end comes.—Matt. 28:19, 20.
