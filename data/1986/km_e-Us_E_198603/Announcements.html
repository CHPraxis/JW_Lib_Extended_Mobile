Announcements

● Literature offer for March: One of the older 192-page books for 50¢. See January Our Kingdom Ministry for list of books to be used and for details as to getting credit on congregation literature account. April and May: True Peace and Security​—How Can You Find It? for $1.00. June and July: You Can Live Forever in Paradise on Earth for contribution of $2.50. Reduced-size books are $1.25.

● Some of the older publications we are offering in the field during March are no longer being printed. Therefore, any needing copies of these publications for their personal library should get them before supplies are depleted. Publishers should also be alert to offer these publications at libraries, rest homes, orphanages, reform schools, and similar institutions.

● At the Memorial celebration, there is no need to play music during the passing of the emblems. Speakers and those making arrangements should follow the directions in the Memorial talk outline and not endeavor to be innovative or introduce personal ideas.
