Is there life after death? Many people, including some doctors, believe so. Why? Because of the revelations of hundreds of patients who have had what are called “near-death” and “out-of-body” experiences. But have these people really had a glimpse of an afterlife? Do their experiences actually prove the existence of an immortal soul that survives the body’s death? These and other questions are examined in our opening series.
