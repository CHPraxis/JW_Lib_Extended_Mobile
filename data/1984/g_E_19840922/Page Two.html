Galileo was awed when he looked at the Milky Way through his telescope. Leeuwenhoek was appalled when his microscope revealed a heretofore unseen world teeming with life. Yet their discoveries were as nothing compared with those now revealed by the telescopes and microscopes of modern science. How have these revelations affected your faith in the Bible? Has it been undermined? Or strengthened?
