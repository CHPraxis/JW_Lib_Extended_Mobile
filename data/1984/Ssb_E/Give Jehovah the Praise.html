Song 48

Give Jehovah the Praise

(1 Corinthians 3:7)

1. Give praise to Jehovah.

By his love divine,

You have this commission

His truth to let shine.

You have nothing worthwhile

You did not receive

From God, your Provider;

To him honor give.

2. Set him e’er before you;

Keep self out of sight.

Thus for him you can shine

With heav’n’s radiant light.

Walk e’er in his presence;

Keep seeking his face

And always assigning

His work the first place.

3. Go forth with rejoicing

God’s service to do;

Give thanks for each priv’lege

He offers to you.

Give praise to Jehovah,

His Kingdom proclaim,

Till all on earth living

Will honor his name.
