TREASURES FROM GOD’S WORD | EZEKIEL 1-5

Ezekiel Delighted to Declare God’s Message

In a vision, Jehovah gave Ezekiel a scroll and told him to eat it. What was the significance of that?

2:9–3:2

  • Ezekiel was to absorb fully God’s message. Meditating on the words in the scroll would affect Ezekiel’s deepest emotions and motivate him to speak

3:3

  • The scroll tasted sweet because Ezekiel maintained a good attitude toward his assignment
