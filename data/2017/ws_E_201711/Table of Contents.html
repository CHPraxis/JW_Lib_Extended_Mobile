3 Make a Joyful Sound!

For Jehovah’s people, singing has always been an important part of worship. However, some may feel uncomfortable singing in front of others. How can we learn to sing praises to Jehovah with confidence? This article gives us good reasons to sing out joyfully and provides some suggestions on how to improve our singing voice.
