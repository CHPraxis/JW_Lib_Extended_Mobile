
Introduction



WHAT DO YOU THINK?

The ride of the four horsemen of the Apocalypse is one of the best-known scenes from the book of Revelation. Some are scared by it. Others are intrigued. Note what the Bible says about such prophecies:
“Happy is the one who reads aloud and those who hear the words of this prophecy.”​—Revelation 1:3.
This issue of The Watchtower explains how the ride of the four horsemen can mean good news for us.
