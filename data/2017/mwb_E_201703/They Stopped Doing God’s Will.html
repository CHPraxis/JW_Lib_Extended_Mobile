TREASURES FROM GOD’S WORD | JEREMIAH 5-7

They Stopped Doing God’s Will

7:1-4, 8-10, 15

  • Jeremiah boldly exposed the Israelites’ sins and hypocrisy

  • The Israelites considered the temple to be a talisman that would protect them

  • Jehovah let it be known that ritualistic sacrifices did not make up for wrong conduct

Consider: How can I make sure that my worship is in harmony with Jehovah’s will and does not become formalistic?
