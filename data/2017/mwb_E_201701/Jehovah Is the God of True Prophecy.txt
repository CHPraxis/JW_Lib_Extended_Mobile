
TREASURES FROM GOD’S WORD | ISAIAH 43-46
Jehovah Is the God of True Prophecy














About 200 years before the conquest of Babylon, Jehovah through Isaiah gave a detailed prophecy describing what would happen.
44:27–45:2
  Cyrus would be the conqueror of Babylon

  The city’s double doors would be left open

  The Euphrates River, a part of the city’s main defense system, would “dry up”
