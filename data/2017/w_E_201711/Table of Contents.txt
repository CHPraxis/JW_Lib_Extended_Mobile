WEEK OF DECEMBER 25-31, 2017
 3  Make a Joyful Sound!
For Jehovah’s people, singing has always been an integral part of worship. However, some may feel uncomfortable singing in public. How can we overcome the fear of using our voice to sing praises to Jehovah? This article gives us good reasons to sing out joyfully and offers some suggestions for improving our singing voice.
