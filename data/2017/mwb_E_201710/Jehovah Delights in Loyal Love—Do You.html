Jehovah Delights in Loyal Love​—Do You?

Loyal love is love motivated by commitment, integrity, loyalty, and deep attachment. Jehovah used the experience of Hosea and his unfaithful wife, Gomer, to teach a lesson in loyal love and forgiveness.​—Ho 1:2; 2:7; 3:1-5.
