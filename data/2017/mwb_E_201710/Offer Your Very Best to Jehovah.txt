Offer Your Very Best to Jehovah




14:2, 4, 9
Offering your very best to Jehovah pleases him and benefits you





YOUR RELATIONSHIP WITH JEHOVAH




            You offer Jehovah sacrifices of praise

            Jehovah grants you forgiveness, approval, and friendship

             You appreciate the benefits of obeying Jehovah’s commands, and your motivation to praise him increases


DID YOU KNOW?
Bulls were the largest and most valuable animals that the Israelites sacrificed. At times, bulls were offered in behalf of the priesthood or the entire nation of Israel. Jehovah considers the praise of our lips to be one of the finest sacrifices.







In what ways can I offer my very best to Jehovah?
