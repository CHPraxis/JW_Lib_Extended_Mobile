
TREASURES FROM GOD’S WORD | JEREMIAH 39-43
Jehovah Will Render to Each One According to His Works



Zedekiah disobeyed Jehovah’s direction to surrender to Babylon

39:4-7
  Zedekiah’s sons were slaughtered before his eyes. He then was blinded, bound with copper fetters, and imprisoned in Babylon until his death










Ebed-melech demonstrated trust in Jehovah and concern for His prophet Jeremiah

39:15-18
  Jehovah promised to safeguard Ebed-melech during Judah’s destruction










Jeremiah preached boldly for many years before Jerusalem was destroyed

40:1-6
  Jehovah protected Jeremiah during Jerusalem’s siege and arranged for him to be released by the Babylonians
