





TREASURES FROM GOD’S WORD | 1 TIMOTHY 1-3
Reach Out for a Fine Work




3:1, 13
It is best for brothers to begin reaching out when they are young. This allows them to receive training and demonstrate their fitness to be appointed as ministerial servants when they get older. (1Ti 3:10) How can a brother reach out? By cultivating and demonstrating the following qualities:
  Self-sacrifice.​—km 7/13 2-3 ¶2

  Spirituality.​—km 7/13 3 ¶3

  Reliability and faithfulness.​—km 7/13 3 ¶4
