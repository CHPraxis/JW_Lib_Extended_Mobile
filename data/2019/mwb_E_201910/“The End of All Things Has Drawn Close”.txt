
TREASURES FROM GOD’S WORD | 1 PETER 3-5
“The End of All Things Has Drawn Close”




4:7-9





We will soon face the greatest tribulation the world has ever seen. How can we keep spiritually strong now and into the future?
  Pray continually, using every form of prayer

  Develop intense love for our Christian brothers and sisters, drawing ever closer to them

  Show warm hospitality







ASK YOURSELF, ‘What are some practical ways that I can show intense love and warm hospitality to brothers and sisters locally and around the world?’
