TREASURES FROM GOD’S WORD | JAMES 3-5

Display Godly Wisdom

3:17

Jehovah’s wisdom is practical. For instance, it can help us to restore peace with fellow believers. If we have truly acquired godly wisdom, it will be evident in our conduct.

ASK YOURSELF: ‘Which of these aspects of godly wisdom have I displayed recently? How might I improve in displaying some of them?’

  • Pure

  • Peaceable

  • Reasonable

  • Ready to obey

  • Full of mercy and good fruits

  • Impartial

  • Not hypocritical
