TREASURES FROM GOD’S WORD | 1 JOHN 1-5

Do Not Love the World or the Things in the World

2:15-17

Satan uses the following three worldly enticements to try to draw us away from Jehovah. How would you explain them to someone?

  • “The desire of the flesh”

  • “The desire of the eyes”

  • “The showy display of one’s means of life”
