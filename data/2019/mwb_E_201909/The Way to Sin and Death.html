TREASURES FROM GOD’S WORD | JAMES 1-2

The Way to Sin and Death

1:14, 15

When improper thoughts enter your mind, do this:

  • Make a conscious effort to focus on something else.​—Php 4:8

  • Reflect on the hurtful consequences of giving in to temptation.​—De 32:29

  • Pray.​—Mt 26:41

When you have improper thoughts, what upbuilding things could you think about instead?
