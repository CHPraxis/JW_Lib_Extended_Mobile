TREASURES FROM GOD’S WORD | HEBREWS 11

The Importance of Faith

11:1, 6, 33-38

How can strong faith help you in the following situations?

  • You receive a challenging theocratic assignment.​—Heb 11:8-10

  • You lose a loved one in death.​—Heb 11:17-19

  • The secular authorities place restrictions on your worship.​—Heb 11:23-26
