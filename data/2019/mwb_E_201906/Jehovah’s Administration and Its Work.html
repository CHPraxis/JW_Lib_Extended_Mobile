TREASURES FROM GOD’S WORD | EPHESIANS 1-3

Jehovah’s Administration and Its Work

1:8-10

Jehovah’s administration is an arrangement to unify all his intelligent creatures.

  • It prepares the anointed for life in heaven with Jesus Christ as their spiritual Head

  • It prepares those who will live on earth under the Messianic Kingdom
