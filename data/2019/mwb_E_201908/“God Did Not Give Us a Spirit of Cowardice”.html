TREASURES FROM GOD’S WORD | 2 TIMOTHY 1-4

“God Did Not Give Us a Spirit of Cowardice”

1:7, 8

We can find strength in the apostle Paul’s inspired words to Timothy. Rather than be ashamed of the good news, we can boldly speak up to defend our faith, even if this requires “suffering adversity.”
