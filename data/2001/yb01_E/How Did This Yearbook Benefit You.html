How Did This Yearbook Benefit You?

A good report from a distant land is refreshing, as stated at Proverbs 25:25. There are many such reports in this “Yearbook.”

◂ In the reports, did you observe events that fulfill certain Bible prophecies?

◂ As you read this Yearbook, what evidence of Jehovah’s loving care for his servants especially impressed you?

◂ How did you benefit from the way that fellow Christians in other lands view the very difficult situations that confront them?

◂ What did you learn from the experiences of your Christian brothers that can help you to make your own ministry more effective?

◂ In what ways will the examples of faith recounted in this book affect your life?

◂ Which experiences did you mark because you felt that someone else would be helped if you shared these with him?
