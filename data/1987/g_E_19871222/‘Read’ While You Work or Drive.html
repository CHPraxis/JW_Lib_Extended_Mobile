‘Read’ While You Work or Drive

Do you have a hard time finding time to read such important material as The Watchtower? Now you can do such ‘reading’ while you work or perhaps while driving.

Beginning with its January 1, 1988, issue, The Watchtower will be available to subscribers on cassette tapes in English only. A recording of each issue of the magazine on a single cassette will be mailed to you, two cassettes a month, 24 for a year’s subscription. The contribution is only $36. For persons certified as blind or physically unable to read normal reading material, the year’s subscription will be only $12. Outside the United States and Canada, subscriptions are available only to the blind and visually impaired. Write the U.S. office concerning rates and certification.

Fill in and mail the accompanying coupon, along with the proper contribution, and receive your subscription to The Watchtower on cassette tapes.
