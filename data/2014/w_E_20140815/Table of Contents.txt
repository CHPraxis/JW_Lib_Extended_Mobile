STUDY ARTICLES
▪  What Is the Role of Women in Jehovah’s Purpose?
Learn what the rebellion against God in Eden has meant for men and women. Consider the experiences of some godly women of the past. Also, find out what role Christian women play in God’s purpose today.

▪  Use the Word of God—It Is Alive!
Every Kingdom publisher wants to be effective in the ministry. Consider some practical suggestions on how to use the Bible along with our tracts to engage people in conversations and to reach their hearts with Jehovah’s living Word.

▪  How Jehovah Draws Close to Us
We need to have a personal relationship with our Creator. Learn how Jehovah’s provisions of the ransom and of his written Word prove that he has taken the initiative to draw us close to him.

▪  Hear Jehovah’s Voice Wherever You Are
In order to walk in the way of the truth, we must listen to what Jehovah says. Learn how we can listen to Jehovah’s voice despite interference from Satan and from our own sinful inclinations. This article will help us to appreciate the importance of good communication with the true God.
