
Field Service Highlights




Harbor witnessing is established in 38 major ports in the United States. In January 2014, the harbor witnessing groups in Los Angeles, Philadelphia, and Seattle reported that 98 brothers visited 274 cargo ships. They placed 1,349 books, 289 magazines, and 2,110 brochures. They also conducted 75 Bible studies. Truly, merchants, shepherds, and traders are part of the expansion of Jehovah’s organization!—Isa. 60:6-9.
