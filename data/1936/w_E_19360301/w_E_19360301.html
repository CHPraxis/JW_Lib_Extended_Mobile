* Faithfulness means more than merely to obey the letter of the law. A faithful one is trusty, trustworthy, established, steady, firm, secure, true and certain. To be faithful means to be honest, constant and unchangeable. It means integrity. To maintain one’s integrity toward God he must be faithful.
