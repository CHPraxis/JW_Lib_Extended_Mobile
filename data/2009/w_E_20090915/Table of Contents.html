Purpose of Study Articles

Study Articles 1-3 PAGES 7-20

We know that in many ways Jesus was a model for Christians. This series of articles focuses on Jesus’ mental attitude and course of action. You will find examples that you can apply in your family and in the congregation as well as when coping with challenges.

Study Article 4 PAGES 21-25

How much do you appreciate the truths you have learned from God’s Word? This article will help us to reflect on the remarkable benefits of divine education. It also discusses the blessings we receive when we make sacrifices for the sake of the good news.

Study Article 5 PAGES 25-29

What has Jehovah done to provide for our deliverance from sin and death? At what cost? Knowing the answers can fill our hearts with appreciation and move us to show Jehovah how much we value the hope of deliverance that he and his Son have made possible.
