What to Say About the Magazines
The Watchtower July 1
“Do you think it’s possible to find inner peace in these troubled times? [Allow for response.] Notice what the Bible says at Philippians 4:6, 7. [Read.] This article explains how we can experience the peace that God gives.” Highlight the article that begins on page 10.
Awake! July
“Most of us are thankful for what our parents have taught us. Do you think it is disrespectful to examine the religious teachings we learned as a child? [Allow for response. Then read 1 John 4:1.] This article shows whether it’s wrong to change our religion.” Feature the article that begins on page 28.
The Watchtower August 1
“Some say that all religions are different roads that lead to the same God. Do you think that all religions are good? [Allow for response. Then read Matthew 7:13, 14.] This magazine outlines three specific things that the Bible says good religion should promote.”
Awake! August
“All of us have been victims of unfair discrimination. How do you think God feels about prejudice? [Allow for response. Then read Acts 10:34, 35.] The Bible can help us to cope when we are victims of discrimination. It also helps us to root out any prejudices that we may have. This magazine explains.”
