A Night to Remember
Please Meet With Us—Thursday, April 9, 2009
On the night before he died, Jesus Christ instituted the Memorial of his death, using wine and unleavened bread as symbols. Jesus commanded: “Keep doing this in remembrance of me.”—Luke 22:19.
Jehovah’s Witnesses warmly invite you to assemble with them in obeying Jesus by observing this annual event. Please check with them locally for the exact time and place.
