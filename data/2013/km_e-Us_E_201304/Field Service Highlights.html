Field Service Highlights

During the month of October, the United States branch territory reported three new peaks in regular pioneers: 75 in Bermuda, 54 in the Turks and Caicos Islands, and 153,039 in the United States. Additionally, the Bahamas reported an increase of 10.7 percent in regular pioneers over the same month the previous year. Truly, Jehovah’s name is being praised throughout the earth.—Ps. 98:4.
