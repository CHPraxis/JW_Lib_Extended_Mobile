Lesson 4

Acts 14:17

A rainy day.

“I’m stuck inside.

Why won’t it stop?”

Tomoko cried.

But then, surprise!

The sun appeared.

The rain was gone.

Tomoko cheered!

She ran outside, and to her delight, Tomoko saw an amazing sight.

Tomoko said, “I did not know that rain from God makes flowers grow!”
