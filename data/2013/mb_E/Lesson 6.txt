Lesson 6
Psalm 139:14
Look at your fingers, and wiggle your toes.
Point to your ears and then to your nose.
Look at those legs that allow you to run, to jump and to spin and have all kinds of fun!
Look in the mirror, and what do you see?
Jehovah made everything wonderfully!
ACTIVITIES
Read to your child:
Psalm 139:14
Have your child point to his or her:
Fingers   Toes   Nose
Ears      Mouth
Find the hidden objects.
Crab   Cat
Ask your child:
Who created you and me?
