Lesson 3
1 Peter 3:8
David learns that his friend is sick.
So he says: “I know what I’ll do.
I’ll write him a letter to make him feel better, and then I’ll deliver it too!”
Show kindness, and you’ll both be happy!
ACTIVITIES
Read to your child:
1 Peter 3:8
Have your child point to:
House   Desk   David
Sun     Bird   Tree
Ask your child:
Do you know someone who is not feeling well?
How can we help that person feel better?
