Lesson 4
Acts 14:17
A rainy day.
“I’m stuck inside.
Why won’t it stop?”
Tomoko cried.
But then, surprise!
The sun appeared.
The rain was gone.
Tomoko cheered!
She ran outside, and to her delight, Tomoko saw an amazing sight.
Tomoko said, “I did not know that rain from God makes flowers grow!”
ACTIVITIES
Read to your child:
Acts 14:17
Have your child point to:
Window    Bird    Tomoko
Tree      Flowers
Find the hidden objects.
Ladybug   Airplane
Ask your child:
Why did Jehovah create the rain?
