Lesson 1
Revelation 4:11
Who made the earth?
Who made the sea?
Who created you and me?
Who made the butterfly with its colorful wings?
Jehovah God created all things.
ACTIVITIES
Read to your child:
Revelation 4:11
Have your child point to:
Stars   Clouds   Sun
Boat    Earth    House
Sea     Butterfly
Ask your child:
What is God’s name?
Where does Jehovah live?
What did he make?
