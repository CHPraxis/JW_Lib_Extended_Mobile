District Assemblies in the British Isles for 1974
This year Jehovah’s Christian witnesses will be having four-day district assemblies in many parts of the world. In the British Isles the assembly dates and locations are as follows: July 11-14: Waterford, Ireland. July 18-21: Sheffield, Yorkshire. August 1-4: London. August 8-11: London; Edinburgh, Scotland. This assembly information is in addition to what was published in the February 1, 1974, issue of The Watchtower.
