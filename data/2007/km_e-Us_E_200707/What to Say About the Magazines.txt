What to Say About the Magazines
The Watchtower July 15
“The death of loved ones can cause much sorrow. Do you think that a part of them continues to live on? [Allow for response.] Jesus gave this encouraging promise. [Read John 5:28, 29.] Since Jesus said that a resurrection ‘is coming,’ this magazine explains from the Bible where the dead are right now.”
Awake! July
“Many people live their life the best way they can and feel that it is important to be a good person. Is that your approach to life? [Allow for response.] Notice the danger if our definition of what is good is different from God’s. [Read Proverbs 14:12.] This article discusses what it means to be a good person in God’s eyes.” Feature the article that begins on page 20.
The Watchtower Aug. 1
“I would like to get your opinion on something that Jesus said. [Read Matthew 5:3.] Do you feel that spirituality is important for happiness? [Allow for response.] This magazine shows what the Bible says about true spirituality and how we may obtain it.”
Awake! Aug.
“Where do you think parents can find advice that will really help them? [Allow for response.] Notice this promise made in the Bible. [Read 2 Timothy 3:16.] This magazine shows just how practical the Bible can be in helping parents raise happy children.”
