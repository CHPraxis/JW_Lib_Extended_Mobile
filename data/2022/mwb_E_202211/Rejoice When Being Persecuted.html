LIVING AS CHRISTIANS

Rejoice When Being Persecuted

Christians can expect to be persecuted. (Joh 15:20) Although persecution brings a measure of anxiety and sometimes pain, we can find joy when we endure it.​—Mt 5:10-12; 1Pe 2:19, 20.

WATCH THE VIDEO WE CAN REJOICE DESPITE PERSECUTION, AND THEN ANSWER THE FOLLOWING QUESTIONS:

What did you learn from Brother Bazhenov’s experience about the value of

  • reading the Bible daily?

  • receiving support from fellow Christians?a

  • praying frequently?

  • singing Kingdom songs?

  • talking about our faith?

a We can pray for imprisoned Christians, even doing so by name. However, it is not possible for the branch office to forward personal letters to them.
