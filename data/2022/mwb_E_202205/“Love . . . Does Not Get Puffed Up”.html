LIVING AS CHRISTIANS

“Love . . . Does Not Get Puffed Up”

Love helps us to show humility. (1Co 13:4) When we love our brothers, we do not view ourselves as superior. We see the good in others and use our abilities to help them. (Php 2:3, 4) The more we display such love, the more Jehovah can use us to do his will.
