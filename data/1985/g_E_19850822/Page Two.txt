Forty years ago this month, the bombing of Hiroshima ushered in the nuclear age. Yet, today many have forgotten what was learned from what happened then. To help readers recall, this issue of Awake! relates remembrances by survivors of that holocaust. And it explores the question, Has the lesson of Hiroshima been lost?
