What Made the Difference?

A schoolteacher in Central America asked that question regarding an exemplary student of hers. The child replied that she was one of Jehovah’s Witnesses and that each morning her family had a discussion of a Bible text published in the Witnesses’ Yearbook. From then on, the teacher asked her each day to read to the class the text and comment from the Yearbook.
