Child molestation, a growing horror! Although the cases number into the scores of thousands yearly, one child welfare league calls the figures “only the tip of the iceberg.” Who would stoop to such depraved and cowardly immorality against helpless children? Strangers, yes, but much more often the abusers are relatives of the victims or friends of their family. What steps can parents take to protect their children? This opening series reviews the problem and offers suggestions
