Theocratic News
◆ Italy and Spain both reported new peaks of publishers in September: 98,637 for Italy and 51,084 for Spain.
◆ France had a new peak of 875 regular pioneers in September and Puerto Rico had a new peak of 413 regular pioneers the same month.
◆ New peaks in publishers and studies were reached in September in a number of countries including Argentina with 41,959 publishers and 43,376 studies, Bolivia with 3,049 publishers and 4,433 studies and Ecuador with 6,524 publishers and 8,896 studies.
◆ Mexico reported 114,491 publishers in September—a new peak. They also had new peaks of 4,522 regular pioneers and 122,960 studies.
◆ Haiti reported that Bible studies in September were up 18 percent over the same month a year ago.
