● After September 1 congregations may order additional copies of the United in Worship book. Remember, this publication will not be used at present as a house-to-house campaign offer. As a start, we suggest you order about one book for each Bible study being conducted in the congregation as well as one for each publisher who may need a personal copy. When additional supplies are needed, these may be ordered.
