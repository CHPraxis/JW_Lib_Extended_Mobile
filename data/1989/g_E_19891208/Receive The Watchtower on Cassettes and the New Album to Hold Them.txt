Receive The Watchtower on Cassettes and the New Album to Hold Them
Along with millions of others, you may have enjoyed reading The Watchtower for many years. Now you can have each issue read to you. Fill in and mail the accompanying coupon, along with the proper contribution, and receive your subscription to The Watchtower on cassette tapes. For persons certified as blind or physically unable to read normal reading material, the year’s subscription is only $12.
In addition, you can receive the album for holding your entire year’s subscription of cassette tapes. This handsome blue vinyl album is only $7.
