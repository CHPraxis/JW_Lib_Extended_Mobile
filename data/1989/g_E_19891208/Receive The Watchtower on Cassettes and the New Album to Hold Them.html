Receive The Watchtower on Cassettes and the New Album to Hold Them

Along with millions of others, you may have enjoyed reading The Watchtower for many years. Now you can have each issue read to you. For persons certified as blind or physically unable to read normal reading material, the year’s subscription is only $12.

In addition, you can receive the album for holding your entire year’s subscription of cassette tapes. This handsome blue vinyl album is only $7.

Please send a year’s subscription for the cassette tapes of The Watchtower and/​or the new carrying album. Check the applicable box, and remit the proper contribution. (Outside the U.S.A., write to local Watch Tower branch for information.)
