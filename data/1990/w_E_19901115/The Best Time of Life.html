The Best Time of Life

A youth from Nigeria, West Africa, recently wrote: “Before, I thought that the time of youth was the hardest time of a person’s life and that the happiest age is the age when a person is a father or a mother or when he is an adult.”

But then this African youth received a copy of the book Your Youth​—Getting the Best out of It. “Thank you so much,” he wrote. “This book has helped me in so many ways.” He concluded: “Now, after reading this book, I realize that youth is the best age.”

Youths growing up in these troubled times face many new situations and must make weighty decisions. Should a youth smoke or accept drugs? What conduct is proper with one of the opposite sex? What about masturbation and homosexuality?
