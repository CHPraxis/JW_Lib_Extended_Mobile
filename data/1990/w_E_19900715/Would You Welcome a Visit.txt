Would You Welcome a Visit?
One of Jehovah’s Witnesses would like to visit you and help you increase your knowledge about God, his Kingdom, and his purposes as recorded in the Bible. We are confident that this information will help you, for it will provide reliable answers to problems that all of us face today. So if you would like to have a qualified person visit you in your home, or at some other convenient place, we will gladly arrange for it. You will be helped to find not only answers to your Bible questions but also answers to problems that can be solved by applying Bible principles. To avail yourself of this service, simply fill in and mail the accompanying coupon.
