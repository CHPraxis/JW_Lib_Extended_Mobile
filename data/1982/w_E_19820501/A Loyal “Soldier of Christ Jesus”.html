A Loyal “Soldier of Christ Jesus”

Johan Henrik Eneroth was born June 9, 1893. He symbolized his dedication to Jehovah God by water baptism on June 9, 1918. Two years later he entered full-time Kingdom service at the Watch Tower Society’s branch in Sweden. Later he served there for 50 years as branch overseer, as well as taking care of responsibilities from time to time in other northern European countries. Beloved by many for his loyal support of theocratic organization, Johan Eneroth finished his earthly course on February 7, 1982.
