We are said to live in the scientific age. Many understand this to mean that all basic truths must come from scientifically established causes and effects. Thus the progress of truth is directly related to the progress of science. But is this true? Truth will always result in benefit, but has science always been a benefit? Can one find truth beyond the reaches of science?
