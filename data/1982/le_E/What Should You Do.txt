What Should You Do?
50 Do you want to live forever in that beautiful paradise?
Then learn more about what God says. Try to learn to read the Bible.—John 17:3; Revelation 1:3
51 Learn more about Jesus.—Deuteronomy 18:18, 19; John 3:16; Acts 3:19-23
52 Try to do only good things and obey Jehovah.—Romans 6:17, 18, 22
53 Remember, Jehovah says we must not kill people.—Exodus 20:13; 1 John 3:11, 12
54 We must not take things that belong to other people.—Exodus 20:15; Ephesians 4:28
55 A man should not live and sleep with a woman unless she is his own wife.—Exodus 20:14, 17; 1 Thessalonians 4:3
56 Do you remember how many wives God allows a husband to have? How long should a husband stay with his wife?—Genesis 2:22, 24; Matthew 19:5, 6; 1 Corinthians 7:2, 10, 11
57 Remember, too, that we must worship only Jehovah.—Matthew 4:10; 1 Corinthians 8:6
58 Idols and images cannot help us. Why?—1 Corinthians 8:4
Is it good to have idols?—Deuteronomy 27:15; 1 John 5:21
59 Why is it bad to have charms and use juju?—Deuteronomy 18:10-13; Revelation 21:8
60 The bad angels or demons rebelled against God. They use fortune-tellers to mislead humans.—Acts 16:16
61 We must pray to God. Prayer means to talk to God, to tell him we want to serve him and to ask for his help.—Philippians 4:6, 7
62 We must obey Jesus and put faith in him.—Hebrews 5:9; John 3:16
63 Remember that he died to save us.—Romans 5:8
64 Remember that Jesus is our invisible King. We should obey him.—Philippians 2:9-11; Revelation 19:16
65 Jesus said that you should tell others about the good things you are learning and that those who want to serve God should be baptized.—Matthew 28:19, 20; John 4:7-15
66 So you can talk to your friends about these good things.—Matthew 10:32
67 If you learn to read well, you can learn many more things and will be able to help others better.—2 Timothy 2:15
68 Jesus also taught little children to obey God. He was never too busy to talk to them.—Matthew 19:13-15
69 Parents should always teach their children to obey God and love him.—Deuteronomy 6:6, 7; Proverbs 6:20-22; Ephesians 6:4
70 There are many different churches. Many of their teachings are not from the Bible. Jehovah tells us to leave religions that do not teach the truth.—Revelation 18:4; John 4:23, 24
71 Jehovah has people on earth who can teach you more about him. Do you know who they are?—Acts 15:14; Romans 10:14, 15
72 They are Jehovah’s Witnesses. They have peace among themselves. Do you know why? Because they love one another.—Isaiah 43:10-12; John 13:34, 35
73 Because they also love Jehovah, they have been baptized. This is how they show publicly that they have left their bad way of life and want to spend their lives serving God.—Acts 2:41
74 Jehovah’s Witnesses hope to live in the beautiful new paradise.—Psalm 37:9-11, 29
What can you do so as to live there with them?—James 1:22, 25; 2:20-26
75 Join with them in learning to serve Jehovah. They love and obey Jehovah and Christ Jesus. Do you love them? Would you like to help others to know about God?—John 6:45-47
76 Jehovah and Christ Jesus love you and want you to live forever in paradise.—John 3:16
What you have learned from considering the pictures and information contained in this booklet has surely given you the desire to enjoy life on earth forever. If you would like to learn more about this, we suggest that you talk to one of Jehovah’s Witnesses locally. Or, you may write, or have someone write for you, to the office nearest to where you live, as shown in the list of addresses on page two of this booklet.
