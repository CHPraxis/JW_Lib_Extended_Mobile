A Heavenly King Brings Blessings to Earth
41 Jehovah made Jesus King in heaven.—Isaiah 9:6; Daniel 7:13, 14; Acts 2:32-36
42 He will rule over the whole earth.—Daniel 7:14; Matthew 28:18
43 Do you remember what will happen to the bad people?—Psalm 37:9, 10; Luke 13:5
44 Do you remember the name of the first angel that sinned? Jesus will remove him and the other bad angels. Their idols and images will be destroyed.—Hebrews 2:14; Revelation 20:2, 10
45 Jesus will do many good things for the obedient people.—Hebrews 5:9
46 No one will be sick anymore.—Isaiah 33:24; Revelation 22:1, 2
Do you remember how Jesus healed sick people?
47 Everybody will have good things.—Isaiah 65:17, 21-23
48 God remembers even people who have died. He will use Jesus to bring them back to life again. This is called the resurrection.—John 5:28, 29; 11:25
49 After bad people are all killed off, nobody will die anymore. Even the wild animals will not be dangerous. Everyone will be happy forever.—Revelation 21:4; Isaiah 65:25; Psalm 37:11, 29
