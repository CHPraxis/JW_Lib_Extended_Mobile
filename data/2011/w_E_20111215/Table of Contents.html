Purpose of Study Articles

STUDY ARTICLE 1 PAGES 8-12

Some people mentioned in the Bible are good examples for us, but they may also serve as warning examples. This article will help us to examine Solomon from both standpoints. What can we learn from him that will help us live as Christians?

STUDY ARTICLE 2 PAGES 13-17

There is a powerful force in the universe that can guide us successfully through life in this wicked world. What is it, why should we want to be guided by it, and what can we do to benefit fully from its influence?

STUDY ARTICLES 3, 4 PAGES 18-26

Many servants of God in the past were filled with holy spirit. In what ways did God’s spirit operate upon them? Studying how Jehovah directed them will be of great encouragement to us in our service.
