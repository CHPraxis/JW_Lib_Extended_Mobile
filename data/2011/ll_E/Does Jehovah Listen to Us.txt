
PART 11
Does Jehovah Listen to Us?



God does hear our prayers. 1 Peter 3:12






Jehovah is the “Hearer of prayer.” (Psalm 65:2) He wants us to speak to him from our hearts.





Pray to Jehovah and not to anyone else.
Jesus taught us how to pray.​—Matthew 6:9-15.

To whom does God listen?​—Psalm 145:18, 19.







We can pray about many things. 1 John 5:14






Pray that God’s will be done in heaven and on earth.
Pray in the name of Jesus to show that you value what he did for you.





Ask Jehovah to help you do what is good. You can also pray about food, employment, shelter, clothing, and health.



Jehovah listens to people who do what is right.​—Proverbs 15:29.

Do not be anxious.​—Philippians 4:6, 7.
