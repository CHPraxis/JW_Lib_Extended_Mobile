HEZEKIAH
PROFILE
A faithful king who repaired and reopened God’s temple, destroyed objects of false worship, and urged people to observe the Passover. (2 Kings 18:4; 2 Chronicles 29:3; 30:1-6) Despite the bad example of his father, King Ahaz, Hezekiah “kept sticking to Jehovah.”—2 Kings 18:6.
ANSWERS
A. 25 years old.—2 Kings 18:1, 2.
B. 15 years.—2 Kings 20:1-6.
C. 185,000.—2 Kings 19:15, 19, 35, 36.
