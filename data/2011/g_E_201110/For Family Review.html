The second son of David and Bath-sheba. Solomon ruled Israel for 40 years. He built a great house for Jehovah’s worship. (1 Kings 5:2-5) Jehovah used Solomon to write Proverbs, Ecclesiastes, and Song of Solomon. Bad association with foreign wives led him away from Jehovah.—1 Kings 11:1-6.
