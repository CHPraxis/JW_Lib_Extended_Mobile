Field Service Highlights

In November 2010, the United States reported a new all-time peak of 136,885 regular pioneers. This is an increase of 188 over the previous peak in October 2010. Also, the Turks and Caicos Islands reached a new all-time peak of 35 regular pioneers in November. Bermuda had an increase of 6.1 percent in regular pioneers over the same month last year. What a joy to see Jehovah’s people “intensely occupied with the word”!—Acts 18:5.
