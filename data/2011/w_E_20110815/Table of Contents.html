Purpose of Study Articles

STUDY ARTICLES 1, 2 PAGES 8-16

The Hebrew Scriptures contain scores of Messianic prophecies. Examining some of them will enable you to identify the promised Messiah. The information in these articles should prove useful to you as you engage in the ministry. The material will also undoubtedly strengthen your faith in Jehovah’s prophetic word.

STUDY ARTICLES 3, 4 PAGES 23-31

The unity enjoyed by Jehovah’s Witnesses is unique, and we should never take it for granted. The first article highlights Bible examples that encourage us to be peacemakers. The second article shows how we can pursue peace.
