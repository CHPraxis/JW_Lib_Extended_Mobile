Field Service Highlights

The Turks and Caicos Islands reached new peaks in Bible studies, return visits, regular pioneers, and literature placements in January 2011. Also, when compared with the first five months of the previous service year, Bermuda saw a 4.9 percent increase in publishers and a 3.3 percent increase in Bible studies while the United States had a 7 percent increase in regular pioneers and an 8.8 percent increase in auxiliary pioneers. Let us continue to ‘fully accomplish our ministry’!—2 Tim. 4:5.
