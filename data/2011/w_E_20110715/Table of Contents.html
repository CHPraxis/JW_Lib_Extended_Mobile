Purpose of Study Articles

STUDY ARTICLES 1, 2 PAGES 10-19

Jehovah lovingly warns us about negative influences that could cause us to veer off the road that leads to everlasting life. These two articles discuss six bad influences and how we can avoid them.

STUDY ARTICLES 3, 4 PAGES 24-32

The Bible says that after man’s creation, Jehovah “rested” on the seventh day. (Heb. 4:4) The first article in this pair will explain the purpose of God’s rest day and why it is relevant to us. The second article will show some ways in which we can prove that we personally have joined Jehovah in his rest.
