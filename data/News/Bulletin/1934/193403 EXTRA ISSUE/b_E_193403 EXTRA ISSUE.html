Tile portable transcription machine activity throughout the earth is going ahead with leaps and bounds.

A method that has been found very practical in the large cities and should also work ont advantageously in the smaller places, is the following :

The brethren come in contact with a home where considerable Interest is manifest by the people. Inquiry is made of that party if they would like to have a transcription meeting in their home to which they could invite their neighbors, friends and relatives and if they would be willing to have any other outsiders who would be interested in such a meeting also attend. If they give assent they are provided with as many invitations as they think they can use among their relatives, friends and neighbors dnd are requested to personally invite these people to this meeting.
