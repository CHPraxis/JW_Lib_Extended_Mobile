During the months of July, August and September the rural territory should be completely and thoroughly worked; the combination to be used, one bound book and six booklets, as suggested in the last Bulletin.

It is true that the people in the rural districts are undergoing considerable hardship due to the drought and the depression in general. They are losing their stock, and their homes; and the prospects for the future are very bad. The Kingdom has the only permanent and real solution for their trouble. Many think that by sympathizing with people in these conditions they are being a comfort to them, where in reality they are contributing nothing .to their welfare; but if we leave the Kingdom message in their hands in printed form it will be a real aid to them ; it will give them something worth while to look forward to. Therefore the entire rural territory should be thoroughly covered before Sept. 29, the next Testimony Period.

Where the people do not feci that they can contribute 50c (so as to have the Preparation book and six booklets), Jehovah’s witnesses should stress particularly their need of having six of the new booklets, because six booklets will cover such a variety of subjects. The Lord’s anointed rallied to this occasion with tremendous zeal and determination. During the month of September more than two million pieces of literature were placed in the hands of the people, aud these mostly with Catholics.
