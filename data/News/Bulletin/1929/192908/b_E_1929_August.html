THREE BIG DAYS

As a climax to August IBSA Week we have Saturday, Sunday and Monday, Angust 31 to September 2, during which practically all the friends will be released from their secular employment and thus be enabled to devote the entire time to the witness work. There will, consequently, be more time available for the work than there was during May IBSA Week.

A method that has been found very beneficial in using periods like this has been to have a number of the friends get together on Friday evening or Saturday morning and go to some of the larger towns in the outlying territory and establish a headquarters, and work from that headquarters during the three days. Thus they save time and energy and give a much more effective witness than if they hud to drive to the territory every morniug.
