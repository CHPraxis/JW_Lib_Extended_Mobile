The Lord Has Given the Angels Charge over the Remnant

The Lord has provided bountiful protection for his people, and we believe it would be of interest to every one to read the little write-up that appears in the calendar for February,

“As Boaz charged these young men concerning Ruth, so the Lord has given his angels charge over the remnant in this day, using them to convey to his workers the reviving and life-sustaining waters of truth, that they might go forward doing the work of Jehovah’s witnesses in the earth. The remnant now rejoice to see how the Lord, during the years recently past, has employed his angels to place before them his life-giving message of truth and permit them to have something to do with passing it on to one another.” —Preservation, page 269.

The Devil is not at all pleased with the remnant’s passing this truth on to others, and is making every effort to attack the remnant and cause them to be arrested or hindered In the progress of this work. We relate here how the Lord has protected his people who arc making every effort to carry the life-sustaining truth to the people of good will.

In Alexandria, Va., a Greek brother was arrested while preaching the message to a colored man. It happened that he had offered the Prohibition booklet. He was arrested, brought before a judge, and the local brethren took It upon themselves to pay his bail. The judge said that it would be necessary to have a regular attorney appear at the hearing; therefore the brethren hired a local attorney. At the hearing, the hired attorney cross-examined the complaining officer and the colored witness in tbe usual technical style. Then he placed an English-speaking brother on the stand who tried to outline the nature of the Society's work. The brother said he knew the defendant to be a sincere and true follower of Christ. The judge then angrily snapped, “Cut that out; I don’t want to hear a sermon.” The attorney theu had to go into technical questioning and tried to bring out some of the facts about the Society’s work, but was cut off by the judge and threatened to be committed for contempt if he persisted in that line of testimony. After brief questioning of the Greek brother by the attorney, the judge requested one of the booklets. The Prohibition booklet was given him. The attorney summed up the case at great length. Finally the judge said in conclusion, “After examining this book I find that it is utterly impossible to sell this for a profit at 5c. In addition, the foreword of this pamphlet is very pleasing to me. It I

Another arrest had taken place In Jersey City, N, J. There it happened that three colored brethren had been taken to police headquarters for several hours' questioning and then released. On advice from the Society, three brethren in a New Jersey company called upon the chief of police, outlining to him the nature of our work, and cited the needless interruptions. The chief of police said he know nothing about the matter, aud arranged an interview for the committee of three with the head of the detective bureau, to whom he spoke and said that the committee would explain about the arrest of some colored people who should not have been arrested. These are only two of many arrests which have occurred recently. The Devil and his organization are trying hard to stop the Lord’s work; but by putting full trust in Jehovah, His protection is provided and the work of tiie Kingdom progresses.

“In Mexico there are not many good roads. Some of them can be traveled over only by oxcarts. Some roads in the mountains are mere donkey or foot paths.
