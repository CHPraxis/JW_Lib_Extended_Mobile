Plan Evening House-to-House Witnessing Groups

The mighty postwar United Nations monstrosity is rearing its ugly head over the earth issuing forth a loud, arrogant bid for universal rule in defiance of Jehovah’s righteous kingdom. This defiant challenge is heard by all people on the earth, and they are now forced to take sides with either the Devil’s old world headed for sure destruction at Armageddon or the side of the righteous kingdom of Jehovah God. These bragging claims do not go unanswered by the great God of the universe, but Jehovah sends forth to all these people the opportunity of learning of and living under his righteous kingdom forever. This he does by sending forth his witnesses who confess his name to all nations so that those of goodwill may be made glad and rejoice with the Lord's favored ones.

The light of God's Word shines forth in these troublous days like a beacon light at midnight lighting the way for all those in this world of blackness and distress to come unto the great Source of light and understanding, Jehovah God. Jehovah lias been using the Watchtower magazine to spread forth the light of truth for 67 years. The number of people reached by this magazine is rapidly growing, and by the time Armageddon strikes the nations will know that Jehovah is God and the rightful ruler of the universe.

During January of this current Watchtower Campaign a total of 39,768 new subscriptions were put through the Brooklyn oilice, to compare with 20,656 entered last year during the same period of time. Up to the date this went to press we had entered 74,000 new subscriptions, and there are many more in the mail. This shows real effort to ‘make glad the nations’ with Jehovah’s joyful message. Plan Evening Activity

The days are now getting longer and it is possible to engage in the house-to-house work much later in the evening than during the winter months. Now that most of the winter weather has passed, this is the time to make plans for increasing our activity in the field service. Servants should make arrangements for additional evening group witness service during the weekdays so that more territory can be covered with the Kingdom message and the people of good-will invited to attend the public meetings and other headquarters studies where they may gain the necessary knowledge and appreciation that will lead them to life in the New World. Publishers will gladly support such group service arrangements and increase their hours in the field service. Thus they will be confessing unto others what they themselves believe in their hearts. “For with the heart man believeth unto righteousness; and with the mouth confession is made unto salvation.” (Rom. 10:10) It is necessary to have the right heart attitude and faith in the Lord’s promises, and then with the mouth speak forth what is in the heart. This preaching or confession is required by Jehovah’s righteous law in order to bring salvation to the one who confesses these Kingdom truths to other men of good-will.

Our Leader and Commander, Christ Jesus, set us the example of how this confessing is to be done by devoting his time to preaching directly to the people of good-will in their homes. He instructed the disciples how to properly and tactfully engage in the house-to-house witnessing, and impressed upon them the fact that this was necessary to insure their own salvation as well as publicize the Kingdom gospel. Today, the same law applies. Instead of being content with just spending our time passing out handbills or engaging in the magazine work only, we should devote a good portion of our time to the actual house-to-house confession of our knowledge. Every Kingdom publisher should be skilled in wielding “the sword of the spirit” in door-to-door conversations with the people. By spending more time in the service we shall place more literature and obtain more subscriptions, as well as enable ourselves to make more back-calls and start more studies. This, in turn, will reflect favorably in our reports for the month.

Call Back on the Good-Will

After we place literature or obtain subscriptions we should never forget to call back on these persons. Our confession to each person has not been completed until we exhaust every means possible to aid these of good-will to gain a knowledge of the truth. We should call back on these people again and again until they either follow along and take their stand for the Lord's organization or definitely make it known that they do not wish to receive these blessings. Go back within two or three weeks and leave them the next issue of the magazine if their subscription is not coming along yet. Prove to them that you are interested in their welfare, by calling back. Each time we call we should be well prepared, knowing how to present the truth and how to use all of the equipment needed for a back-call. Thorough previous preparation is essential.

“Be Glad, Ye Nations” New Booklet Released at Baltimore Assembly


Ten Million to Be Distributed!

First at Cleveland, next at Copenhagen, then at London, and finally at Baltimore, eager multitudes listened with rapt attention as Brother Knorr delivered the speech “Be Glad, Ye Nations”. With masterly logic he proceeded to marshal the Scriptural testimony and physical facts disclosing the real secret for gladness and how to be among the only joyful people on earth today.

Why be glad now? “The transcendent reason ... to rejoice and be glad is the establishment of the kingdom of God and the operation of it for the vindication of Jehovah’s universal sovereignty and his sacred name.”

So that men of good-will everywhere may share In this rejoicing, the gladsome invitation has been put in booklet form: “Be Glad, Ye Nations". It contains 64 pages and has been given a beautiful colored cover in keeping with its heartcheering message, and is now being printed by the millions.

Jehovah’s Servants Vindicated

This booklet not only contains the most forceful and joyous presentation of the Kingdom theme yet given, but in “Jehovah’s witnesses in the Crucible” we have the finest argument ever published proving that Jehovah’s witnesses, and they alone, are the true servants of the Almighty God Jehovah. Those who were fortunate enough to hear the powerful delivery of this message by Brother Knorr last fall at Cleveland will never forget it.

With telling effect it refutes the Hierarchy’s taunting charge, “They have no evidence whatever that Jehovah ever asked them to be His witnesses,” by showing the Scriptural qualifications of speaking in the name of Jehovah, turning the people to Him, and teaching them His Word as clearly applying to the witnesses of Jehovah and to none others. Strikingly contrasted is the course of the Hierarchy with that of Jehovah’s witnesses. On the one hand we have the evidence of an organization, claiming to be Christian, conspiring with the totalitarian forces of darkness in their efforts to rule the world, playing favorites with them and sharing in their victories; and on the other hand we have the record of the true witnesses of Jehovah: the letters and telegrams of October 7, 1934, so clearly stating their position, official documents, records from the public press, private letters, etc., all bearing unequivocal testimony to their immovable stand for the right worship; never for a moment compromising in spite of their receiving the worst treatment accorded to any in camps notorious for their revolting sadistic brutality and fiendishness.

Jehovah's witnesses came through that fiery trial with their organization as witnesses of the true and living God unconsumed, unsinged, and unsullied. They stand vindicated as being true to their Godgiven name. Vindicated, not by the actions of official bodies in lifting the restrictions placed upon them, but by the Almighty God himself. He it is that sustained them. To Him belongs the praise. “If it had not been Jehovah who was on outside, w’hen men rose up against us; then they had swallowed us up alive.”—Psalm 124:2,3, A. 8. V.

We can! How? By each company publisher’s accepting as an obligation before the Lord the minimum quota of 100 booklets, and each full-time publisher 500, and then talking and placing booklets until all of them are In the hands of the people. Do so, first of all, by engaging regularly in the house-to-house work, endeavoring to leave a booklet at each home called on during April, in connection with the Watch tower Campaign, and continuing through May and June. Keep singing it by placing the booklet at every opportunity. By each publisher’s putting his shoulder to the wheel our goal of ten million can be reached.

During the campaign months of April, May, and June, a special rate of two cents a copy for companies will apply. Pioneer rate will be one cent per copy. Companies and pioneers should order supplies immediately, and wherever possible combine their orders so as to save shipping charges.

Kingdom News No. 15

The new Kingdom News, No. 15, released at the Northeastern Assembly of Jehovah's witnesses, at Baltimore, Md., Friday evening, February 8, is now available for distribution. Order your supply immediately.

The title of the subject matter, “World Conspiracy Against the Truth,” is striking, but no more so than the series of pertinent questions regarding the world-wide crisis we are now facing, and their forceful Scriptural answers. This Kingdom News is not to be passed around carelessly or indiscriminately, to be thrown away and trampled underfoot by those receiving it. Its message is too important and valuable for that. It should be delivered to the homes of tlie people and handed to the person answering the door, with a brief, dignified statement emphasizing the importance of the message.

Cover each individual territory, group-witnessing assignment and public meeting area once; not once for each type of work, but cover the territory thoroughly once with the Kingdom News. One good way to cover group-witnessing territory and public-meeting territory with the Kingdom News is to have as many publishers as possible devote one or more evenings a week to that work, covering the section that you contemplate working that week-end. In such evening work the booklet, “Be Glad, l’e Nations", in addition to the Kingdom News, may be presented on a contribution, or in combination with other booklets, if you so desire. Be sure to leave a Kingdom News in every home where there are people who will read it.
