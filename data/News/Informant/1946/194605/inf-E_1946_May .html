A Month in Which to Show Forth His Praise!

The all-important issue of Jehovah’s supremacy presses ever closer for final settlement. World leaders insist not only that they can remedy the steadily increasing sorrows of mankind, but that they can build a world of wondrous opportunities, with security, freedom and plenty for all. Standing squarely in opposition to those vain imaginations, Jehovah’s chosen people lift high their voices in the bold proclamation that He alone is God, and that his righteous government is the only hope for men. Thus the issue is squarely joined. The people for the issue cannot be silent or inactive. During May they will rejoice to show forth Jehovah’s praise, because they have been formed for that very purpose. Our text for May’s service activities sets forth these precious facts, as recorded at Isaiah 43: 21: “This people have I formed for myself: they shall shew forth my praise.”

Abundance of Offers During May

What a busy month May is going to be! There will not be an idle moment as the three main lines of activity are engaged in. First comes the main offer of the month, 3 bound books and the booklet “Be Glad, Ye Nations”, on a contribution of 50c. All the bound books from The Harp of God to Religion will be used in this presentation. Beginning with The Harp, Deliverance, Creation, etc., pioneers and companies will make up combinations according to the books available. Pioneers, especially, should make up combinations of six, on a dollar contribution, and see that all interested persons are given the opportunity to obtain these bound books while the special offer lasts. This provides an excellent opportunity for all brethren and goodwill interest to complete their personal sets with any books that are now missing. All the people whose names are on the back-call files, and those subscribing for The Watchtoicer, are excellent prospects for visiting with these various book combinations. Call their attention to the various references to the bound books made in The Watchtower and what a great assistance the books will be to them in their search for truth. This will be a great opportunity to clear the shelves of books that will do far greater good in the hands of the i)eople of good-will. Companies and pioneers will, of course, co-operate with each other in placing whatever stock of these books is on hand. Orders will be filled from the Brooklyn office and the depots according to the supply available. At Brooklyn we are out of stock of all books from The Harp of God to Riches; therefore

do not include these in orders sent to the Brooklyn office. Our largest stock is the book Salvation; hence these should be stressed when ordering from the Brooklyn office. By unified and diligent action during May and June, all the present supply of these books can be left in the homes of the people for their comfort and enlightenment.

Issue Emphasized

Every one of these volumes, The Harp of God, Deliverance, Creation, Reconciliation, Government, Life, Prophecy, Light (1 and 2), Vindication (1, 2, and 3), Preservation, Preparation, dehor ah, Riches, Enemies, Salvation, and Religion, stands four-square for the issue of Jehovah’s supremacy. They are all dedicated to the exaltation of his holy name. They show forth His praise alone. They will enable all who read them to clearly understand the issue of universal domination, guiding all lovers of righteousness to take their stand on God’s side and live. For all these reasons, the people for the issue will rejoice to take these books to the people during May and June, and by specially concentrating on the back-call names not only will back-call activity be increased, but the foundation will be laid for many book studies. Follow up all interest found, so that many others may come to know of and serve the Lord and show forth his praises.

The Society will credit the companies, where desired, with 15c for each combination placed; so, if the credit is wanted, show on the bottom of the company monthly report card the number of combinations of 3 books placed by the company publishers, and the literature account will be credited accordingly. (Company publishers may obtain these books on contribution of 15c each during the campaign.) Pioneers obtaining books from a company will contribute the regular pioneer rates to the company, and a statement showing the complete transaction, signed by both parties involved, should be sent to the Society, so that the company account can be adjusted. All exchange of literature between companies can be settled locally, each company co-operating in using up all the stock of the older books on hand.

While diligently presenting the offer of 3 bound books, we have the joy of also offering the new booklet, “Be Glad, Ye Nations”. It will be left with the three-book combination, of course, but where the combination of 3 bound books cannot be placed one bound book and the booklet will be offered, on a 25c contribution. Additionally, however, this booklet, with its message of joy and hope, will be left everywhere possible. It is an honor to Jehovah

Subscription Period Extended

The third line of activity will be the extension of the special subscription offer of the Watchtoicer Campaign to the 15th of May, so that all on our back-call lists may have full opportunity to avail themselves of the subscription offer, and also others who may not have been able to contribute during the campaign but desired to do so at a later date. You will all rejoice to know that the office is still receiving vast numbers of subscriptions. The total number now entered is 195,000, for which we give thanks to Jehovah.

Right in line with the terms of their commission, the people for the issue will show forth His praises by standing in all prominent places with The Watchtoicer and Consolation. Street-corner and store-to-store magazine activity will be in full swing during May; and here again the Lord’s blessing is so manifest upon the efforts of His servants that it has required an extra night crew in the mailing department at the Society’s factory to handle the great demand for the magazines. Does not all this evidence of Jehovah’s favor upon the activity of his chosen ones cause you to rejoice and press on with increased zeal to greater exploits by His grace?

Still further, in this busy month the public meeting activity will be zealously engaged in. By now the speakers have the new talks prepared, and it will be a great joy to invite the people throughout the territory to come and hear the new series. How aptly, therefore, the text for the month describes Jehovah’s chosen in action! They have been formed for “myself”, says the mighty Lord of Hosts. They are the people for the issue; “they shall shew forth my praise.”

The Pioneer Call

Each One to Consider

Are you serving Jehovah with a perfect heart? That is the question that each consecrated servant must answer today. True, there is a great deal of comfort in the realization that God requires perfection of heart devotion rather than perfection of the flesh, never making a mistake. And it is gratifying to know that every one of His servants can be pleasing to Him and receive His rewards, regardless of one’s circumstances and conditions, regardless of how much or how little one may be able to do in Jehovah’s service.

But there is also a serious and sobering thought connected with that. To have His approval we cannot hold back anything. We cannot make excuses by comparing ourselves with others (an unwise thing to do; see 2 Corinthians 10:12), but must realize that regardless of what any other creature may or may not do, we must be perfect in faith and wholly given over to serving Jehovah God. Thousands, fully appreciating this fact, have entered the pioneer service, and are thus walking perfectly before their Creator. Does walking with a perfect heart before Jehovah mean the full-time service for you? Only you yourself can answer that question. However, we are confident that there are many, even thousands, who, upon carefully considering that question, will come to the conclusion that they too can, yes, must serve Jehovah full-time so as to walk perfectly before Him. It was to assist these very ones to see their privileges that three letters regarding the pioneer service were sent to all companies in recent months.

Vacation Pioneer

Then there is the vacation pioneer privilege. You school teachers, school children, and others who will be having several months’ vacation soon, what are you doing about your opportunity to serve Jehovah perfectly during the summer vacation? Are you preparing to enjoy the blessings of full-time service, of daily bringing honor to Jehovah’s name, and of daily inviting the people of good-will to be glad with His people? Will you keep busy this summer “redeeming the time” as a pioneer publisher?

In addition to the many blessings that will be yours as full-time publishers, you youthful publishers will be laying a good foundation for full-time service when you grow up. Then, becoming a year-round pioneer will not seem to be a hard thing.

Let each consecrated servant of Jehovah God who is not already a full-time worker carefully examine himself to determine if he is perfect in faith and wholly given over to the service of Jehovah God. If such examination reveals that the full-time service is also for him, then let him send for a pioneer application today. (Summer pioneers should specify that a vacation pioneer blank is wanted.)

“Defense Through Knowledge” Testimony Period Right Knowledge Shelters God’s People


June Features Intense Educational Work

This world possesses much knowledge. Yet, with centuries of learning behind them, can men of this world point to one instance in whicli knowledge has served them as a real defense? They cannot. Even within this generation, with its magnificent schools and brilliant educators, worldly wisdom has not shielded mankind from the ravaging effects of two world wars, with its horrible aftermath of famine, disease, pestilence, and death of millions. Nor will it furnish protection against the coming world disaster, Armageddon.

Nevertheless, knowledge is a defense. Not ‘knowledge falsely so called’, but true knowledge, gained through careful study of God’s Word, knowledge that Jehovah is reigning and the Kingdom established, knowledge that Satan’s day is over, with Armageddon and eternal blessings on the way. Can Jehovah’s people prove that such knowledge has served them as a defense? The proof is abundant. Through the crucible of flaming-hot persecution they have passed with integrity unconsumed. In the face of mounting hardships, they have continued to press forward zealously in Kingdompreaching work. Further, while hundreds of millions sink deep into the mire of despair, losing all hope, Jehovah’s people live free of fear and anxiety, a joyful and contented people, glad to be alive in this day. And all this because of right knowledge, the foundation for their unshakable faith, by reason of which the Lord has been pleased to cast up for them a strong defense against the enemy’s attacks.

Defense against demonic assaults in this critical postwar era is assured, but only to those who continue to absorb protective knowledge. A portion of one’s time each day, therefore, should be devoted to study of the Bible and the Society's publications: Watchtower, bound books, booklets, and others.

Casting Up a Strong Defense In June

Fittingly, the testimony period for June is termed “Defense Through Knowledge”. “For wisdom is a defence, money is a defence, and the advantage of the knowledge of wisdom is, she reviveth her possessors.” (Eccl. 7:12, Younff) During June Kingdom publishers will not merely take in right knowledge through study, but will unselfishly give it out to others, aiding them to find defense against the destruction ahead. This is the wise course that merits divine approval. In June the offer of any three bound books from The Harp of Goel to Reliffion will continue. What a splendid way to impart true knowledge! Three 384-page bound books on a contribution of only 50c. Surely no company in America should have difficulty in clearing off its shelves by the 30th of June every one of these older books, getting them into the hands of the public, where they belong. Servants and all publishers will work enthusiastically to this end.

Simultaneously the special campaign with “Be Glad, Ye Nations” will enter its third month. The final days of June should climax the greatest single-booklet campaign to date. During June every effort possible should be made, every avenue of distribution should be employed, toward the goal of getting at least. 10.000,000 copies of “Be Glad, Ye Nations” distributed before

the end of the year. With longer days, evening booklet service can be instituted in all companies. Evenings now open may be utilized, in addition to an hour or so preceding company book studies or individual back-calls and book studies, in territory adjacent thereto. In this work only a minute or two need be spent at each door, giving a brief witness and placing a booklet. If interest is located, arrange for a return visit.


Uptrend In Public Meetings Anticipated

Publishers nation-wide appear to be lending greater support to public meetings. This is as it should be, and June, the fourth month of public meetings with the new talks, ought to be marked by an even more intensive effort to engage in all features of this educational work. In addition to indoor halls, parks, pavilions, or any other suitable outdoor arrangement, can be used during summer months. Companies may wish to inaugurate, where practical, a series of weekdayevening meetings; this has proved quite satisfactory in some places. Reports indicate that the new handbills, giving a brief but powerful witness through the advertising on the back, are serving an excellent purpose. Distribute them widely; it is believed that they will do much toward stirring reasonable minds to earnestly seek ‘defense through knowledge’. Hundred-percent publisher support of each public meeting in June, that might well be the aim of all companies. Lend a helping hand to the irregular; encourage newly interested to join in; do everything possible to make June a banner public-meeting month.

While covering public meeting territory with literature and handbills the publisher should have as his objective starting at least one study in the area by the end of the series. Cleveland is in an ideal setting for this postwar assembly of all nations. The stadium where the convention will be held is sheltered. has normal provisions for 8.3,900 people, and overflow space inside for 17.000 more on the playing field. In addition, thousands of others can be accommodated in adjacent sections. The cool Lake Erie breezes blow right into Cleveland, and particularly on the stadium which we will use: it is situated right on the lakeside. All brethren will be adequately provided for in one spot, and this will work to good advantage, both in edifying assemblies and in reunion of fellowship after five years of multicityassemblies. due to wartime restrictions and limitations.

Considerable work will, of course, be required to ready the place for the convention. However, the Society requests that only those brethren who receive official notice to proceed to Cleveland ahead of time do so. Others will remain in their respective assignments until the time of the convention, caring for the good-will interest and preparing for the local brethren's attending at Cleveland.
