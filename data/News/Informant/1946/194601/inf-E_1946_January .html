Immovability for Right Worship


January Begins Four-Month “Watchtower” Campaign

What a blessed privilege it is to be alive in this day, and on Jehovah’s side of the world-rocking issue pressing for immediate settlement, the issue of right worship I Can the Almighty God draw to himself without compulsion the unbreakable worship of humans? No ! challenged the Devil, He cannot. But time would tell; and time has told. Down through sixty centuries, repeatedly, has come the answer in favor of Jehovah. There were Abel, Noah, Daniel, and many others of a great cloud of witnesses who stood immovable for the right worship, no matter what the cost. There was Christ Jesus, who under faith-testing temptation settled the issue forcefully when he told Satan, “Thou shalt worship the Lord thy God, and him only shalt thou serve.” Is it different in this day? No; if anything, today the issue is more definitely settled, for today there live on earth not 100, not 1,000, nor even 10,000, but more than 100,000 immovable worshipers, so that more than 100,000 times daily Satan is proved the liar that he is!

Right worship Is not easy. It is not inseparable from work, hard, persistent work for every servant. It means, further, the loss of worldly “friends” and pleasures, the casting aside of many wants and luxuries, as well as self-pride. It means facing persecution, rebuffs at the doors, mobs, jail sentences, ridicule, discouragement at times, and even death. Yes, It means a constant fight against the demons; for the Devil will not cease trying to blot out the true worship, until he himself is obliterated. But, though faced with all this, what worshiper of the Most High wants to quit his ministry and seek after other gods? Where among religious worshipers can you find the peace of mind, the unquenchable joy, and the full satisfaction and freedom that pervades the ranks of Jehovah’s people today? Worship another than Jehovah? Never, says the consecrated servant, as he looks ahead to the New World. But not in our frail selves can we continue firm for true worship; solely by complete reliance upon the invincible might of Jehovah, who backs ns up, will we stand. As the psalmist puts it, “I have set the Lord always before me ; because he is at my right hand, I shall not be moved.”—Psalm 16: 8.

Remaining intrenched in our blessed position requires right knowledge. This the Lord provides through The Watchtower. What would be your reaction if The Watchtower suddenly stopped coming? Would you miss it? The answer is obvious. A deep craving would be set up in our hearts and minds for the vital Kingdom truths which fill its pages. To us The Watchtoucr is indispensable. It is not the will of God, however, that its message be limited to his present worshipers. To the contrary, Jehovah’s mandate is that ‘this gospel be preached in all the world’, that others may learn of the true worship.

How Does Your Kingdom Hall Impress Good-Will Attendants?

Do people of good-will comment favorably on the cleanliness and orderliness of your Kingdom Hall? Do they feel so much at home that they want to return? If not, then something should he done about it The appearance of your Kingdom Hall reflects either favorably or unfavorably upon Jehovah’s witnesses and The Theocratic Government, because it is natural for people to judge by outward appearances.

The Kingdom Hall is to be used to the Lord’s honor and praise and । to bring life-giving knowledge of Jehovah’s purposes to people of goodwill who are served by it It should at all times be clean, tidy, well lighted and ventilated, properly heated and orderly, having no unauthorized signs or slogans displayed in the hall. Every company should, however, if they have not already done so, arrange to have the new yeartext neatly and properly displayed in the Kingdom Hall. Window displays should be changed frequently and the windows kept clean, so as not to resemble some dingy religious mission on a side street. Faded, soiled literature or unsightly signs should not be used. Cleanliness and neatness, which attract favorably the attention of every passer-by, should mark every window display. Better to have no display than a poor one.

Many brethren are meticulous about the appearance of their own homes; but how much more concerned should we be about the appearance of our Kingdom Halls, which are a provision for giving life-education and happiness to all those served by it! The company servant should arrange to schedule willing volunteers to systematically care for keeping the ball clean, neat, orderly, attractive and inviting. They will then know it is part of their responsibility to Jehovah. “I have given him for a ... commander to the

people.”—Isa. 55:4

During the month of February, we have a testimony period of great importance: the “Commander's” Testimony Period. Not only is this of great importance to the Lord’s people, but it is of vital concern to every creature on the face of the earth. The demand for, the desire for, and the need for a Commander was never so great as it is now. All realize the need for such leadership. Numerous “commanders" have been selected, but none of them have been entirely satisfactory, even to those who selected them. And, on the whole, they have left the people in general thoroughly disappointed, discouraged and disillusioned.

The motive that prompts the desire for such leadership varies according to the heart conditions of the people. The ultraseifish politicians, financiers and ruling clergy want a “commander” of their own choice, who will recognize and accept them and keep them in power. The deluded and blinded peoples of the world, who blame all their troubles on demonized human leaders of the past and present, want a “commander" who will wreak vengeance on their enemies, and exalt themselves to a position where they may enjoy the fruits of their so-called “victory”. The meek people of the earth, who are sick of war and its attendant bloodshed, are feeling after and hoping for a righteous condition. They want a “commander” who will bring about these conditions for themselves, their loved ones, and all mankind. These are really ‘feeling after God, if haply they may find him’.

The People with Vision and Responsibility

The only people on earth who can really evaluate the situation that exists today and who discern the true reasons for the failure of all human commanders and the only effective remedy for man’s present condition are the witnesses of the Most High God. They know who the real “Commander” is, and why the selfish desires of the wicked will be shattered, and how the longings of the meek and teachable will be realized. This knowledge is a gift of God, and not of themselves. They are, therefore, responsible to the Lord God to use it according to his direction. In fact, they are made of the Lord signposts to direct the people in the proper course. This knowledge and responsibility separates them from all other peoples of the earth. They are a people for a purpose; which purpose they must fulfill.

The delusions of the adversary, minimizing the true “Commander” and magnifying all other commanders, are snares and pitfalls set out to entrap the people and keep them from recognizing and submitting themselves to the Lord's appointed Commander. The Lord knows this, and, centuries ago, made the necessary arrangement to counteract the schemes of the Devil. Through His Word lie outlined the proper course of action to be taken by his people in order to fulfill their part in his grand arrangement. All of His people, whether anointed or Jona-dabs, are. because of the knowledge they have, obligated to gather out these obstructions developed by the Devil and intended to thwart the purposes of Jehovah. Go After the Isolated and Scattered

There is considerable territory in every company assignment and in many pioneer assignments that has not been reached during the Watchtower campaigns in recent years. A definite effort should be made to give this territory a thorough coverage this year. Plan now to reach these people during February, and continue working until all of them are reached. These people have been neglected not only in the Watchtower campaigns, but also in the public meeting campaign and other features of the Kingdom service. See that they are thoroughly served this winter through the Watch tower Campaign, public meetings, back-calls, and book studies. Follow through in all features of the work. Let each one take this assignment of ‘gathering out the stones’ very seriously. It is from the Lord, and all must answer to the Lord for their faithfulness in discharging their obligations. Plan now to care for all the good-will interest in the territory, that they may know and accept Jehovah’s “Commander to the peoples”.

Use Your Theocratic Library

A Theocratic library plays an important role in every Theocratic school. It contains much or all of our Kingdom instruction and educational material and can be a source of real Theocratic aid to every Kingdom publisher. There is no good reason why every company cannot have a good, usable, practical library for the use of all the Kingdom publishers and persons of good-will associated with the company. The company servant and school servant should arrange for an up-to-date, neat, clean, practical library, and show all of the publishers how to get the most good out of it.

The Theocratic library should contain all of the Society’s publications, or as many as are available, including books, booklets, Bibles, magazines, etc. It should have as many translations of the Bible as are obtainable, for comparative study. Exhaustive concordances, Bible dictionaries, encyclopedias, and other reference books are also valuable assets to the library. You may also include books by recognized authorities for the purpose of refutation in talks. Do not include books which delve into religious interpretations, higher criticisms, or religious commentaries, of which ‘books there is no end; and to study them ' is a weariness of the flesh’.—Eccl. 12:12.

This Theocratic library may be used by all publishers in gathering material for back-calls and book studies, or for Improving their door-to-door presentations; in further research when studying The Watchtower and books, or to gather more information on some Scriptural point that is not clearly understood. Public speakers and student speakers will find it a priceless gem for making up and arranging talks. Slaterial can be gathered from this source for presentation In service meetings. Brethren, there is no end to the value of the Theocratic library. Use this instrument constantly and profitably in the Lord’s service.

Support the Company Book Study

In the great work of ‘discipling all nations’ every feature of the work is important. ‘Because the foot is not the hand, is it therefore not of the body?' the apostle asked. And so today, ‘because the company book study is not the service meeting, is it therefore not a part of the “discipling” work?’ Every feature of the work is important and must be properly taken care of if we are to carry out our commission in a mannerpleasing to Jehovah and thus maintain our integrity.

In many companies the organized book studies are very weak. Conductors fail to see their obligation and privilege to properly prepare, and then fail to so conduct the book study as to make the meeting interesting and truly helpful; company publishers fail to support it by attendance, by comments, and by bringing their new interest. Some even excuse their failure to attend by stating that they would if they could count time; which reminds one of the priests and Levites who would not open and close the doors of the temple for nought.—Mal. 1: 10.

Of course, if one has a back-call book study on the night of the company book study, and it is the best evening for that study, one's back-call study should be given preference. But even here, if possible, it would be better to bring your goodwill interest to the company book study than to study with them alone, as by making this effort they are already making progress toward associating themselves with the Lord's organization.

A number of companies are inquiring as to what to study next, since they are completing their study of ‘'The Kingdom Is at Hand". This would depend upon the circumstances : how new the interest is, what book was previously studied, etc. Without doubt, much benefit would be gained by going through "The Kingdom Is at Hand" again. Or a study may be started in "The Truth Shall Make You Free” or The Yew World, either of which would be very profitable.

Let all get behind the company book studies and support them by proper preparation, regular attendance, ready comments, and by bring' ing new interest to them. Remember, “He that is faithful in that which is least is faithful also in much” applies also to the company book studies.
