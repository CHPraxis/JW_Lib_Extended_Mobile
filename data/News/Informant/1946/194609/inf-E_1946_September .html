Service Echoes from the Glad Nations Assembly

Though the long-awaited and eagerly anticipated Glad Nations Assembly has come and gone, the spirit lingers on with thousands of Theocracy publishers, now back at their posts serving more energetically and enthusiastically than ever before. To be glad in the face of the conspiracy rising, and to be fearless against it, was the theme; and the secret of it all was the getting into and staying in active service of Jehovah God. In fact, the spirit of service permeated the entire eight days.

The model Watchtower study on Sunday, August 4, well demonstrated the correct procedure in this regard. The evening witnessed the warm address by the chairman and the excellent talk on “The Harvest, the End of the World". The lecture on Monday by the Society's legal counsel on “Proper Procedure in Court” showed the reason why court cases were necessary, how to prepare for them, and the need for thorough preparation, not to win, but to give a witness, and leave the victory to the Lord. Then, on Monday came the Theocratic ministry school, something even the smallest companies should have in operation. A lively review, helpful instruction talk, well-prepared student talks, and constructive counsel by a Gilead instructor for the students, was followed by a multi-demonstration on how to refute arguments from door to door.

On Tuesday Brother Knorr sounded the keynote—“Fearless Against the World Conspiracy.” Here the clear contrast was made between the United Nations and the Glad Nations. Publishers saw how the second try for world peace and second girding of itself was Scripturally doomed to failure, and how now His witnesses must stand firm for the Theocratic Government of Immanuel, for “God is with us".

Came the release of Awake! How the truth proclaimed by Theocratic publishers would break the sheer synthetic new-order gown of the old world, and how Awake! (replacing Consolation) would be used to spread the truth to the people was clearly set out.

The service meeting was well handled. It was realistic in every detail, even to the late-comers who disturbingly tiptoed into the hall!

On Thursday Brother Knorr delivered “Equipped for Every Good Work"; and what a thrill when the new field instrument by that name was released! Following this, next day, “All Nations” Day, representatives of thirty nations told how the work had increased and expanded during the past few war years. This information followed Brother Knorr’s talk on “The Problems of Reconstruction and Expansion” the previous day, wherein it was outlined how the true worship is on the increase, the plans for the future, the new factory and Bethel in the United States, and how publishers world-wide had come up from 71,000 to 169,000 since 1939.

Servant to the Brethren Work Adjusted


Circuit Arrangement to Aid Reconstruction and Expansion

The clock fairly spun around on that Thursday evening of the Glad Nations Assembly as Brother Knorr presented a multitude of facts relating to the problems of reconstruction and expansion. One feature of reconstruction mentioned affecting every publisher in the world is the enlarged and improved servant to the brethren work.

In what manner will this accomplish a reconstruction and expansion? First, the increased time spent with the companies will mean a great deal. True, some companies did have a week or maybe a two-week visit by the servant to the brethren, but the majority were served by two-or three-day visits. How these smaller companies have wanted a few more days during which they could work with the servant to the brethren in the field I Now all companies will be served a full week. Additionally, the provision of more time will mean more actual field instruction for the individual publisher.

Meets Vital Need of Expansion Program The good work of Kingdom-preaching rapidly expands. To keep step witli its forward movement the publisher must equip himself with greater Scriptural knowledge. He should learn the origin and history of the Bible, be familiar with the contents of its books, know something of original Bible languages and manuscripts, and acquaint himself with some information on the Apocrypha and Talmud. Equipped thus, the minister will be able to slash away religion’s lies, refute arguments, prove God and his Word to be true, and furnish sound instruction to right-hearted Catholics, Protestants, Jews and others in his territory.

Jehovah, aware of the needs of his people, has graciously provided the Bible handbook “Equipped for Every Good Work". Publishers should begin studying it immediately. Scan the table of contents; note the wealth of material treated. Familiarize yourself with its various features, including the maps, tables and the section entitled “Scriptural Summary”.

A Bible Handbook for Use in the Field

“Equipped for Every Good Work” is designed as a practical field-service help; it is a Bible handbook for constant reference use in door-to-door work, on back-calls and book studies. In a moment, an array of scriptures (pages 359-379) is at hand on topics such as “Hell”, “Soul,” “Dead,” “Church,” “Earth in God's Purpose,” etc., for use at the door, or at a back-call. Should questions arise relating to the authenticity of the Bible, or one of its books, reference to the Contents and the Index will enable you to locate quickly enlightening, factual data. A Jew will question Christ’s being the Messiah. Does he believe the Hebrew Scriptures? Turn to the table on page 21 and prove that the man Jesus fufilled to the letter everything the Hebrew prophets foretold regarding the Messiah. Encourage those of good-will to obtain and read “Equipped for Every Good Work"; it will quicken their interest and firmly ground them in the truth. Companies may arrange at service meetings to show by demonstrations and talks how this Bible handbook may be used for reference in the field.

In this circuit arrangement, with the servant to the brethren visiting each company approximately every twenty weeks he will be able to get acquainted with the brethren and their various problems in the preaching work. After his first time of serving all companies in the circuit he will be able to go into a company and from his previous acquaintance get right to the heart of their problems and be of real assistance.

Circuit Assemblies

That is not all. Beginning January 1, 1947, assemblies of all companies in the circuit will be arranged so that many cities can benefit by being thoroughly covered in field activity. These assemblies will carry forward the same spirit of peace and oneness that prevailed at the Glad Nations Assembly.

The expansion work is moving ahead. Study the instructions to come forth on this work shortly. Support this arrangement. Watch the results in the field and rejoice in whatever part you have as you see the Lord bring in the increase.

A diligent, conscientious study of “Equipped for Every Good Work" will deepen the publisher’s appreciation of the Creator's perfect Book, the Bible. It will make you realize more keenly the glorious privilege you have in knowing and serving the God of Truth. It will move you to do with all your ‘heart, soul and strength’ the good work of gospelpreaching. Dig into its pages at every opportunity: discuss it at home and in the field. Through its instruction parents will find themselves better equipped to teach their children in this “time of the end”, and all publishers will find themselves speaking forth God's irrefutable Word with greater force and conviction.

The Kingdom work, every feature of it, is expanding. Equip yourselves, therefore, to share fully in the ingathering of the “great multitude” in your territory and all the world. Equip yourselves by studying and using “Equipped for Every Good Work" on every occasion, in this pre-Armageddon period.

When ordering the book “Equipped for Every Good Work" please keep in mind this book is a cash item and not chargeable to an account. A remittance of 50c for each book ordered must accompany the order.
