Jehovah Blesses

World-wide Broadcast

By far the most blessed and enthusiastic convention of the Lord’s people to date has come and gone in the General Convention emanating from New York, June 23-25. The high points were so numerous that it is impossible for us to cover all of them in the Informant.

Brother Rutherford’s public address of Sunday afternoon was the high point. This speech was delivered under most tense and dramatic circumstances and will not be forgotten by those who were privileged to witness the event and hear the talk.

Madison Square Garden Packed

Visualize, if you can, an immense auditorium such as Madison Square Garden with close to 20,000 people packed into it, and a powerful witness for Jehovah and against the Devil being delivered. Then some three hundred fanatical Catholics in one section of the upper balcony tried to break up the meeting. Another small group in another section tried to do likewise. However, the Lord directed the handling of this situation so that not only were their efforts unsuccessful, but the Lord, maneuvering the enemy’s forces as well as his own, caused the entire setup to work to the glory of His name and for the blessing of his people and the confusion of the enemy. The enthusiastic applause with which the mighty audience received each point of Brother Rutherford's speech struck terror to the hearts of the hoodlum disturbers. The results of the upheaval were—

  • 1. To cause Brother Rutherford to determine by God’s grace to drive the message he had to deliver into the microphone at any cost. This he did with a force and authority never equaled in the past.

  • 2. The attack established a background for everything Brother Rutherford said, and by their own words and actions they stand condemned of doing the very things that the Lord reveals they have been doing and which Brother Rutherford was telling the audience about at that particular time.

  • 3. Many honest Catholics in the Madison Square Garden, and those attending the other assemblies, and those listening to the radio, have indicated that they are heartily disgusted with and ashamed of the disgraceful tactics of Catholic Action. Evidently this is also percolating through to the Hierarchy; for they are breaking their necks now to discountenance what happened, claiming that it was radical Coughlinites that were responsible and that these are only a minor faction of Catholicism and do not properly represent their organization.

However, they waited too long. The facts got out and exposed their true position before they could be stopped, and honest people are getting their eyes opened. Not only that, but the whole disgraceful action on their part has, under the Lord’s direction, been recorded and will encircle the earth again when this is reproduced in the homes of the people by the phonographs of tens of thousands of Jehovah’s witnesses. This honor have all his saints and also their companions.

"Victory”

Another high point was Brother Rutherford’s speech on Saturday afternoon, entitled “Victory”, clearly proving that we have come to the time when the Lord’s people are to take aggressive action; that the people of God are to go forward like locusts; not killing the people physically, but destroying the provender which the religionists have dished up for the people.

"Salvation”

Another high point was the release of the new book Salvation, which is a very important step in the progression of the Lord’s arrangement, particularly in providing for the great multitude.

All through the convention the brethren had before them on the program “Salvation belongeth unto Jehovah”; so when Brother Rutherford introduced and released the book, the significance of the quotation came to all. Surely “Salvation belongeth unto Jehovah”, the knowledge of which he has committed to his people that they may publish It abroad to the glory of his name and to the blessing of the people of good will.

Saltation was simultaneously released in all convention cities at the same time, In Australia, the British Isles, Canada, Hawaii and the United States, each point receiving its respective consignment on time. The brethren eagerly devoured its pages between sessions —reluctant to waste one precious second.

World-wide Broadcast

Another remarkable indication of the Lord’s supervision of the entire convention was the unanimous reports from all parts of the earth that the reception was excellent.

Both lectures circled this planet. Reports have come from every part of the world indicating unusual reception. One engineer handling the overseas service said, “You people are always lucky.” We know that Jehovah supervises these arrangements to His praise.

The key city for this convention was New York. There the convention opened in Manhattan Center. This spacious auditorium was well filled by Friday evening. By Saturday it was crowded out, and some 2,500 to 3,000 could not get in to hear Brother Rutherford at all on Saturday afternoon. Saturday night the convention moved into Madison Square Garden without confusion or commotion. Sunday morning the brethren assembled there for the final day’s services.

Information marches were another outstanding feature of the convention. Between 3,000 and 4,000 brethren participated in these marches, giving New York City the greatest witness it has ever had, and one which it will not soon forget. There was much enthusiasm and joy manifested by the brethren who participated in this most effective means of advertising.

The enthusiastic and thrilling Declaration meetings indicated the Lord’s blessings were many upon his people. Space will not permit us to go more into detail here. Read it all in The Messenger.

Remember, “Salvation belongeth unto Jehovah.” He has entrusted us with the job of taking the facts concerning it in print to the people. Let everybody be ready for the “Divine Mandate” Testimony Period, commencing August 1.
