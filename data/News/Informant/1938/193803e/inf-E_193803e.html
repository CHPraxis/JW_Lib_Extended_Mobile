Jehovah Gives World-wide Warning to “Christendom”

“Enemies,” “Cure” and “Consolation”, the Message

Call to Action! All to ‘Rise Against This Siege to Continue Three Months


Eleven Million Pieces of Literature to Be Distributed

Some months ago Brother Rutherford outlined a campaign which calls for the greatest united effort ever put forth by the Lord’s anointed and their companions. This campaign begins April 9 with the “Companions” testimony period and will continue to the end of June. This is a period of nearly three months, and it Includes two testimony periods, “Companions” and “Boldness”.

The personal letter by Brother Rutherford to the witnesses of Jehovah throughout the earth which you receive with this Informant sets out the importance of this witness and shows that a warning from the L o r d’s Word must be given to “Christendom”. Jehovah’s witnesses and their companions are charged with the responsibility of giving this warning. So that this warning may be clear and understandable to the people, the literature that will be offered will be Enemies and a new 32-page booklet, Cure, which is full of fire and contains a pointed message that every person in the world should have. The first printing of this booklet in English alone will be ten million copies. The foreign languages will greatly add to this total. With Jehovah’s blessing and favor all of these Cure booklets and one million Enemies (or Riches) will be distributed in the three-month campaign. All the Cure booklets must be distributed during these three months because of the special offer for Consolation made on the back of the booklet, which expires June 30.

Study the Cure booklet carefully. Know its contents and be acquainted with the special offer. You will then most certainly be one of the “companions” who will ‘rise against her in battle’.

A World-wide Quota

For this three-month period, April, May and June of 1938, a world-wide quota is being set for the distribution of the Kingdom message. It is confidently expected that the greatest number of publishers ever engaging in the field at one time will share in this mighty witness. In the United States alone we expect that there will be upward of 30,000 different persons ‘putting forth their best endeavors to carry this testimony to those who have a hearing ear and to give warning to others’. So that this warning and testimony may be given the world-wide quota Is set at

10,000,000 Booklets 1,000,000 Bound Books 100,000 New Subscriptions

This will require a very extraordinary effort on the part of all those fighting for God’s kingdom. It means more than double anything ever done heretofore in the same length of time. For months past every branch of the Society in the world has made great preparation in announcing this campaign and seeing to it that It will be carried forward to the honor of Jehovah’s name. Think of it 1 10,000,000 of the Cure booklet, a million of the book Enemies (or Riches), and 100,000 new subscriptions for Consolation!

The Special Offer

As stated on the back of the Cure booklet, beginning with April 9 the brethren of the Lord and their companions will offer Cure, Enemies (or Riches), and one year's subscription for Consolation, on contribution of the amount usually received for Consolation alone, which is a dollar in the United States and $1.25 in other countries. This is the finest offer that has ever been made to the peoples of the world, and surely never before has such a powerful message of warning been carried to them.

Three-Month Siege

It is the privilege of the publishers of the Kingdom in the United States to place seven million of the ten million Cure booklets printed in the English language. To place these seven million of the Cure booklet is a tremendous but possible task. To do this it will be necessary to reach at least fourteen million homes. That will mean that each company publisher in the United States must call on at least 500 homes In these three months and leave the Cure booklet in 200 of them. The Individual quota for each company publisher is 200 booklets, 20 bound books, and three subscriptions for Consolation, in this three-month period. This tremendous task of distributing seven million of the Cure booklet and hundreds of thousands of the book Enemies (or Riches), and obtaining thousands of subscriptions for Consolation can be accomplished if each individual takes upon himself the responsibility of doing his share of the work. The auxiliaries and the pioneers have a higher quota than that of the company publishers. Each one of the auxiliaries during this entire period will, by the Lord’s grace, place 500 booklets and 75 bound books and obtain six Consolation subscriptions. The full-time publishers—the pioneers and the special pioneers—have the privilege of making a quota of 1,000 booklets, 150 bound books, and 15 Consolation subscriptions. If everyone in the United States attains this personal quota for three months, the quota for the United States will be attained and the world quota will be met! What an opportunity I What a witness this will be to Jehovah’s name!
