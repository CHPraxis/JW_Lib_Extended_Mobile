"Cure” Booklet

For this campaign the Lord through Brother Rutherford has provided a special 32-page booklet, entitled CURE. It is full of fire and will be a most effective weapon of war to use in exposing the enemy. Its front cover is brilliant red, and its appearance and the questions asked will create a desire on the part of everyone who sees it to read it. The questions “Do we need more religion?” and "Is religion the cure for nations and individuals?” will certainly arouse the interest of any thinking person. The Lord's people know the answers, but it is necessary now for millions of people throughout the world also to know these answers. The people are perplexed and in distress, and Cure contains just the message they need. This booklet is to be included in a special offer to be made during the campaign of three months, and this special offer is set out in the one ad which appears in the booklet.

The manuscript for Cure reached the Brooklyn factory on December 14. Typesetting began immediately, platemaking was rushed, and by January 1 tin' first press began to turn out this new booklet. During the first two weeks of January the presses turned out over 100,000 daily, and then the production was stepped up to 200.000 daily. This will continue until 10,000.000 copies have been printed and shipped out. The first 530.000 printed will go to Australia and New Zealand. These are on the way and should be there by March 1, 1938. Then will follow shipments to South Africa of 80,000, West Africa 30.000, India 20,000, Philippine Islands 15,000. Hawaii 4,000, England 1,500,000, Jamaica 20,000, and Canada 700,000. For the United States alone more than 7,000,000 copies will be set aside. Because of the special offer made in the advertisement, which offer expires June 30, all these copies must be distributed by that time. Can we do it? We believe we can. by the Lord's grace, if everyone will do his part. Everyone at the Brooklyn factory is working at top speed so that the publishers can have this literature by the beginning of the campaign. From then on it will be up to the publishers to carry out their part in the campaign and get these booklets out.

English and Foreign Records
May Now Be Ordered Separately if Desired

Heretofore the policy of the Society has been not to ship out records except in complete sets where the lectures are in series, namely, “Exposed,” “Religion and Christianity,” “Safety,” and “Worshiping God”. However, to aid the brethren in getting these records, this policy is being changed, and anyone who desires to order one or more records of any series may do so. Many of the brethren probably feel that they cannot afford to get the complete sets at one time, but would like to get a few each month until they have complete sets. Also, many publishers would like to have one or two records of a series to use in regular witnessing. This will help to interest the people to hear the entire series of lectures, and back calls can be arranged for some convenient time. As most companies have one or more complete sets of records, the publishers can borrow these sets from the company stock to take out on such back calls.

The above applies to foreign records also. Any company or individual who cannot get an entire series at once may get any part of the series desired. We now have the lectures “Exposed” and “Religion and Christianity” in English, Arabic, Italian, Polish, Spanish, and Ukrainian. In each of these languages the even numbers are coupled together and the odd numbers together; for instance, P-61-A with P-63-C, P-62-B with P-64-C, etc. When ordering, please be definite about which records you desire.
