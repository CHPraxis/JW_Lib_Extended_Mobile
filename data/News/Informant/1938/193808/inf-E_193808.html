United States Receives Its Greatest Witness Three-Month Campaign Surpasses Every Other


New Peak in Publishers Reached in June

World-wide Convention Another Advance Movement

The report for June, the final month of the three-month campaign with the Cure booklet, Enemies and Consolation, surpasses anything previously accomplished in the United States and the outlying countries reporting to the Brooklyn office. In the United States alone there were 31,110 publishers in the field—a new peak—showing the continued increase of the multitude.

These brethren placed 168,579 bound books and 3,408,636 booklets. In June every publisher got down to business and put this campaign over the top. No wonder we are now hearing loud squawks from the “old lady” ! Including the 241 publishers in outlying possessions we find that the literature placed under the Brooklyn report makes a grand total of 3,597,730, the greatest witness given in any one month.

And now for the three months’ work. It Is astounding! According to the reports there were 31,461 different publishers in the field during April. May anil June. Bound books placed, 480,453; booklets, 6,540.243. What joy this brings us when we see the strenuous witness that was
