Shall We Have

35,000 Publishers???

Jehovah has revealed to his ‘faithful and true’ witnesses, in the publications Vindication 1, 2, and 3, his glorious universal organization; also the wicked and cruel organization of Satan, and his (Jehovah’s) purpose soon to utterly annihilate Satan and all his religious cohorts, that all people who love righteousness may know that Jehovah is the only true and almighty God. Therein is disclosed also the grave responsibility of the anointed and their companions of now widely warning the people of this impending destruction, and the urgent necessity that the people of good will ride in the chariot of the Greater Jehu.

By the most gracious provision of the Lord, we are privileged to offer these three books on a contribution of only 35c, thus bringing this message well within the reach of all people who love righteousness. Also, during December, if you have no Vindication, you may offer Preparation and Warning on a contribution of 25c, dropping to Womwiy alone on a 5c contribution if they do not take books.

Necessarily now the obligation is laid upon all the anointed and their companions to bring this unusual offer to the attention of as many people as possible throughout the 31 days of December. Each company should set as Its goal the distribution of all the Vindication books in stock and as many Preparation and Warning as possible. Make it at least one set of Vindication per publisher.

Offer Most Seasonable

This wonderful offer comes at the most appropriate time of the year. It is during December that the people are “ ‘Christmas’-shopping-con-scious,” so called—they are on the lookout for the best bargains that they can get as presents for their friends and for themselves. This false sense of ‘good will toward men’, which has been foisted upon them by the religionists, can be used to their advantage by bringing to their notice an offer that they cannot duplicate elsewhere. Vindication, Books 1, 2, and 3, on a contribution of 35c cannot be beat!

Use Own Stock

Most of the companies have a plentiful supply of Vindication, Preparation, and Warning; so there will be little need of companies’ ordering for this campaign, except Vindication to make complete sets. The stockkeeper should arrange to have an exact count of this literature on hand as of Sunday, November 13, and post this information so that ail publishers may know what


is to be put out. This information is to be kept up from week to week until the campaign is over. The company servant should see to it, by the time of the last service meeting of November, that all the publishers have on hand at least one or two sets of Vindication, some Preparation, and a good supply of the Warning booklet. All should be fully armed to start this campaign December 1 and place all of them by December 31.

Pioneers and Auxiliaries

Pioneers and auxiliaries located in company territory should arrange to draw on the company stock of Vindication, Preparation, and Warning, only. The company servant is hereby authorized (during this campaign only) to transfer to pioneers and auxiliaries as much of this stock as they can use, on credit, on their word that their account is in good condition. At the end of December, that proper adjustment may be made on the different accounts, the company servant is to send in a statement signed by himself and the pioneer or auxiliary showing the literature so transferred. This one notice, covering literature turned over to each individual, will be all that will be necessary to send in to this office. It only causes confusion if both parties notify this office of the transfer.

Isolated Pioneers and Auxiliaries

Pioneers and auxiliaries not located in company territory, as well as those companies that have but a limited supply of this campaign literature, should send in their orders at once to Brooklyn for whatever Vindication, Preparation and Warning are required. We will promptly arrange to fill your order from our own, or, if possible, from some nearby company stock. Brethren whose


accounts are in poor condition should accompany their order with remittance to cover.

Territory to Be Worked

During December the brethren should return to their urban territory, to the large towns and cities. The territory servant should immediately line up and assign such territory as can be best witnessed to in cold and inclement weather. The purpose should be to aid the publishers in calling on as many people as possible during the winter months.

Additionally, the back call servant should arrange for the publishers to call on all the interested ones on his list with this special Vindication offer, not only that they can take advantage of this opportunity for themselves, but that they can also be encouraged to get a set for their friends.

Carry Your Phonographs

Introduce the phonograph as quickly as you can. Run records "Relief” and “Resolution”, P-151, P-152. Briefly present the Vindication offer, pointing out the need of studying them at this crucial time. If they cannot take these books, endeavor to leave Warning at least. Those brethren who because of infirmity or for other reason cannot carry along sets of Vindication should stress Preparation and Warning on a contribution of 25c or Warning alone on a contribution of 5c.

Where there is interest manifested, arrange for a back call for the “Warning” and “Violence” series. Use every means at your command to aid the interested people in recognizing that Jehovah is supreme and that their safety lies in aligning themselves under his organization. The sound servant should see that all phonographs are in working condition and in use and that the brethren have record P-151, P-152.


December Report

On the December report card, in the column “Books” Is to be shown the total number of Vindication and Preparation (as well as any other books) that have been placed during that month. In addition, the companies and auxiliaries are to show at the bottom of their December report card the number of complete sets of Vindication placed, by stat-


ing there, “Placed


sets of


Vindication,” and giving the number. All companies and auxiliaries giving this information will receive a credit on their account of 25c and 13c a set respectively. If this information is not on the December report, it will be assumed that the company or auxiliary so reporting is desirous of helping the Society to bear the burden of this special reduction. As the cost to pioneers is already very low, no credit will be given on sets placed at this special rate.

Warriors All

This “Faithful and True” period should see all the soldiers of the Kingdom wielding the sword of the spirit to the honor and praise of Jehovah and his King, Christ Jesus, and to the continued perplexity and pain of the religionists and their Godless allies. This period should start us well on our way toward our goal of 35,000 publishers as well as clear the decks for the great battle to be fought during the world-wide campaign of January-April.

Continue to use the new record, “Relief,” “Resolution,” P-151, P-152, in the regular house-to-house witness, which will materially aid you in arranging for studies in the homes of the interested ones. At these back calls, use the “Warning” and “Violence” series of records.
