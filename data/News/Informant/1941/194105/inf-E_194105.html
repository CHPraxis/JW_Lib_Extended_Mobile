“Watchtower” Campaign Extended One Month 4-Month Campaign, Ending with May, Should Show Results


Go over Your Quota on Books, Booklets and Subscriptions 11

There Is no doubt that the Lord is directing His organization in the earth. Manifestly the extension of the Watchtower Campaign to include the month of May is a merciful provision of the Lord to permit us to make up our quota for the Watchtower Campaign. The quota set for February, March and April was 100,000 new subscriptions, 1.000,000 bound books, and 6,750.000 booklets. This was a reasonable quota for the more than 50,000 company publishers and pioneers now reporting regularly in the United States. However, according to the rate at which new Watchtower subscriptions are being received at Brooklyn now, by the end of April we shall have obtained just a little better than 50,000, which is only half the quota for the threemonth campaign. Up to March 31 there were 37,521 new subscriptions sent in to the Brooklyn office. It appears, too, that we shall not attain the quota of books and booklets. Why?

50,000 Subscriptions to Go!

February, March and April have slipped by and the quota is only half made. This means there is much work to be done during May. There are 50.000 publishers regularly reporting in the United States. If each publisher sees to it that he obtains at least one subscription during May (regardless of how many he has previously obtained), we shall gain our objective, 100,000 new Watchtower subscriptions for the campaign. The very fact that the Lord has arranged through Uis organization for the extension of the campaign indicates that it is His will that the quota be made. Therefore let each publisher of The Theocracy soberly determine to make it his business to get at least one subscription before May 31.

Books and booklets also are low, but these will show a corresponding increase with the subscriptions. If the new subscription quota is reached, undoubtedly the rest of the quota will be reached. Each company publisher, therefore, should put forth every effort during May to place five bound books and at least thirty booklets, in addition to getting subscriptions ; pioneers, 40 books and 250 booklets. Elsewhere in this Informant suggestion is made as to how to increase the booklet placements. and other features of the work. More time in the field is required. If each individual assumes his responsibility and takes care of his share of the quota we shall more than make the quota. Wherever you go, always be prepared to take a subscription. By the Lord's grace we shall go over the top on everything, books, booklets and subscriptions if we do our part, namely, be a publisher and put in time in the field.

There is every reason why the quota can be reached in May. Weather conditions no longer hinder anyone in any part of the country. Rural territory can easily be covered, usually yielding much fruit. With favorable weather the doorstep setups using the “Instruction” record will be unanimously pressed into service. Evening witnessing will be carried on all over the country. We shall have a new peak of publishers. With the willing publishers of The Theocracy moving forward unitedly, each having in mind his share in the quota, we shall reach our goal.

April 1, 1941

To the Magazine Publishers:

The witnesses for Jehovah are the representatives of the Most High and should deport themselves accordingly with dignity and gentility whether on the street or elsewhere. In the magazine street work avoid anything that has the appearance of rudeness. It is reported that some call out: “Vitamins for the mind. Read The WATCHTOWER." That is hardly proper.

The exhibiting of signs during this magazine service seems to have performed its work. Henceforth no more signs will be used. Calling attention in a dignified way to the headings of the articles in the magazine will be more effective, no doubt.

Let the people observe from your words and conduct that you desire to help and comfort them in these days of great stress.


Be fearless of man and trust entirely in Jehovah and his King; therefore be very courageous. Jehovah and the Lord Jesus guide and bless you.


Your brother and servant by His grace,



Proper Expressions for Street Work

The brethren, in offering the magazines on street corners, should use expressions that will not cause provocation. The expression “Religion is a racket" lias been used extensively in the past, and from now on should be discontinued, and constructive statements be used. The publisher can express to the passerby, “Remedy for would distress. Read The Watchtower, 5c.’’ This should not be shouted in one’s ear or be heard across the street, but should be spoken in a moderate tone of voice. Publishers may call out the titles of the articles in the magazines ; or, set out below are some expressions that Brother Rutherford has approved in connection with “Read The Watchtower, 5c”:

Remedy for world distress The Theocracy, the hope of mankind Escape from death at Armageddon Bible removes fear of death

Prophecy shows deliverance near Truth brings light, peace, Joy World peace by God's Kingdom Comfort for ‘men of good-will’ quota. In addition, make a separate quota for the month of May, reckoned at one subscription, five books and thirty booklets per publisher, and then permit nothing to prevent your company from making it. Keep a weekly check on your progress; and if you begin to lag behind, put forth additional effort. Let the servants take the lead. Undoubtedly May will witness the greatest effort put forth by Jehovah’s people during the Watchtower Campaign.

These are serious days. Throughout the earth freedom of action is rapidly being taken from Jehovah’s people. This may be the last Watchtower Campaign. Do not neglect your privileges. Do your part. With full confidence in Jehovah join in the victory song I Lay aside your “weights” I Remember your individual quotas! One subscription, five books, thirty booklets, during May! Jehovah’s will shall be done.

Seek righteousness and meekness Theocracy means totalitarians’ end Understand God’s present purpose Kingdom benefits for humankind

Your Bible questions answered Teach children the truth

The Consolation magazine has printed on it (in large type, displaying the contents of the magazine) expressions that you may use when on the street corners.

All the publishers of the Kingdom should be diligent in having a part in the magazine work. It is hoped that in this manner, throughout the United States, more than one million magazines (of both The Watchtower and Consolation') will be distributed each month. We feel sure that everyone of the magazine publishers will give special heed to Brother Rutherford’s letter printed above and follow the organization instructions.
