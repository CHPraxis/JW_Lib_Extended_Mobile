Jehovah in his goodness and mercy poured out upon his people a blessing to overflowing, a blessing all of which could not be contained by his grateful people. The Theocratic convention of Jehovah’s witnesses came to a conclusion Sunday night, July 28, but what occurred at the convention will never be forgotten; for a witness began there which will be carried to the ends of the earth. Brother Rutherford was richly blessed by the Lord and, because of Jehovah’s favor, was able to come to the convention and address the assembly at Detroit and 17 other convention cities. It was a most joyful occasion to hear the instruction of the Lord brought to us. and the climax of the convention came when the president of the Society delivered the lecture “Religion as a World Remedy”. IIow true it was, as Brother Rutherford stated, he had to present both sides of the argument because of the failure of the Roman Catholic Hierarchy to put up their man to talk in favor of religion I

Religion received a real beating at Detroit, and the exposing of that wicked organization continues as the dirt of that wicked organization is brought out and made plain to the people.

New Book Released

The companions, alongside of Jehovah’s witnesses, cheered and applauded with real joy the presentation of the new book Religion by the president of the Society. This was one of the most thrilling moments of the convention. There is no question In the minds of the brethren who attended the Theocratic Convention of Jehovah's witnesses that It was the I,ord's will that his people assemble this year in Detroit at the Convention Hall and the other convention cities tied in by direct wire. The Lord’s hand was manifest from beginning to end in all the preliminary arrangements, in trying to find a place for the convention, and in all the detailed activity of the convention itself. Even as the Lord has directed his organization for many years and continues to do so, so did he direct what should be accomplished at this convention.
