Glorious Opportunity to Cast Off Bondage

The issue of freedom to worship Almighty God or remain in bondage to the present evil world still persists. Jehovah, in the exercise of his loving-kindness, has brought his people to another year, and we find ourselves at the most eventful time of that year. Preserved by Jehovah’s almighty power and made free by Christ, opportunity now presents itself for every individual claiming any measure whatsoever of love toward God to make use of the present freedom and he found wholly worshiping Him by serving the interests of the Free Nation. Who, out of sheer gratitude, will avail himself of this privilege?

No text could be more appropriate than Galatians 5:1, reminding us that we have not been made free to become entangled again in the yoke of bondage of Satan's world. All restraining bonds and hindrances, then, will be cast aside by those who have been made free, that during the April Testimony Period no one will be idle.

The week-end of April 8-9 will be set aside by all companies as a special period of activity, and should (if our freedom to worship is really appreciated) result in a peak number of publishers taking part in field activity. On Saturday evening April 8, many thousands of God’s people will assemble In commemoration of the Memorial. Proper reaction to Instruction received at that time will result in a peak of publishers by the evening of April 9. With such an early start, the month of April will provide excellent opportunity for the greatest number of publishers yet to engage in Kingdom service. Additionally, from the thousands of book studies that have been held countrywide by the Lord's servants, there must be many more now able to take the message of freedom to others. Last year 72,490 , lovers of freedom and righteousness availed themselves of freedom to worship God, establishing an all- i time peak of publishers. This year the Society hopes 80,000 will respond. Actually, there are many thousands more than this number that could be free and worship God by sharing in active service. The question must he decided individually. What will be the total response for 1944?

Will you share in the forward MOVEMENT?

Again the monthly message to the Lord's people to consider pioneer activity has been read and dealt with at the company service meeting. Again the question presents itself, "How have I responded?”

The hindrances and obstacles that prevent many from taking part in the pioneer service were shown in the letter to be surmountable. The pioneer life is not an easy one; all contemplating taking it up will first sit down and count the cost; but the joyful fact in the whole matter is, it is a happy life and the faithful pioneer servant will be sustained. Perseverance, faith and trust in the great Provider make it possible. You can be a pioneer; not in your own strength, but by God’s help.

The educational angle is also set forth in the letter as being an inducement to take up full-time activity. The peace of mind and inward joy are also dealt with. Over 180 have enrolled between February 15 and March 15. With Satan’s world offering nothing but misery and final death, and God’s new world extending the opportunity of life by fully serving its interests, why longer delay?

Progress of “Watchtower” Campaign

Subscriptions Pouring in by the Thousands!!

All expectations have been shattered ! Although we have had some excellent Watchtower campaigns in the past several years, none of them have come anywhere near the present one. We are thrilled to announce that 46,044 new subscriptions were received in this office for the United States alone from February 1 to March 10, which is nearly one-half of the quota of the entire campaign. We are now two-thirds through the campaign and each day the mail is heavily laden with more subscriptions to be entered. The country as a whole is keeping up with its quota very well. Have you reached your quota?

The Greatest “Watchtower” Campaign in History

In comparison with last year’s campaign, we now have several thousand more subscriptions for the same period of time. This stupendous increase has been made despite gasoline rationing, bad weather, restrictions, travel difficulties, and many other inconveniences. The brethren this year have the advantage of the Theocratic ministry training, greater appreciation of their part in the free-education work, and a greater determination to overcome all barriers to advance the Kingdom interests. Naturally, this tremendous influx in a few weeks taxes the capacity of the office here. Therefore, if the subscribers do not receive their copies as soon as anticipated, explain the cause.

Great has been the joy of Jehovah’s people as they have gone forth during this campaign. Keep up the good work during April, and let’s make it the top month of the campaign.

The brethren will hail with delight the provision by the Society of a detailed folder of information that will enable the companies everywhere to follow a unified system of keeping the various company accounts. Two copies of this four-page folder are being sent to all companies, one for the company servant and the other for the accounts servant, so that they may go over the situation together and put the system into operation right away. Complete illustrations appear in tlie folder as to how each account will be kept, thereby eliminating all guesswork and providing for a uniform system to be used by every company. Companies that heretofore have had difficulty in keeping their accounts in proper order will be especially benefited.

The Assistant Company Servant

In co-operation with the company servant, the assistant company servant has an excellent opportunity to aid and direct the company toward a condition of unity and efficient operation in Kingdom service. Essentially, then, the assistant company servant should be able to take over all duties of the company serv-■ ant, be capable and duly qualified to handle the affairs of the company in the absence of the company servant.

The co-operation between these two brethren, therefore, must he very close, resulting in mutual agreement and action. The assistant company servant will strive for neatness, accuracy and proper order in all his records, having this end in view. Efficient and up-to-date records are necessary for a progressive and diligent service unit. The converse also is true: where no attempt is made to maintain proper records, slackness and looseness are evident in the field activity. Substitution of other forms than those provided by the Society is unnecessary and only results in confusion.

In small companies where there are only two servants, the assistant company servant will co-operate with the company servant, each performing his assigned duties as shown on the Society’s appointment sheet. This includes making territory assignments, caring for supplies of literature, magazines, and sound equipment, or looking after accounts and back-calls. In large companies the assistant company servant may need some aid in properly caring for the company records. In all companies, when handling Watchtower and Consolation subscriptions he will see that the Subscription Record sheet is properly filled out and sent in with the subscription blanks.

Assisting Other Servants

By completely filling out the Publisher’s Record cards, the monthly breakdown of study conductors’ monthly reports, and the monthly record of company activity, the assistant company servant has at his finger tips an exact knowledge of the company’s condition. Where laxity appears in any particular line of activity, he is able in co-operation with all the other servants of the company to stress these points and find the remedy therefor. He will also watch for individual ability as indicated by the different publishers’ field reports, seeing to it that active and zealous publishers are considered for use in various capacities in the company organization.

The assistant company servant will be able to furnish necessary information to all other servants of the company for the proper functioning of their duties. If a publisher holds a territory without working it properly, this information is conveyed to the territory servant for action. Likewise where the records show failure to advance the back-call, book-study, or any other feature of service, the assistant company servant is able to lay the facts as they exist before the various servants for correction. All servants, therefore, by having access to the assistant company servant’s files, will be able to keep their respective line of service in the best possible condition.

Thus the assistant company servant holds a position that may be used to great good for the entire company. The Lord’s blessing is upon orderliness and cleanliness as expressed in efficient prompt recording. Prosperity will attend faithfulness in this line; carelessness will result in a corresponding laxity in company activity.
