“Ready Always for Defense”

Requires Service, Vigilance, and Study


Is the Pioneer Service for YOU??


Be a Defender of the Gospel by Being a Publisher

Jehovah God has granted all publishers of The Theocracy freedom to worship Him. This freedom is secured to them alone and apart from all peoples. Worldly authorities attempt through mischievous laws to regulate, restrict and control their worship. The issue of God’s law or “Caesar’s” law comes to the fore, requiring the fighting witnesses of Jehovah to be “ready always for defense” of the gospel and their right to preach it, as well as the right of misinformed millions to hear the Kingdom evangel.

To be “ready always for defense” of this freedom to worship Jehovah requires that one be a user of that freedom for the intended purpose, namely, worship. This worship is rendered in the form of service in the field, Kingdom service in all its features. Thus, the primary requisite is to be a Kingdom publisher, an active ‘true worshiper’ of the Almighty God.

To be at all times ready to defend this right to serve God requires, too, that the publisher be vigilant and continually prepare himself to meet, resist, cope with and push back opposition to his worship that is designed to push him off from proper performance of his commission to preach the Kingdom gospel.

As will be observed, the service poster for November, headed by the words “Ready Always for Defense”, illustrates a Theocratic publisher making his defense before the court concerning the Kingdom and his right to preach its gospel. He has made himself “ready always for defense” of his hope and his Godgiven right of freedom to worship God prior to this time. He has made a thorough study and has a good working knowledge of the procedure as outlined in the Freedom of Worship booklet, and he is applying it rightly here. He has a comprehensive knowledge of Kingdom truths through diligent study of The Watchtower, the Informant, Organization Instructions, and other communications from the Society, the Lord’s instrument on earth. However, it is not his knowledge of these things that brings him into court. No; it is the application of this knowledge by actual worship, house-to-house preaching of the Kingdom evangel. His constant devotion to this service work in all its features is his worship, and it is this that he is defending. Through this constant devotion, study and application of knowledge gained through study, he has prepared himself, and made himself "ready always for defense” of freedom of worship.

However, it is not only in the courts of “Caesar” that the free worshiper of God must make a defense. In his daily activity, constantly, he is always ready to defend his right to preach the gospel. When in the house-to-house work he finds opportunities arise constantly to make defense of the truth. In back-calls and book studies he has the opportunity to explain the truth of the gospel and defend it against religious lies that have for years stumbled the good-will people. By constantly keeping on the watch for privileges of service, and being “ready always for defense” of these privileges of service, he maintains his position of freedom to worship God.

November’s Equipment for Worship

November will complete the regular offer of the book “The Truth Shall Make You Free” and the two booklets The Coming World Regeneration and Religion Reaps the Whirlwind, on a contribution of 30c. This will mean that six months will have been afforded to get this vital information into the hands of the people. Most individual territories will have been covered fully with this presentation. However, the reports to the Society indicate that there is still much territory throughout the country in company assignments : isolated sections and “undesirable” territories that are accessible, and that need to be covered with the above offer. This will afford the opportunity to clear out any surplus stock of the above three publications in preparation for the December activity. All publishers not holding individual territories are urged to get into this group work and assist in getting this territory covered and the people of good-will reached.

Alternative Offer

For those publishers and companies who have fully worked their territory with the above offer, an alternative offer is provided. This will be made up of one colored-cover booklet and three self-covered booklets released prior to the booklet Freedom in the New World. This combination will be presented on a 10c contribution. This too will afford companies and pioneers the opportunity to clear out any surplus stock they might have of these booklets and get them into the hands of the people where they will accomplish their purpose.

By continually engaging in preaching freedom and fighting off all attacks to prevent you from doing this work, not only will you maintain your right to worship God but you will maintain the right of “strangers” to hear this message and you will have the opportunity to present the truth that will wipe away the misrepresentation of religion that has so long confused and blinded them. Also, you will defend your position of strength as a Kingdom publisher by ‘studying to show yourself approved’ and thus “be ready always for defence to every one who is asking of you an account concerning the hope that is in you.” (1 Pet. 3:15, Young’s)

Consider Your Privileges

The Lord Jesus laid down the Father’s policy for judging his people when he said that they would be judged by His word in the last days. This undoubtedly has reference to the revealed word brought forth for our guidance now. Recent Watchtowers, on ‘The Holy Spirit’, “The King’s Marriage Feast,” and “Organized for Final Work”, clearly reveal the Lord’s requirements and bring forcibly to our attention the principles the Lord uses in judging his people.

He invites those who wish to engage in his service faithfully to do so. Some joyfully accept, and receive his favor and blessing; others, burdened with the cares of this life, reject his gracious invitation. This was true of the anointed as outlined in “The King’s Marriage Feast”. Some refused to accept the invitation. Others accepted the invitation but refused to put on the wedding garment, that is, to faithfully and joyfully do the Lord's work as he directs. Self-examination by each one enables him to see where he stands in this judgment.

The Jonadabs are undergoing a similar test. They are to wash their robes in the “blood of the Lamb”, and to manifest their appreciation of the Lord’s goodness toward them by ‘standing before the throne

Consider the Field

There are almost 60 counties in the southern part of the United States that contain a population of 50 percent or more colored people. Neither the white nor the colored in this territory have been witnessed to since the dates shown. Some of this territory goes as far back as 1935. There are undoubtedly people of good-will in these counties to whom the Lord's people are obligated to carry the Kingdom message. Pioneer brethren are the ones upon whom this responsibility rests particularly. We would ask all of them, therefore, to seriously consider this matter, and, those pioneers desiring an assignment to any of these counties shown, please write the Society immediately for an assignment, stating a first, second, and third choice.

“Count the Cost”

Rather than just ‘counting the cost’ in terms of pleasures, comforts and conveniences to be sacrificed, and the hardships of pioneer life, consider above all the rich blessings, enjoyable experiences, and joy in the Lord’s service and receiving of his approval that might be lost if you don’t become a pioneer. Ask yourself, “Can I afford to pay the price?”

The “Freedom Now!” Testimony Period


Campaign for December

Wisely Use Your Freedom by Preaching

Freedom now ! What good tidings for tlie meek! Good news for men of good-will held prisoners by Satan’s organization, and who long for freedom from sin and selfishness, from superstition and religion. Yes, Jehovah, as the God of Freedom and the Free is today, now, offering this freedom to all lovers of righteousness, to all men of goodwill toward him. And by what does he make men free? By means of the truth; for ‘’the truth shall make you free”. To this end he is causing a world-wide campaign of education in freedom-giving truth to be carried on.

To carry on this campaign he has commissioned all of his servants today, the only ones who are free in the true sense of the word, to “preach good tidings unto tlie meek”, to “bring out the prisoners from the prison", to proclaim “freedom now”. In fact, that is the very purpose for which they were set free: to preach freedom. And their continuance as free men depends upon their asserting their freedom by preaching this good news.

The New Offer

To this end Jehovah has arranged the “Freedom Now!” Testimony Period for the month of December, and has furnished us with two new instruments, the bound book “The Kingdom Is at Hand’’ and the booklet “The Kingdom of God Is Sigh", which will be offered to all during this campaign on a contribution of 25c. What a hope-inspiring message they contain! With clearness and beauty they show the Kingdom to be the main theme of the Bible, tracing it from Genesis to Revelation, and giving cogent proof that the Kingdom is here. That is why freedom exists and can be offered to the people now. By the time this campaign begins more than three months will have passed since we received these instruments at the United Announcers’ Theocratic Assembly, and by which time all should be thoroughly familiar with their contents and filled with a keen desire to bring them to others. Let us, therefore, begin this campaign with an impetus of zeal and enthusiasm that will carry us through the entire month.

So that this campaign may get off to a proper start, however, it is also necessary that all preparations be made ahead of time. Sufficient stock should be on hand, so that there will be no slowing up because of lack of literature; all territories should be checked, so that those being worked may be renewed if due, and those being neglected be turned in for others to work. The back-call files should be carefully examined, and all whose names appear therein should be given an opportunity to obtain the new literature at once, and also to join in offering freedom to men of good-will. This activity could be very well worked in with the group back-call work on the company's back-call night. Arrangements should also be made for group house-to-house witnessing, particularly for the benefit of new and irregular publishers. In making plans it should be borne in mind that December brings cold weather with it, and therefore may somewhat circumscribe our activity. However, the righteous requirements of Jehovah’s organization remain the same, regardless of the time of the year.

We still have our quotas as special pioneer publishers, general pioneers, or company publishers, and therefore all will put forth diligent efforts to overcome this winter-weather obstacle instead of permitting it to overcome them.

All territories are virgin soil for this new offer. Go over the territory carefully, taking time to note the result of each call on the House to House Record form; calling back at some other time of the day or week to contact all not-at-homes. , Territory cannot be considered completely witnessed to even once, until every home has had the opportunity to accept or reject the message of “Freedom Now”. As interest is found and literature is placed, this should be noted, and back-calls made upon all such; for it is necessary not only to plant the seed but also to water it. Therefore call back and stimulate their appreciation of the message they have received. Place the question booklet with them and show them its value and how to use it, thus working toward a book study.

Nor will we preach “freedom now” only from house to house, at the doors and in the homes of the people, but all Theocratic publishers during December will also engage in the magazine work, on the street and from store to store. And while engaged in this work they will always carry with them a copy of the book “The Kingdom Is at Hand” and several copies of the booklet “The Kingdom of God Is ' High”, so as to be able to make the most of every opportunity that comes their way.

The Resolution

At the time of the release of the new book “The Kingdom Is at Hand” a resolution was made and adopted and all who accepted the spirit of that resolution were given a free copy of the book. That resolution clearly showed that the Kingdom has been established; that tlie nations have had the Kingdom gospel preached to them and that they have chosen to ignore it; that they are going ahead with their own scheme for world rule; that Jehovah’s servants have given their allegiance to Jehovah’s kingdom; that the command to preach this gospel of the Kingdom still holds; and that they will continue to preach it down to the final end of Satan’s organization, never slacking the hand. Therefore all who accepted a copy of the book thereby expressed their unqualified allegiance to that Kingdom and unequivocally committed themselves to zealous, consistent and continuous activity in preaching tlie gospel of the Kingdom. By announcing the Kingdom we shall be preaching “freedom now”; for only the Kingdom can give true freedom.

There is much work to be done. “Freedom now” must be preached in all the world for a testimony. Will it be accomplished within the divinely allotted time? Jehovah will see to It that it will be. The question that concerns us individually is: Will I have a part in this great and all-important work of preaching the gospel of the Kingdom, of proclaiming freedom now ? That will depend upon our faith and appreciation. If we have put our faith in the Kingdom of Jehovah as the government of our choice; if we appreciate it as tlie Instrument which Jehovah will use to vindicate his name and give everlasting life and true freedom to man; if we appreciate tlie glorious treasure of service that is ours in connection
