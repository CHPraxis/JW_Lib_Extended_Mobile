"NAME

With God’s children there should be nothing in a name to furnish an excuse for the exaltation of men. Some persons appear to gladly seize upon the meaning that may be placed upon a name as an excuse for self-exaltation or for unnecessary criticism of others.

The name “Director'', by which this paper has been known for some time, means in the English merely one who transmits information concerning the directing or instructions relative to field service work. In some languages, however, the word when translated appears to have a different meaning. Eor instance, the name “Director” translated in one language is given the meaning of “Commander, Big boss, or Important one”. All such names are displeasing to the Lord and should be avoided. Eor reasons assigned, this paper hereafter will be designated as informant. That name means merely one who or that which gives information. The purpose of this paper is to give information to the field workers about the work and the manner in which it is to be done, so that all may work harmoniously. When you receive your copy you may expect to find therein up-to-date information given out by the Society organization for the benefit of those who do the publishing work concerning the Kingdom.

When a man desires to be approved of men, the Devil seizes upon that selfish desire to induce such a one to exalt himself and thus cause friction in the Lord’s organization. It seems best, therefore, that the following changes should be made in the names or titles of positions held in the organization, to wit: Instead of using the name or title “company director”, hereafter that position will be known as “company servant”; instead of using the name “regional service director”, hereafter that position will be known as “regional servant”; instead of “divisional leader”, hereafter that position will be known as “divisional servant”. Remember the words of the Lord Jesus: ‘For one is your Master, even Christ, and whosoever shall exalt himself shall be abased.’ (Matt. 23:9-12) All the true followers of Christ Jesus are servants. There is no excuse or justification to exalt one above another. To whatsoever position one in the Lord's organization is assigned, he should serve obediently and joyfully and without murmuring, complaining, or faultfinding.

Where a number of companies are working together, for instance, such as the area of Greater New York or London, the person who takes the lead and transmits the instructions received, instead of being known as “service director”, will hereafter be known as “servant of companies”, and the servant of the separate companies in this organization will be known as “captain of service”.

Hereafter none of the servants in the field will be known as “sharpshooters”. That is eliminated, and all such workers are considered as companies or integral parts of companies. If there is just one isolated person in the community, instead of being known as “sharpshooter”, he is merely a servant, meaning, of course, servant of the Lord.

Let us see to it that no excuse is furnished for self-exaltation of any creature. We are all brethren in Christ and delight to serve one Master and one God. All at unity in Christ will work exactly in harmony and will be willing and anxious to carry out the information received through the INFORMANT, or other means that proceeds from the proper source.

Heretofore the Society’s representative at a branch office has been known as “manager” or “director”. Hereafter such representatives will be known as “branch servants”, meaning the representative of the Society at a branch office, acting with authority from the main office of the Society and, of course, as servant of the Lord. All branch offices, please be governed accordingly.

New Phonograph

Weighs Only 9% Pounds
