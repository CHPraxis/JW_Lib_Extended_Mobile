New Peak of 76,373 Publishers Reached in March!


Back-Call and Book-Study Activity to Be Stressed

The tongue is a little member, but even though small, it is difficult to tame. When used unwisely it is capable of inflicting deep wounds and stirring strife. But when used wisely, the tongue of a Kingdom publisher brings health to himself and others and praise to Jehovah. In the words of our text for this month, “There is that speaketh like the piercings of a sword: but the tongue of the wise is health. The lip of truth shall be established forever : but a lying tongue is but for a moment.” (Prov. 12:18, 19) Indeed, “death and life are in the power of the tongue.” Hence, the publisher who wants health and life for himself and others will make wise use of the tongue during May, presenting the book “Let God Be True”, together with the booklet, The Joy of All the People, on a contribution of 35c.

In presenting the month’s offer, wise and faithful ambassadors will apply instruction received through the service meetings and Theocratic ministry school. A wise, healthy tongue is a tactful tongue, kind, considerate, helpful. To use it aright, converse with the people on points of agreement, showing how “Let God Be True” will aid them to get further information on subjects they are interested in. These subjects can be readily learned by courteously allowing them to use their tongues to express themselves. Our ministry at the doors is a conversational one, not the delivery of a lecture. Barriers and stumbling stones can be gathered out of the way and much literature placed by wise use of the tongue in our house-to-house ministry during May, and the way opened up for further education of people of good-will.

Back-Calls and Studies Essential

The truth is not generally learned in one day or by the casual reading of a book. It takes time, effort and the assistance of an equipped minister. Back-calls and book studies are essential. Here the faithful ambassador can regularly bring the healing words of the Great Physician to those of good-will. And as each succeeding lesson in “Let God Be True" is carefully studied, they are brought closer to the point where their lips, too, can speak truth and be established forever.

Let your tongue be used wisely during May, then, by calling back on each person who has obtained “Let God Ue True”, or only the booklet The Joy of All the People, or even where interest was shown but no literature placed. Call back also on those whose subscriptions have expired; stir interest in the magazine and seek to start home studies. As notices are received by the companies they should be distributed to the publishers and called on promptly. Remember, if they resubscribe, the subscriptions can be counted as new.

Some people of good-will may have been unable to subscribe to The Watchtower during the four-month campaign. Although the special subscription offer is not to be featured from door-to-door during May, we may give those who have desired subscriptions and those on our back-call lists the benefit of the eightbooklet premium. Every effort should be made to complete this final cleanup of the Watchtower campaign early in the month, so that the subscriptions can be in the hands of the Society by May jo, and thus be counted in the campaign totals.

Public Meetings Aid Back-Calls and Studies

By this time we should all be well along in our new public meeting campaign. Companies that use the tongue wisely in this feature of the service are almost always healthy in other features as well. Careful coverage of territory means that more good-will persons will be visited and more “Let God Be True” will be placed, resulting in added opportunities for back-calls and studies. Do not wait until interest has begun to wane before making such calls. Go back the next week. Give them an invitation to the current lecture. Then, by wise use of the tongue, fan the spark of interest into flame. By the time the two-week back-call activity arrives, many of those not really interested will have been determined, permitting special attention to be given to those manifesting real interest.

"Let God Be True” Service Meeting

After having spent four months presenting magazines and booklets, a service meeting during May devoted to the book “Let God Be True" will be beneficial. Parts on the program may include a discussion of talking points on the book, demonstrations to illustrate its presentation, information on following up placements of “Let God Be True”, or how to present it to Watchtower subscribers. Wise tongues can also be taught to start book studies. There is a wealth of material which alert servants can work into this meeting, presenting it in a variety of ways, so that it will be interesting and profitable to all.

Let the month of May, then, be one in which the tongue of the wise publisher is kept busy bringing health, leaving no time for things which cut life prospects to ‘but a moment’. How wise it is to use the lip for truth and ‘be established forever’ I

Subscription Renewals and Inquiries

Renewal blanks are sent to each Watchtoioer and Awake! subscriber prior to expiration of subscriptions. These blanks should be filled in completely and sent in at the time of renewal, making sure to note any recent change of address.

It will be of even greater assistance to the Society if you will pin or paste to the renewal blank a recent address label from your magazine or its wrapper. All essential information, such as name, address, zone number, etc., appears on each address label. The expiration symbol, such as W 8 48 or G 9 48, should also appear on the label you select to send in with your renewal slip. The Watchtoioer magazine is denoted by W and Awake! by G. The numerals denote the month and year of expiration.

When writing the Society asking for a change of address or making other inquiries as to your own or another’s subscription, the matter can be more quickly handled if you will attach the address label to your card or letter.

Subscribers in other countries will, of course, send renewal blanks to their respective Branch ofiices.

Welcoming the Stranger Essential

This world has utterly forsaken Jehovah’s righteous rule of action: ■‘Love thy neighbor as thyself.” Hence, hate, greed, lying, terror and Godlessness form the pattern of life in this doomed old world. However, love, generosity, faith, truthfulness, and Godliness order the steps of Jehovah’s New World ministers, whose lives are governed by genuine “neighbor love”.

Universal hatred, characteristic of Satan’s crumbling organization, casts mankind into a whirlpool of warmongering, hate propaganda and wholesale slaughter. On the other hand, sincere “neighbor love” finds its expression through the worldwide ministry of Jehovah’s witnesses in preaching the life-giving Kingdom gospel. Is there any better way to demonstrate love of “thy neighbor” than by preaching the gospel of peace and life? Of course not! Then preach we will during the “Neighbor Love” Testimony Period in June by our actions supporting God’s own rule: “Love thy neighbor as thyself: I am the Lobd.” —Leviticus 19:18.

Expressing Love

Just as we received a knowledge of Jehovah’s purposes through patient, loving instruction in God's Word, so we should unselfishly afford our neighbors the same opportunity. During June the first step to this desired end will be presenting the offer, “Let God Be True” and The Joy of All the People, on a contribution of thirty-five cents. Where “Let God Be True” cannot be placed, endeavor to leave a copy of TJie Joy of All the People.

Through the diligent efforts of all Kingdom publishers, many bound books will be placed during the twomonth campaign of May and June, and a solid, basic foundation laid for back-call activity. Then, call back with a purpose on every placement. The purpose? To establish home Bible book studies! With the comprehensive Bible coverage of “Let God Be True”, its thought-provoking questions and with a full knowledge of its contents, active Theocratic publishers should not encounter difficulty in starting home Bible studies in “Let God Be True".

Shortly it will be time for all companies to express their “neighbor love” by calling on our rural friends, some of whom have uot been visited since last summer. Much work was accomplished then and much literature placed. During the winter months our out-of-town neighbors who took literature have had ample opportunity to examine it. Hence, the time is now ripe for all companies holding rural territory to start organizing for rural activity at convenient times throughout the month. The better summer weather will permit a more diligent and expansive follow-up of interest in rural communities. Attend to this, and try to start studies.

The Pioneer Rai

Join the Joyful Throng

Jehovah’s blessing has been manifest in the gratifying response to letters inviting publishers to take up the pioneer service. The growing list of pioneers has not come from the ranks of young single persons alone. Many of the new pioneers are married, some with families and other obligations. Convinced that Satan’s old world is on the way out, they have resolved to no longer spend time unnecessarily in old-world pursuits. Instead, they are now spending their time and energy wholly to Jehovah’s praise.

But our quota has not yet been reached. There are many more who can and should be pioneers. Earnestly and prayerfully weigh all the points involved, and perhaps you, too, may make this decisive Theocratic step. Many have written in to tell us that their previous objections and excuses have been completely broken down by the Scriptural reasons advanced for pioneering, leaving them with but the one course: become a pioneer. And this course they have joyfully followed with full appreciation that pioneering is not ‘just another job’, but a full-time service that will bring forth Jehovah’s blessing.

The Joy of the Full-Time Ministry

Many expressions of joy are constantly being received from those now enlisted as pioneers. This does not mean, however, that pioneering is an easy life. Without doubt, many spread that will be well worth the added effort.

And what are some of the things to be expected that will be of such value? Do you sometimes feel that you need additional instruction in house-to-house witnessing? that your efforts, though diligent, are not accomplishing as much as they should? Such instruction will be given at the district assemblies.

Or, perhaps your difficulty lies in making back-calls or getting book studies started. If so, you should not fail to attend the district assembly. All features of the service will receive their fair share of attention, including street-work and public meeting activity.

Others are seeking a remedy for poor meeting attendance or company difficulties. What are the basic causes for poor attendance at meetings? Can they be overcome? Is there any practical way to erase company difficulties and work together in unison? Who could fail to profit by hearing representatives of the Society present information on these Important matters?

It will be Impossible for one to retain all of the practical information there presented. It would be wise, therefore, to come prepared to take extensive notes, so that you can apply the instructions in the field, as well as share things learned with persons of good-will and publishers who were unable to attend. Undoubtedly, you will want to jot down suggestions on handling your service meetings and the main points of demonstrations and experiences, as well as material presented in the various talks.

Theocratic service instruction, divine enlightenment, comfort and many other blessings await you at your district assembly. Are you putting forth every effort to be there? No one who is able to attend can afford to miss the 1948 district assemblies.

Vacation Pioneer Service

Again vacation pioneer time is at hand. Instead of being taught in school, during vacation young ministers oan go out and teach others. By spending the equivalent of school time in field service, six hours a day for five days a week, it will be possible to meet the new reduced quota of 120 hours per month. Undoubtedly, many more young folks will feel capable of meeting this quota and will readily respond. Let your youthful zeal and energy be used in singing praises to God. putting to practical use the things you have learned in Theocratic ministry school and service meetings.
