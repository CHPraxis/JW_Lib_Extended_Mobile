Expanding Your Ministry 3-Bound-Book Offer to Be Presented


Increase Your Praise as a Pioneer


Outdoor and Rural Public Meetings Increase With a deep appreciation for the glorious light of the gospel and for the inestimable privilege that is ours in being Jehovah’s witnesses at this world’s end, each and every minister should seek to expand his ministry by now ‘praising more and more’. The Lord through his organization assists you to increasingly serve him as his minister through ample provision for this individual expansion. Just consider:

Spiritual food so necessary for strong Theocratic growth is made available in The Watchtower. Awake! keeps ministers abreast of the times, provides material useful in Kingdom service. The Informant gives practical counsel on ways and means of advancing praise and expanding your ministry. Weekly company meetings and other assemblies are arranged for enlarging your Theocratic education. Circuit servants visit all companies and assist in increasing local field activity. Now, to further expansion through orderly and unified action, efficient, comprehensive Counsel on Theocratic Organization for Jehovah’s witnesses is provided. And the Lord through his Word establishes the various features of public praising.

As God’s minister, you have these varied provisions available for your use. Therefore, “attend to these duties, let them absorb you, so that all men may note your progress.” (1 Tim. 4:15, Moffatt) By all means expand your ministry!

The Offer

Kingdom publishers will ‘let men note their progress’ in May through an extensive bound-book campaign. The offer will be the three latest bound books and the new booklet The Kingdom Hope of All Mankind, on a $1.00 contribution. Instructive demonstrations should be arranged to “put you always in remembrance” of the various ways to present the offer. When offering the literature, be alert to any situation. And remember: An engaging smile and a pleasant introduction oftentimes gain a hearing ear. Reflect genuine interest in the one to whom you are ministering. Display patience when confronted with an “I’m busy” or “I’m not interested”. Tactfully refute such objections and proceed to “put your message attractively, yet pointedly” with an enthusiastic and convincing delivery.

Are you talking about “The Truth Shall Make You Free"? Then highlight such features as the explanation of the Biblical account of creation ; where we are in the stream of time or how the Bible was produced. Truly the Bible truths contained in this volume break the fetters of worldly ignorance and open wide the only way to real freedom.

Or are you discussing “The Kingdom Is at Hand”? Reference can be made to the current conflict for survival between man-made systems, showing that the only lasting government is God’s kingdom, which offers the remedy for all human ills. This book gives a detailed account of that kingdom.

As for “Let Ood Be True”, call attention to the truths regarding man. What is a soul? Who made the Devil, and why does God not destroy him? Why are so many prayers unanswered? And what is the truth about Jehovah’s witnesses? This Bible textbook brushes aside the plague of worldly propaganda in favor of Godly truth and the path to life-giving knowledge is made bright. All publishers are encouraged to try these and similar suggestions in field activity and realize their practical value in gospel presentation.

Of course, with the placement of literature the minister assumes the obligation and privilege of keeping a personal record of placements and then cultivating the interest by making back-calls. Home Bible studies continue to be sought after by each minister.

Public Meeting Progress

The possibilities of expanding your ministry through the public meeting activity are great when viewed in the light of public meeting attendance at recent circuit assemblies throughout the country. Just study these figures showing Saturday evening attendance compared with public meeting attendance: 1,101-2,551; 840-1,914; 633-1,638; 593 -1,004; 191 - 418. Does not the public’s attendance at these meetings reveal the prospects for expansion in the United States? Every company and publisher should lend support to developing this interest.

In addition to arranging for public meetings at Kingdom Halls, at the company book studies, public halls and schools, outdoor meetings can now be planned. Private yards, vacant lots, ball fields and public parks are a few of the locations that can be used.

Rural public meeting work will afford many companies the opportunity to expand their ministry into their rural territory. Many persons living in these rural communities cannot take time to travel to the towns or cities to attend public meetings, because of chores and other responsibilities, but can attend a near-by meeting in a schoolhouse, community hall or a neighbor’s yard.

Surely, then, progress in May will be noted by all men if you as a Kingdom publisher “attend to these duties, let them absorb you”, thus expanding your ministry.

Upon surveying the works of men, wise King Solomon was inspired to conclude that all things done by men are but vanity, the single exception being the service one renders to Jehovah. Having the riches of a king, a dominion, the conveniences of life and all that the world of that day could offer served only to convince him further that proper service to Jehovah was the only work of lasting benefit to men.

As we approach the time when Jehovah will completely destroy the works of vanity and wickedness, consecrated servants understand with greater appreciation the import of King Solomon’s conclusion. Following the example set by the Greater than Solomon, Christ Jesus, they shun the temptations to further entangle themselves with the things of this world, which serve to weight them down and hinder them in giving proper service to the Most High.

Desiring to sing Jehovah’s praises “more and more” and to take advantage of every opportunity to expand their ministry, many publishers are joining the ranks of the full-time ministers. But the call still goes out for more, for truly the harvest is great. The need for pioneers is great the world over and daily requests are made by companies for pioneer assistance. In fact, all companies could very effectively use the services of more pioneers in caring for the Kingdom interests assigned them. In addition to this, much isolated territory is in need of pioneer attention. At present there are in the United States more than 1,400 cities with a population of 4,000 or more without a local company organization to properly take care of the Kingdom interests.

New Cost List

With this Informant the Society is sending the new Cost List; one for each pioneer and two for each company.

It is important that the company servant and stock servant carefully study the instructions on how to order which appear in the first few pages of the booklet. If pioneers order direct from the Society they should also follow these instructions. many good-will persons desire to know the truth, the possibility of becoming a pioneer is again presented before all servants with the hope that at least 10 percent of the publishers will become pioneers. By exercising strong determination and putting complete trust in Jehovah, would it not be possible for you to begin pioneering now? or, if not immediately, within a short time?

Approach with confidence all the problems that the Devil puts forth to discourage you, knowing that Jehovah will bless your efforts as you keep working toward the pioneer goal. Thousands who are pioneering testify in “questionnaires” recently sent them that, though confronted at the start and still faced with problems of one sort or another, the power of Jehovah has been marvelously manifested in their behalf day after day. “Contending for the Faith” Testimony Period June to Be a Back-Call Month


Three-Bound-Book Offer Continued

In view of the evil times now prevailing, how imperative It is that everyone who has the faith of God’s kingdom be found earnestly, courageously and unceasingly ‘contending for that faith’! How can this be done? By taking the offensive and pushing the advance of true worship! During June, the “Contending fob the Faith” Testimony Period, we will use as aggressive instruments the three latest bound books together with the booklet The Kingdom Hope of All Mankind, on a contribution of one dollar.—Jude 3.

Contending at the Doors

The first place for using these weapons is at the doors of the people. With some this may mean first contending with themselves and overcoming a tendency to fear man or to love ease. Arriving at the doors of the people we must use the instruments effectively. That requires tactfulness, that sensitive mental discernment that will enable us to present the message attractively. Call attention to the truths that you think will especially appeal to the one to whom you are speaking. Point out the value of the scripture and subject indexes and tell them of your willingness to aid them to understand these books.

As you go from door to door seek to apply the lessons recently studied in the Theocratic ministry school on “Oral Emphasis”, “Modulation,” “Confidence and Poise,” and, above all, put your heart into your presentation. Remember, that is the most effective manner of speaking.

Gifts and Wills

From time to time brethren who own property and who have much or little of this world's goods have inquired of the Society as to how they can see to it that their property is disposed of according to their wishes when they die.

The Society’s legal counsel has prepared a Memorandum on Oifts and Wills which will be of great aid to any individual who wants to see to it that someone else will not dictate what shall be done with his possessions after his death.

This notice is not a solicitation of funds on the part of the Society, but rather a notice to the brethren that the Society will be glad to have its legal counsel advise you as to how to make proper provision so that your wishes will be carried out.

If you write the Society concerning gifts and wills, state your problem fully and the Society’s legal counsel will give whatever additional advice is necessary along with a copy of the Memorandum. ies. Hence the Society is designating June as a “back-call month”.

"Magazine Day”

Expand Watchtower and Awake! Distribution

“In one day the company placed more magazines than in the entire previous month!” So declares a circuit servant report, commenting on the results of the company’s first “Magazine Day”. Undoubtedly as “Magazine Day” becomes an established feature of every company, The Watchtower and Awake! will be read by more and more persons. The expanded distribution will bring Jehovah’s kingdom more prominently into public view, and help Increased numbers of the reading public to find hope and life.

Setting aside one day for magazine work does not cancel out distribution of magazines at other times when convenient. On the company’s magazine day, however, a concerted effort will be put forth by the entire company, including pioneers, to get The Watchtower and Awake! into the hands of the people, from house to house, store to store, and on the streets. Public meeting advertising will be carried on in conjunction with magazine work.

Presenting the Magazines

A brief magazine witness to a householder or storekeeper will usually be best. For example, after introducing himself, the publisher might say: “Today the Bible is being pushed more and more into the background. Actually, however, it is more important to know its contents than ever before. The Watchtower turns its readers to the Bible, where they can find comfort and hope. The leading article in this latest Issue is ..................... I will be very happy to leave this copy with you; it is five cents.” Or, if Awake! is featured: “This magazine is devoted to the spreading of truth. It will help you to be informed about what is taking place in the world. Its articles are factual, and varied, as you will notice by glancing at the titles. Awake! is not political, commercial, or religious. You may have this latest issue for five cents.” A number of short, arousing presentations can be developed. Coverage of more territory and placement of more magazines will assuredly result. Try to build a magazine route, with the same householders or storekeepers obtaining magazines regularly.

On the streets, ministers ought to be alert to witness and distribute magazines at every opportunity. Call out slogans in an enthusiastic, yet conversational and Theocratic way. Many have increased placements by approaching people standing about. In a quiet, poised manner inquire, “Have you read The Watchtower or Awake!?" Whether “yes” or “no” is the reply, a brief magazine testimony can follow, and a favorable response will often result. Cleanliness, a dignified, pleasant appearance, and confidence, on the part of each publisher will add to the effectiveness of magazine day.

Quota

It is suggested that each company establish a quota of at least five magazines per publisher per week. This does not mean that each one will be expected to take five copies of each issue. The company will order according to its needs, so that magazines will not accumulate. But publishers can be encouraged and helped to improve their magazine service, so as to reach or exceed this quota.

Deeds for Kingdom Halls

All companies or units that are not incorporated and that own their own halls should inspect the deeds to their property to ascertain whether or not the hall was deeded to the company or unit or to trustees for the company or unit.

If the name of an unincorporated company or unit was used in the deed without specifying the name or names of individuals as trustees, the title is defective and correction deeds should be made.

Any unincorporated company that owns a Kingdom Hall the title to which is not held by trustees should communicate with the legal department about the matter. Enclose with the letter a copy of the deed to the property on which the Kingdom Hall is located.
