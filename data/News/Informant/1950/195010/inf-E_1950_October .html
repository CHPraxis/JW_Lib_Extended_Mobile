Now Is the Time to Study New Legal Booklet

  • 1 Among the releases at the Theocracy's Increase Assembly held in New York was the new legal booklet, Defending and Legally Establishing the Good News. This booklet has been prepared and published to aid Jehovah’s witnesses to know their legal rights in carrying out their commission to preach “this gospel of the kingdom”. How very timely is the advice contained in the booklet, for as we draw ever nearer to this old world's final end, more and more is Jesus’ warning, “if they have persecuted me, they will persecute you also,” going to undergo fulfillment. That means, then, each one of Jehovah’s witnesses should study this new provision to be equipped and ready to fight and defend his legal, God-given right to proclaim the good news.

  • 2 Do you know what your legal and constitutional rights are and how to call upon them when needed? What to do if arrested? How to prepare for trial? How to obtain legal advice? The procedure of a court? Questions that may be asked during the trial? From past studies in Freedom of Worship and Jehovah’s Servants Defended some of this important knowledge has been gained. However, the new booklet supersedes these by bringing the information up to date and adds thereto by citing recent court decisions, giving counsel on how to side-step enemy tactics, showing proper conduct under various conditions, etc. Though few of Jehovah’s witnesses are lawyers by secular vocation, it is, nevertheless, essential for each publisher to study familiar with its contents and to insure effective presentation, all will want to first read and study it themselves.
