Debates

Occasionally individuals or groups in various parts of the country, who are usually interested primarily in gaining publicity for themselves, challenge Jehovah's witnesses to participate in debates. As a result, the question of whether or not to accept such challenges comes to the minds of the brethren.

Knowing as we do that the Scriptures are of no private interpretation and that Christian doctrine is not established by debating, we should not feel under any obligation to accept a challenge to debate. If a person honestly wants to know the truth, he is willing to sit down and dispassionately consider what God's Word has to say. Otherwise, his interest manifestly is to propagate his own ideas, and not to hear and learn.

In a debate as much error as truth is presented and the hearers, frequently moved by oratory and eloquence of speech, are prone to base their decision upon the showmanship of the Individual rather than upon God’s Word. Certainly little opportunity is afforded them to ‘prove all things’ by comparing the Scriptures with what is said. The truth can be much more effectively presented by going from house to house, contacting the sheep and holding regular Bible studies with them.

The final decision, of course, must be made by the local company. If the brethren should take it upon themselves to participate in a debate, they should go ahead and complete the arrangements, accepting the responsibility they have assumed and preparing diligently so as to be able to refute errors likely to be advanced as well as to present the truthful and Scriptural viewpoint. It accomplishes little to write the Society for counsel after such arrangements have already been made.

Company Book Studies

Equip for Efficient Home Bible Studies

The company book study plays an important part in our progress to Theocratic maturity. When properly arranged and supported by the publishers it becomes the very heart of Theocratic growth in that locality, whether rural or urban. It is a center for acquiring Kingdom information and equipping the publishers to more effectively serve the Kingdom.

All Theocratic-minded persons who have hope for life in God’s kingdom will welcome the opportunity to study together and discuss the advanced Kingdom light contained in the new book “This Means Everlasting Life”. The Society recommends that all company book studies change to a study of “This Means Everlasting Life” as soon as the publishers have their personal copies, so that they may become familiar with its contents for later use in the field. The company book study is the place to become equipped for your future service with this wonderful instrument ; therefore, all are strongly urged to attend and support it.

The manner in which the study is conducted determines to a great extent how effective it will be. The study conductor should be well prepared by thorough and careful study of the lesson so that the main points will be clear in his own mind. He should encourage all to comment at the study. This will include new at-tenders and children, who might be reluctant to comment unless encouraged to do so. It is not necessary for the conductor to repeat or sum up all the comments on the paragraph. This will have a tendency to discourage those commenting, because they may feel their comments were unsatisfactory. If the point is not made clear by comments from those present, then the conductor should add whatever is necessary to clarify it. It is best for the conductor to encourage the brethren and those present to do the commenting.

A company book study well conducted and supported is lively and a joy to the heart of all in attendance. It will deepen the impression of the truth on our minds as well as encourage us to dig for new truths. It reveals new points for our use in field service. Hence, the publishers should not only support this study, but also encourage persons of good will in that vicinity to attend with them and share in the spiritual feast.

Home Bible studies may be changed over to the study of “This Means Everlasting Life" as soon as you wish. However, if only a few chapters remain to be covered in the book you are now studying, especially if it is “Let God Be True", then it is suggested that you finish it before changing to the new book. It is well to keep the study within the hour, whether it is a company book study or a home Bible study.

In unison, we, as Jehovah’s consecrated ministers, will push on in this grandest work on earth. We are thankful for each provision our Creator makes for our service to him. Let us show this appreciation by supporting the company book studies and making the most effective use of our new book “This Means Everlasting Life”.
