Jehovah’s witnesses are eager to preach ‘this good news of the Kingdom in all the inhabited earth in fulfillment of Matthew 24:14 (NW) and thus rejoice to serve people living in unassigned territory as well as those living within the bounds of their assigned territory. As in previous years, many of the other sheep living in unassigned territory will be located by publishers and they will respond to the Kingdom message. (270)

Vary Your Sermons

  • 1 Variety is the spice of life! Sameness produces monotony and monotony is unnatural as well as uninteresting. On the other hand, the handiwork of Jehovah is ever fresh and new because of its variety. The alert theocratic minister will make his Bible sermons live by following this same principle in presenting the good news from house to house.

  • 2 Using a variety of three- to eightminute Bible sermons will enable you to put more warmth, feeling and enthusiasm into your discussions at the doors and you will not grow tired of what you say because of repeating the same sermon or become stereotyped or mechanical.
