Will You Be a Summer Vacation Pioneer?

Without a doubt the goal of all youthful witnesses of Jehovah is to be a full-time, year-round minister of the gospel when they become older. What better training could these have than that of spending from two to three months each year in the full-time service until such time as they would be free and able to devote all their time and energy to the service of the King and his kingdom? The summer vacation pioneer arrangement provides this opportunity. Last year there were 392 who took advantage of this arrangement. How many will there be this year?

You have youth and you have health. You have agreed to do Jehovah’s will and have symbolized that consecration by water immersion. You have dedicated your all to God’s service, and that includes primarily your time and your strength. To the extent that you appreciate the great privilege of bringing honor to his name and comfort to men of goodwill, to that extent you will seriously consider the call to use your all in full-time service this summer. Enroll in June and continue until September in joyous full-time service.

In becoming a summer vacation pioneer you will not have to leave your home, but you can continue to witness in the very territory in which you now live. Thus after school begins you can continue to feed the "sheep” you have found, thereby helping to increase your Kingdom interests throughout the year. Your literature you will obtain from a near-by pioneer. Write now for your vacation pioneer application blank. Magazine Distributors’ Order Blanks

A new order blank, complete with all necessary instructions, has now been provided for all companies and pioneers to simplify the ordering of magazine distributors’ copies and extra copies. (Samples enclosed) This form has many advantages over the former order blank, as it contains at least five distinct improvements that will greatly expedite magazine ordering.

It should always be used when ordering magazines. Different sections are provided in which to indicate what you require. Clearly print the name of the company or pioneer in the space provided at the top of the form, and then set out the complete name and address of the person who is to receive the magazines. After this, select that section dealing with your particular kind of order and show plainly therein what you require. So doing, there should be very little difficulty experienced in obtaining what you order.

How to Order

Each section of the order blank is self-explanatory, and complete instructions are given on these different features to insure exactness in ordering. First comes the provision for making a change of address. If you want an increase or decrease in the number of magazines formerly received, you use the next section. If the order is an entirely new one, show that fact by entering the quantity in the place provided on the right-hand side.

The next two sections are for foreign-language or special issues of Consolation or The Watchtower. As there is room for but one foreign-language or special issue to be shown on each form, it will be necessary to list additional kinds on separate forms. However, when many varieties are needed, list them on a suitable sheet of paper and attach it to the order blank. In addition, i read the printed instructions on the । reverse side. Please make all orders neat and plain.
