Do You Preach Every Month?

The mature Christian understands fully his commission from God. His richness of understanding keeps the will of the Lord clearly before him and he delights to do it. Thus Paul prayed that the Colossian brothers might “be filled with the accurate knowledge of his will in all wisdom and spiritual discernment, in order to walk worthily of Jehovah to the end of fully pleasing him as you go on bearing fruit in every good work”.—Col. 1: 9, 10, AH'.

Around the World with Jehovah’s Witnesses

“Obey God Rather than Men"

The apostles, when before the Sanhedrin and commanded to cease preaching the good news regarding Christ Jesus, replied: “We must obey God as ruler rather than men.” (Acts 5:29, NW) The 1952 “Yearbook” shows that the good example they set is being followed by Jehovah’s witnesses today.

The witness work in a certain Syrian town was forging ahead by leaps and bounds, especially the public meeting campaign, partly due to the fact that a clergyman took his stand for the truth. So the Devil influenced his agents to command the brothers to refrain from assembling themselves together. However, they obeyed God rather than men and so the company servant was arrested. At the trial the false charges were refuted and a good witness was given, causing the judge, in dismissing the case, to say: “I have found Jehovah’s witnesses to be the only real Christians.” (170, 171)

Our brothers in Switzerland followed a like course. Although the Supreme Court at Lausanne refused to properly classify their house-to-house activity as preaching and ruled that they must obtain permits, they chose to obey God rather than men and even arranged for a circuit assembly in Lausanne, where the court resided, and featured the house-to-house work. Not a single arrest was made, and the largest attendance ever was seen at the public meeting! (233)

In Yugoslavia the brothers continue to preach and increase despite a five-year ban. They depend upon Jehovah for strength. (244)

Home Bible Studies

One cannot read the reports contained in the 1952 “Yearbook” without being struck with the vital role that the home Bible studies play in releasing the prisoners; where “anyone who is being orally taught the word [shares] in all good things with the one who gives such oral teaching”. —Gal. 6:6, NW.

In Bolivia a clergyman told a young couple with whom a Bible study was being conducted that Jehovah’s witnesses were antichrists. However, having just concluded the chapter on Jesus Christ, they were well equipped to refute him. Result: two more publishers of the Theocracy. Without the home Bible study the Devil’s bird might have snatched away the seeds of Kingdom truth that had been so painstakingly planted in the hearts of those interested ones. (91)

In China one sister invited her former school friends to her own home for a Bible study, and before long there were three more witnesses for Jehovah. One of these in particular received much opposition from her husband and so-called friends, but that only made her stronger. Could she have withstood that heat of persecution without the help obtained from that Bible study? (110)

In France a back-call was made in a town where there were none of Jehovah’s witnesses This grew into a study with ten persons. “Within two months, four were publishing, and by the time of the next circuit assembly six of the original ten were immersed. At the close of the service year there were 12 publishers in that little town, and as many as 30 persons were attending the public meetings held there. Home Bible study work is certainly an effective means of obeying Jesus’ injunction, ‘Feed my sheep.’ ” (137) And also an effective way of telling the prisoners, “Go forth!”

A United Petition for The World Assembly

“Except the Lord build the house, they labour in vain that build it.” (Ps. 127:1) This Scriptural principle applies to all activities of the Lord’s people in caring for Kingdom matters. Having in mind this fact and the world assembly of Jehovah’s witnesses to be held in New York city July 19-2G, 1953, properly it may be said, ‘Unless the Lord’s blessing and favor is upon the multitude of things to be done in connection with preparing the world assembly, they labor in vain that build it.’

Each one of the Lord's people is deeply concerned in the assembly and desires it to be an outstanding milestone this side of Armageddon. The Society prayerfully considers all assembly arrangements before Jehovah and will continue to do so, as counseled at Philippians 4:6 (NW) : “In everything by prayer and supplication along with thanksgiving let your petitions be made known to God.” Knowing that Jehovah delights in the petition of his devoted people everywhere, the Society is inviting all dedicated individuals and companies of Jehovah's witnesses world-wide to unite with us in praying for Jehovah’s guidance upon all undertakings in this regard.—Prov. 15 : 8.

With prayers ascending to Jehovah from all parts of the world in behalf of the assembly itself, our personal arrangements to attend as well as those of our brothers throughout the world, undoubtedly he will be pleased with our united petition and pour out from heaven a manifestation of approval that will result in the greatest of blessing to all of his people.
