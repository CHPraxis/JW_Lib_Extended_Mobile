WHY THE CHURCHES FAILED

The peoples of earth are now in the great transitory period. The greatest crisis of all time is upon the human race. Many are asking, Why has Christianity failed ? Why should this great war come when we have so many churches and like organizations ? The answer is found in the fact that Christians In the church systems have been overreached by Satan, and his “Seed” has there predominated and controlled. The clergy have repudiated the Bible, they have turned the people away from it; their religion has been merely a veneer and could not regenerate the heart.
