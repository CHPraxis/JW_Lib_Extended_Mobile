Theocratic News
Cameroon: A new peak of 18,810 publishers was reached in June, and there were 84 baptized during the month.
Côte d’Ivoire: Publishers reporting in June totaled 4,330, their sixth publisher peak this service year.
Italy: Another peak in publishers was reported in June with 198,179 out in field service. Twenty-​two new congregations were formed during the month.
São Tomé and Príncipe: On June 30 an association was legally established, opening the door for greater activity. The 100 publishers reporting for the month represent a 43-​percent increase over last year’s average.
Uruguay: A new peak of 9,093 publishers was reached in June.
