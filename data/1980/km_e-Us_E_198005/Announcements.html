● The Society now has available in grade-two English Braille the books Choosing the Best Way of Life and Commentary on the Letter of James. These publications may be obtained on a free loan basis or purchased and kept permanently. The Choosing book is in four volumes and costs $5.00, and the Commentary on James is in five volumes and costs $6.25. The proper remittance should accompany orders to purchase these Braille publications. All orders for Braille publications should be directed to the Society’s Braille Desk. They should not be included on congregation literature orders, nor should Braille publications be stocked by the congregation.
