GOOD NEWS FOR ALL

Are you saddened, disturbed or alarmed by the flood of bad news inundating the world? Conditions do not warrant much else—or do they? Could you believe that current events are really good news in the making? The most authoritative source in the world says that they are. That source is the Word of God. If you have never considered such a possibility you will truly rejoice in “This Good News of the Kingdom”. This 32-page booklet takes its title from one of the most encouraging promises ever made to man. That promise of God was spoken by no other than Jesus Christ. He described prophetically the worst conditions that would ever prevail and then foretold the best that were to follow immediately. And then he gave his unconditional guarantee that men throughout the inhabited earth would be made aware of this hope-inspiring message. How present world conditions are to be brought to an end and how the everlasting joy and happiness that Jehovah God has arranged for are to be ushered in is the subject of this timely publication. Obtain your personal copy for only 5c. Better yet, obtain three for 10c or seven for 25c and share “this good news of the kingdom” with your friends and neighbors.
